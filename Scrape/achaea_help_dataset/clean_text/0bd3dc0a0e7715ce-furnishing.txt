9.37.7 FURNISHING
(See also:
HELP DESIGN
, HELP FURNISHING DESIGN, and
HELP
CRAFTING
GUIDELINES)
The art of
Furnishing
enables you to create various furnishings using basic
commodities
and, where appropriate, samples. With advanced knowledge, one can
design
their own furnishings,
hold
more samples, and regain a part of the
materials used in the creation of the furniture by means of disassembly.
To become a professional furnisher, you must be trained by the craft
guild
of
Delos
.
You must be above
level
10 to access the furnishing
tradeskill
.
BUY
FURNISHING PERMIT
- Use this
command
at "A comfortable parlour" in Delos.
- This will cost 200
credits
.
- If you have a bronze
trade
seal, please
READNEWS
ANNOUNCE
4247
- Once purchased, this permit will
show
up on
SCORE
LICENSES
.
- This will never expire and cannot be traded in.
GAINING THE FURNISHING
SKILL
- Make sure you have Bought your Furnishing Permit as per above!
- Make sure you have a tradeskill slot empty
-
TRADESKILLS
will show your tradeskill slots
- SCORE LICENSES will show how many slots you have in full.
- Visit Amarisse, Secretary of the Crafting Council
- ASK AMARISSE GAIN FURNISHING.
Once you are a licensed furnisher, you will have basic
ability
in the
Furnishing tradeskill.
Abilities
Gained
----------------
At Novice:       The ability to Furnish from existing
designs
and the ability
to hold 20 of each sample.
At Adept:        The ability to create new designs and the ability to hold 100
of each sample.
At Transcendent: The ability to Furnish bookcases and bookshelves, and the
ability to hold 250 of each sample.
Kegs, Casks, and Barrels
========================
Kegs, casks, and barrels can now be designed by furnishers! These can contain
up to 40 sips of a beverage (created by a beverage maker).
These items are also the
base
of the keg, cask, or
barrel
artefact
used for
ageing
beverages
in cellars!
A wire-
bound
cork can be purchased from the crafting artefact
shop
.
PLUG CORK IN <keg/barrel/cask> to make a placed item into a non-
decaying
and resetting version, and also expanding its capacity from 40 - 200 sips!

HELP DESIGN
, HELP FURNISHING DESIGN, and HELP CRAFTING GUIDELINES)
