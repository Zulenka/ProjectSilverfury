12.5.6 The Azdun Dungeon
The
Azdun
Dungeon, located beneath the ruins of a castle in the
Dakhota
Hills,
was discovered in the Modern Age by an
intrepid
explorer
named Azhrarn.
Located underground, it is the home of a major
goblin
mining
operation, but
adventurers beware: the depths of its lower
levels
will lead you to foul places
whose
denizens
wield
terrible powers with deadly intent against the living.
