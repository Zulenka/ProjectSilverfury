9.51 Two Arts
Known individually as the
Draw
Art and the Blade Art, the Two Arts are a
formidable and unmistakable
style
of swift, focused, and brutal swordsmanship.
As it is said, it is easier for a trained master to
kill
by
drawing
the blade
from its
sheath
than by swinging a naked sword. Advanced techniques involve the
channelling of
Shin
energy into one's
weapon
to
wield
a blade empowered with
fire
, ice, and
other
potent forces.
Before the Two Arts may be practised, one requires a blade, and no ordinary
blade will do. The smith Murad and the Hatha-yin maiden Kavaya will
help
those
who
seek a named blade of their own, and can be found in an encampment east of
Delos
.
