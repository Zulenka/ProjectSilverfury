19.13.9 Flowerpot Customisations
The flowers generated by the Stone Flowerpot (from the
Shop
of Wonders) can be customised via this in game
system
!
You may have up to 10 different kinds of flowers produced in the stone flowerpot.
Each
customisation
costs 250
bound
or customisation
credits
.
CUSTOMISATION NEW FOR <flowerpotID> FLOWER
- this will create a new customisation entry with the
following
fields:
Alias
- a single word. This will be the
base
noun used to interact with the flower (
lily
, rose, calathea, etc). This noun must then feature as the main reference in your appearance, dropped, and examined.
Appearance - What you see when you use
INV
.
Dropped - What the item looks like when dropped.
Examined - What you see when you
PROBE
the item.
(See also HELP CUSTOMISATION REQUESTS and
HELP
CUSTOMISATION GUIDELINES)
