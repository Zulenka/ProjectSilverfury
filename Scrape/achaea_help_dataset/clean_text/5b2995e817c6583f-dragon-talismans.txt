18.6.1 Dragon Talismans
There are six
talismans
in the
Dragon
set, one for each colour of Dragon: Red, Green, Blue, Silver,
Gold
, Black.
Completing each
talisman
will give you, if you are a Dragon, the
power
to change into a Dragon of that colour. Use
DRAGONFORM
<colour> to achieve this!
Completing all six talismans in the set will make you an Elder Dragon, giving you the
ability
to DRAGONFORM much faster than normal
Dragons
, giving you a different room
look
message
, and letting you choose from one of five pairs of enter/
leave
messages
.
Use TALISMAN SELECT DRAGON
MOVEMENT
<#> to select from the
following
options:
0
Your default movement messages for your dragon colour.
1
Enter:
Light
shines prismatically from the outstretched wings of the elder dragon Cooper,
who
glides in from the $dir before landing with a ground-shaking thump.
Exit
: Launching $him/her/faenself $dir with $his/her/faes powerful hindquarters, the elder dragon Cooper unfurls $his/her/faes enormous wings and glides effortlessly to the $dir.
2
Enter: Cooper glides gracefully in from the $dir on open wings, the air rippling in $his/her/faes wake.
Exit: With a sinuous dip, Cooper wings gracefully away to the $dir, the air rippling in $his/her/faes wake.
3
Enter: With ominous portent, Cooper's draconic form approaches from the $dir.
Exit: Cooper vanishes to the $Dir, the surroundings seeming to
shift
in $his/her/faes wake.
4
Enter: The immense body of Cooper, $his/her/faes iron-hard scales gleaming brightly, emerges from the $dir.
Exit: The towering, draconic form of Cooper
moves
to the $dir, razor-sharp claws
leaving
deep gouges in $his/her/faes wake.
5
Enter: The earth trembles underfoot as the elder dragon Cooper enters from the $dir.
Exit: The elder dragon Cooper leaves to the $dir, the earth trembling in $his/her/faes wake.
