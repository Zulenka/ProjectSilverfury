19.13.5 Perfume and Cologne Customisations
New
perfumes
and colognes can be added to their respective cabinet/case
via the
customisation
system
!
The
perfume
cabinet and cologne case can only contain 4 scent options,
adding a new one will
prompt
a replacement of an existing.
CUSTOMISATION NEW FOR <item#> [<type>]
<item#> can be a perfume cabinet ID, cologne case ID, or silver whistle ID.
Options for <type> are:
PERFUME  - for adding a new scent to perfume cabinets and cologne cases.
DELIVERY - for setting
custom
delivery
messages
for your silver whistle!
See HELP MAIL CUSTOMISATION
ITEM     - for when you want to customise a perfume cabinet, cologne case, or
silver whistle item in
description
.
To add a new perfume/cologne to your cabinet/case:
CUSTOMISATION NEW FOR cabinetID PERFUME
This will create a customisation with the
following
options:
Appearance: <none set>
Dropped: <none set>
Examined: <none set>
Wornappearance: <none set>
Wornexamined: <none set>
Worn_1p: <none set>
Worn_3p: <none set>
Removed_1p: <none set>
Removed_3p: <none set>
Smelled: <none set>
Scent_1p_1: <none set>
Scent_3p_1: <none set>
Scent_1p_
2
: <none set>
Scent_3p_2: <none set>
Scent_1p_
3
: <none set>
Scent_3p_3: <none set>
You can set 1 - 3 scent messages, and each must have a first and third person
version with the
pronouns
/references formatted as above.
When your customisation request has been approved you will need to complete
it as normal with CUSTOMISATION COMPLETE <#>.
When you complete the customisation you will be prompted to choose which
slot you want your new perfume/cologne to replace!
FOR EXAMPLE:
Please CUSTOMISATION COMPLETE 46 REPLACING <#>.
Currently, the list is as follows:
1: a bead of grass-scented perfume
2: a bead of bacon-scented perfume
3: a bead of lemon-scented perfume
4: a bead of mango-scented perfume
---------------------------------------------------------------------------
Example Perfume Customisation Request
Appearance: a bead of vanilla clove perfume
Dropped: A tiny, clear bead holds a bubble of vanilla clove perfume.
Examined: A tiny bead, smaller than a thumbnail, is made of some clear,
gelatinous substance. Within the bead is a tiny bubble of perfume,
enough for a single
application
. The bead emits the faint odour
of vanilla and cloves.
Wornappearance: a dab of vanilla clove perfume
Wornexamined: A tiny bead, smaller than a thumbnail, is made of some clear,
gelatinous substance. Within the bead is a tiny bubble of
perfume, enough for a single application. The bead emits the
faint odour of vanilla and cloves.
Worn_1p: You squeeze $=(replica$), dabbing the rich fragrance behind each ear
and at the hollow of your throat.
Worn_3p: $+(
name
$) squeezes $=(replica$), dabbing the fragrance behind each
ear and at the hollow of $=(his$) throat.
Removed_1p: With a damp
cloth
, you
remove
all trace of $=(replica$) from your
person.
Removed_3p: With a damp cloth, $=(name$) removes all trace of $=(replica$)
from $=(his$) person.
Smelled: smells of vanilla and cloves.
Scent_1p_1: An alluring fusion of vanilla and clove swirls around you,
flavouring the air.
Scent_3p_1: Alluring hints of vanilla and clove swirl around $=(name$),
flavouring the air.
Scent_1p_2: Tantalising the senses, sultry vanilla and exotic clove accents
wrap around you.
Scent_3p_2: Sultry vanilla and exotic clove accents wrap around $=(name$),
tantalising the senses.
Please take note of the pronoun references.
$=(replica$) refers to the Appearance set upon the item.
