12.17.8 The Twin Islands of Kunapi
The twin
islands
of
Kunapi
are shrouded in unpleasantness and mystery, far
beyond the Drauger tier of
Yggdrasil
. Inhabited by primitive tribes each with
distinctly uncooperative cultures, worship of the snake and the bird is rife
throughout this clandestine
world
. It is said that the tribes once coexisted
peacefully, yet the arrival of a powerful
Serpentlord
set into motion a conflict
that would span countless years beyond the ken of mortal minds as the near-
destruction
of the Kunapi tribe brought about the rise of the Snake God and the
vicious, unrelenting zealotry of his
servants
.
