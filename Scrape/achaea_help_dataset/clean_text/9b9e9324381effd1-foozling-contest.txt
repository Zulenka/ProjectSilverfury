17.2.1 FOOZLING CONTEST
COMMANDS
--------
JOIN
EVENT
FOOZLE
FOOZLE <item>
FOOZLE SUBMIT               FOOZLE PASS           FOOZLE PEEK <who>
OBJECT
------
The
point
of this contest is to collect as many points as possible by using
your Foozle box to "foozle" various items. Everyone
who
joins this event will
be given a foozlebox when the event is begun, and by probing it (or just typing
FOOZLE), you will be given a list of
5
different
things
you must find.
SCORING
-------
Each item has a different point value based on its rarity and how difficult it
is to find. You must locate one of these items and then FOOZLE <item> to record
that you've found it. Once you've found at least one item in your current set
of items, you may submit the card and receive the points for the items you've
found.
If you manage to fully complete the card by finding all the items on it, you
get double the points when submitting. Once you submit the card, a new one will
instantly appear, with new items to be found.
PASSING
-------
If you find yourself with a set of items that you are unable to find at least
one of, you have the option to "pass" the card and get another. You can only do
this once every 5
minutes
though.
NOTES
-----
NOTE 1:
Stealing
from players during the event is not allowed,
if either the victim, the attacker, or both, have joined
the game.
NOTE
2
: (For people who have joined the event only, and only if
the option is set) One option of a foozle, like any
event, is that your
artefact
wings, Universe
Tarot
,
worm holes, and certain
other
forms of speedy
movement
will
not work while playing in this event.
NOTE
3
: This game may or may not be FREE-
PK
. Please check EVENT
STATUS
to confirm this.
