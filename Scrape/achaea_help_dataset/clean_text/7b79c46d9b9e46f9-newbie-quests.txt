14.2 Newbie Quests
(see
HELP QUESTS
, HELP QUEST LOG,
HELP LODI
,
HELP MINIA
,
HELP GHELADAN
,
HELP RATTING

BUTTERFLIES

Hello, new adventurer! Wondering how to get started in
Achaea
with
* increasing in
experience
, and
* gaining
gold
?
Read
on!
Quests
are the
key
. With quests you can gain experience, gold, and sometimes
items of great
power
. Some have risen to the eightieth
level
of experience, or
higher, by
questing
ALONE!
Quests are generally for you to figure out, alone, on your own, without help of
any kind. Quests are puzzles and stories and situations, deliveries and
lost
and found and any number of
things
you can do for which there is a reward. See
HELP QUESTS
for more information.
To get you started in Achaea, we have provided a list of
denizens
that you
can
talk
to in
Minia
,
Lodi
, and
Gheladan
, all of whom have one or more quests
for you to help them with. Simply GREET the
denizen
or
say
something
like,
"Hello, do you have a
quest
for
me
?" or "I'm
looking
for work."
You will then be offered the opportunity to accept the quest and prompted to
say "Yes" or "No." Some
emotes
such as "sure" and "okay" will also work to
accept a quest. Sometimes you will have to do something, like pick up an item,
to accept a quest. If you have quest hints enabled (
CONFIG
QUESTHINTS ON)
you will see a
prompt
that
tells
you what to do to accept the quest.
When you have accepted a quest, it will be added to your quest
log
! Just
type QUEST LOG to see a list of all the quests you have accepted.

More Quests
-----------
Two
other
common ways for new adventurers to gain gold or experience are
ratting
(see HELP RATTING) and catching butterflies (see HELP BUTTERFLIES).
There are many quests to encounter outside the
newbie
areas of Minia, Lodi, and
Gheladan. These quests, however, do not necessarily require you to say "yes" or
"no" to accept, and they may not
show
up in your quest log. Some of them are
simply designed to be secret quests that you must discover;
others
just have
not yet been added to the current
system
.
