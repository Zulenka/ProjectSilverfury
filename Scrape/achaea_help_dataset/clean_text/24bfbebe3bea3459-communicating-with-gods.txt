11.1.2 Communicating with Gods
(See
HELP GODS
, HELP DIVINE ORDERS,
HELP OFFERINGS
, HELP DIVINE FAVOUR,
HELP
DIVINE
PUNISHMENTS
)
Approaching a God of
Achaea
may be a simple
task
, or it may not. Much depends
on
who
you are, which God you are approaching, and how you approach.
Consider
this carefully, as the stakes are high!
Gods
hold
your
health
and even life in
their hands. They may take it away from you, or greatly improve it, in a
single stroke.
In attempting to
communicate
with a God, you may
attempt
any of the usual
methods used for approaching a mortal -
tells
,
messages
,
letters
,
says
, etc.
Immortals
are often present but not visible to you. If an
Immortal
wishes
to be
seen, then this will be the case.
What not to do
--------------
Don't ask to
talk
to a God on
Market
channel
. That's a sure way to
experience
new and unpleasant
things
in Achaea (see HELP DIVINE PUNISHMENTS).
Don't assume that the God in
question
has absolutely
nothing
else at all to do,
and that you alone are the only
thing
that matters in the universe.
Don't be abusive, disrespectful, or inconsiderate. True, it's your choice, and
if you want to do any of these things, this writer certainly won't stop you. If
you give a bit of warning to
other
mortals first, that would be nice: it'd
allow
time
for setting up with a good vantage
point
from which to watch the
fireworks. Whee! Crispy crunchy fun.
(See
HELP GODS
, HELP DIVINE ORDERS,
HELP OFFERINGS
, HELP DIVINE FAVOUR, HELP
DIVINE PUNISHMENTS)
