15.3.3 SHARING OF ADVENTURERS / CHARACTERS
Sharing
adventurers (characters), or transferring or
selling
an adventurer from one person to another is strictly prohibited. It will almost certainly be punished by permanent loss or
deletion
. Do not give
permission
to anyone else to
log
on to your
character
, ever!
This also includes
automation
to allow someone else to
control
your character, this is another form of character sharing and will incur the same
punishments
as if you gave them your login. If the person operating your character for you is also logged in, they may be in breach of
HELP MULTIPLAYING
