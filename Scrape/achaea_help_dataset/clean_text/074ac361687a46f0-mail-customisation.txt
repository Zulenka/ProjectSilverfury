19.13.6 Mail Customisation
You can customise the
mail
delivery
messages
for your silver whistle with
the
customisation
request
system
!
The cost for mail delivery customisation is 500
credits
(
bound
or
custom
.)
To customise the mail delivery messages for your silver whistle:
CUSTOMISATION NEW FOR <whistleID> DELIVERY
This will create a customisation request with the
following
options:
Request1p: <none set>
Request3p: <none set>
Mailed1p: <none set>
Mailed3p: <none set>
Deliver1p: <none set>
Deliver3p: <none set>
NOTES
======
- Please format the
pronouns
/item references as shown below.
- $=(replica$) refers to the
letter
appearance.
- If you want to customise the
description
of your whistle, use ITEM instead
of DELIVERY.
- Any named
denizen
featured for mail delivery should be
something
you have
access to (like your own
pet
.)
- Humanoid mail deliverers are not permitted.
- Creatures referenced should make
sense
for your
character
to interact with.
- Do not force extraneous actions upon your recipient.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
An example mail delivery customisation:
Requesting 1p: You gingerly pull $=(replica$) from an overstuffed,
stygian-hued satchel at your side,
ignoring
the sheaf of
papers that threatens to spill from its lip.
Requesting 3p: A sheaf of papers threatens to spill from the lip of
$=(caster$)'s overstuffed, stygian-hued satchel as $=(he$)
rummages through $=(his$) belongings and eventually pulls out
$=(replica$).
Mailing
1p: A soft humming resonates in the distance, heralding the imminent
arrival of a slow-turning swarm of locusts. The roaring beat of
innumerable wings is almost deafening as the swarm descends,
enveloping you momentarily to sweep $=(replica$) from your grasp.
Mailing 3p: A soft humming resonates in the distance, heralding the imminent
arrival of a slow-turning swarm of locusts. The roaring beat of
innumerable wings is almost deafening as the swarm descends,
circling $=(caster$) and sweeping $=(replica$) from $=(his$) grasp.
Delivering 1p: Darkness pervades, and the deafening roar of beating wings
echoes through the area as a great swarm of locusts descends.
Encircling you, the densely populated swarm drops $=(replica$)
into your hands, before sweeping from the locale.
Delivering 3p: Darkness pervades, and the deafening roar of beating wings
echoes through the area as a great swarm of locusts descends.
Encircling $=(caster$), the densely populated swarm drops
$=(replica$) into $=(his$) hands, before sweeping from the
locale.
You can also customise the
letters
generated by a stationery kit!
CUSTOMISATION NEW FOR <kit id> LETTERKIT
SET your desired APPEARANCE, DROPPED, and EXAMINED - these will
apply
to all letters generated by the kit specified.
Use the $fullname tag to insert your
name
into the examined, this includes
suffixes
and
prefixes
and will update if yours change. (EXAMINED ONLY)
Subsequent customisation requests for the same kit are possible, and will simply replace the existing customisation.
This will cost 200 bound credits or customisation credits, where customisation credits are used first as per normal.
Customising
the kit itself will still need to be done through CUSTOMISATION NEW FOR <kit id> ITEM, and will not apply to letters generated.
(See also HELP CUSTOMISATION REQUESTS and
HELP
CUSTOMISATION GUIDELINES)
