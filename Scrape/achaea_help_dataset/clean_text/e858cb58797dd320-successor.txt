Who
is
Successor
?
-----------------
The successor is the one that will take
power
next after the current
dictator
or
oligarch
. Each dictator or oligarch designates a separate, distinct
successor.
Commands
--------
SUCCESSOR
DESIGNATE
<who>  - designate a successor (dictator/oligarch only).
SUCCESSOR
SHOW
-
shows
who your successor is.
Limits
------
A successor may not already be in the
House
Leadership, nor may one person be
successor to more than one Oligarch.
A successor must be a
member
of the same House as the Dictator or Oligarch, and
must be at least House
Rank
5
.
A dictator or oligarch may not exercise most powers of the position unless and
until a successor has been designated.
Powers
------
The successor has no real power, except that if a Dictator or Oligarch gives up
the position, then the successor takes the reins of power at that
time
.
The successor may challenge (see HELP CHALLENGE) the oligarch or dictator at
any time. If the oligarch/dictator fails to answer in a reasonable
period
, or
surrenders, then the successor takes over in the position and is the new
dictator/oligarch.
