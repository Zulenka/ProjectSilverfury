18.5.1 Minerals
(See HELP HEALING LIST, HELP HEALING YOURSELF,
HELP CURES
, and HELP CURE LIST.)
Minerals
abound in
Achaea
.
Learning
their properties should be a high
priority for all adventurers.
By the proper use of alchemical minerals you may:
-
remove
or
heal
numerous
afflictions
.
- erect
defences
that prevent certain afflictions.
- create
salves
and
elixirs
, using the
Remedies
skill
(
HELP
REMEDIES).
Store minerals in the
rift
(HELP RIFT).
Minerals may be eaten or smoked, in addition to their use as ingredients for practitioners of the skill of Remedies.
The minerals listed below are those that can be extracted and created via the
Transmutation
skill and can be either used in and of themselves, or combined with
other
elements into elixirs and salves for beneficial effects. For a list of the rarer minerals found via
mining
, please refer to HELP RARE MINERALS.

Description
