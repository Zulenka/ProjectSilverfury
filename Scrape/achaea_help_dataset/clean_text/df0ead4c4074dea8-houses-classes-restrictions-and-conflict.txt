8.2.3 Houses, Classes, Restrictions, and Conflict
(See also:
HELP HOUSES
,
HELP CLASSES
, HELP CHOOSING A HOUSE,
HELP HOUSE
COMMANDS
)
Not all
classes
may
join
all
Houses
. Sometimes there are irreconcilable
differences. These may stem from fundamental conflicts,
House
restrictions, or
city
based restrictions.
Fundamental Conflicts
---------------------
Once a House has a single
member
with
Devotion
, then it may have no members
with
Necromancy
or
Occultism
. Once a House has a single member with Necromancy
or Occultism, it may have no members with Devotion. These are fundamental
conflicts that cannot be overcome in any manner.
House Restrictions
------------------
Houses may choose to restrict which classes they accept into
membership
, either
via
auto
induction (See HOUSE AUTOINDUCT RESTRICT) or via whatever
policies
they may choose to implement when they induct new members. Through auto
induction you may also forbid the
citizens
of certain
cities
, though that is
not strictly a
class
-related concern.
Specific Restrictions
---------------------
Those of the Grand Merchant Collective and the Carnivalis Institute of Jestering
cannot join factional classes. This is because such classes are restricted to
specific cities.
