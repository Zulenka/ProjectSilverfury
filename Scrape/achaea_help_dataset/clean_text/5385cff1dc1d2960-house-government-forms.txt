Multiple Leaders and the One More Powerful Leader - The
Ascendant
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Triumvirate and Oligarchy have three leaders. In these forms, one leader from
among the three will have more powers (appointments, patron, perhaps a few
others
). The three leaders will select one of their number to
wield
these
additional powers via
showing
support
(see HELP SUPPORT). Each
house
will
select its own
title
for this most powerful leader, referred to generically as
Ascendant.
Government Forms Relate to House Leader(s) Only
-----------------------------------------------
All of these forms are concerned ONLY with
selecting
the House Leader(s). House
Patron,
other
House positions, aideships, and the like, are otherwise
unaffected by government form, and handled pretty much as they have always been
- by appointment.
Selecting Government Form
-------------------------
All houses were originally Elected Autocracies. Most still are. Several chose
to transition to
Dictatorships
, and some to
Triumvirates
, through
referendums
and consultation with the
Garden
in the
period
immediately preceding and
throughout most of 423 AF.
Later Changes in Form
---------------------
Further changes in form may be undertaken via direct negotiations with the
Garden. We are still considering methods which would allow changes in form
without Garden intervention. This may include peaceful and non-peaceful means.
It may not. We'll see!
