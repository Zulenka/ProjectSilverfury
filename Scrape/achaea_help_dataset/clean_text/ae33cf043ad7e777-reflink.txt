19.21 Referring a Friend
Refer a new player or invite an
inactive
player to return to
Achaea
and earn rewards as they play! When your
friend
hits certain milestones or benchmarks, you will both receive in-game rewards.
Referral
System
Syntax
:
REFER LINK  			- Generate a unique link to refer players to the game.
REFER REWARDS	 		- View your current reward
status
.
REFER LIST 				- View a list of everyone you've referred to the game.
REFER CONFIRM <name> 	- Confirm the
name
of the player
who
referred you. This cannot be changed once confirmed!
REFERRAL RULES AND EXPECTATIONS
--------------------------------
There are two types of
referrals
:
NEW PLAYER REFERRAL - Refer a new player to
join
the game.
No self-referrals to
alt
accounts.
The new player cannot have an existing account for the game.
Players should not troll
newbies
to earn referral bonuses. This behavior will result in a permanent ban on referring.
Referrer must be in good standing.
Abuse may result in the revocation of rewards and
penalties
.
RETURNING PLAYER REFERRAL - Invite an existing, but inactive player to return to the game.
Only accounts with no active characters in the game qualify for a returnee incentive.
Only one return invite per
registration
per real-life year.
The referrer must be in good standing.
Abuse may result in the revocation of rewards and penalties.
HOW TO REFER SOMEONE
--------------------
There are two ways to refer someone to Achaea and be eligible for rewards:
Type REFER LINK to get your personal link, then share it!
New Players: When someone
signs
up using your personal link and confirms you as their referrer during their first
character
creation, you'll be confirmed as their referrer.
Returners: When an eligible inactive player
logs
back in using your personal link, you'll automatically be confirmed as their referrer.
New Player / Returner sets you as their Referrer in-game with: REFER <PLAYER>
New Players: Only available in the first 25
hours
of playtime.
Returners: Only available during your first login session after 90+
days
of
inactivity
.
MILESTONES AND REWARDS
------------------------
Rewards are granted to both the referrer and the referee based on milestones specific to their referral type.
Easier milestones will reward both you and your referral with Unbound
Credits
, while harder milestones will reward you both with Iron Forged Bonds, which can be redeemed at The Iron Works in
Delos
for exclusive rewards.
New Player Referral Rewards
For inviting a new player to the game, both the referrer and referee will receive as the new player reaches milestones in the game.
Referred Player creates a character and enters the game:
Reward:
5
credits
Referral Player reaches
Level
30
Reward: 15 credits
Referral Player reaches Level 80
Reward: 1 Iron Forged Bond
Referral Player maxes out
lessons
in all three main
Class
Abilities
Reward: 1 Iron Forged Bond
Returning Player Referral Rewards
For inviting an inactive player (someone who has not logged in for 90+ days) back to the game, both the referrer and returning player will receive rewards when the returner reaches cumulative playtime milestones across all of their characters in this game.
Time
Played:
3
hours
Reward: 5 credits
Time Played: 15 hours
Reward: 15 credits
Time Played: 30 hours
Reward: 1 Iron Forged Bond
IRONFORGED BONDS
-----------------
This currency can be spent in the Iron Works in Delos (v68716), near the
artefact
shops
.
Offerings
include:
- Vouchers which can be traded in for 100
bound
credits, 100
housing
credits, 250
customisation
credits, or 3 bound Mayan
Crowns
.
- Statutes that grant the wearer unique deathsights on their kills.
-
Vials
of warpaint.
- Exclusive
minipets
(a lycopod and baby
humgii
).
- Exclusive
mounts
(a flameforged nightmare and an iron carriage).
- A
figurehead
of Aeolos.
-
Buckets
to transform a
ship
into an iron-chained version of itself.
Note: Rewards available may fluctuate and change over time.
FAQ
----
What if my friend (new player) forgot to use my referral link when creating their character?
They can REFER <yourname> before 25 hours of total playtime to confirm you as their referrer.
Can I change my referrer?
No. Once the New Player or Returning Player confirms their referrer, the
selection
is locked.
Do milestone hours across multiple characters count for Returning Player invite milestones?
Yes, they're cumulative for all characters on that player's registration for this game.
Can I invite someone who still has an active character to return?
No, they must be fully inactive, which means they have not logged in to any characters in the game for 90+ days.
How often can I invite the same person to return to the game?
Once every 12 real-life
months
, as long as they’ve been inactive for 90+ days.
What if the Returning Player I referred logged in and forgot to use the REFER URL or click REFER <PLAYER> before
logging
back out?
Your character will be considered "Active" and ineligible to be a returning referral. Make sure you complete this process correctly!
Do Returning Player invite rewards differ from New Player referral rewards?
Yes, they're set separately by the
admins
and outlined above. Referral
Shop
reward offerings are the same for all players, but are subject to change.
