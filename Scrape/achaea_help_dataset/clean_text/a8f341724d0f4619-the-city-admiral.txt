10.4.6 THE CITY ADMIRAL
The
City
Admiral (within the
Defence
Ministry) is responsible for the upkeep and wellbeing of all
things
seafaring
related in a city.
If a city has a
harbour
, these powers are invested to the Admiral:
FUNDS DEFENCE
- See how much
money
the Ministry has. This must be done from the Council Room.
CITY FLEET
-
Show
the details of the city's fleet.
CITY HARBOUR INFO
- Show information on the city harbour.
CITY HARBOUR SET DOCKING FEE
CITIZENS
/NONCITIZENS <amount>
- Set the harbour's default docking fee for citizens and non-citizens.
CITY HARBOUR ADD/LIST/
REMOVE
DOCKING
PERMISSIONS
<permissions>
- Modify the docking permissions for the harbour.
CITY HARBOUR UNDRYDOCK <
ship
name
or number>
- Removes a vessel from
drydock
.
CITY HARBOUR EJECT <ship name or number>
- Cut a vessel
lose
, removing it from the protection of the harbour.
