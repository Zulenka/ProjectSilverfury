Introduction
------------
The
skill
of Tattoos is an ancient one, having been discovered and perfected by
the earliest Tsol'aa. Using special dry-powder
inks
made from berries and
roots, the tattoo
artist
magically inscribes, without a needle, various types
of symbols onto a body (his own, or another's).
Tattoos are not permanent. You may purchase permanence for existing tattoos as
an artefact option. See HELP PERMANENT TATTOOS.
Each type of tattoo requires a different
combination
of inks (see HELP INKS).
I. Inking a Tattoo
----------------------
In order to
ink
a tattoo, use the
following
command
, with options as follows:
INK <tattooname> ON <body part> OF <adventurer|ME>.
Body parts are: head, torso, right arm, left arm, right leg, and left leg.
For example: INK
BUTTERFLY
ON RIGHT ARM OF
ME
.
Inking a tattoo requires that you have the proper inks in your
inventory
. It
will take about half a
minute
, during which neither the inker nor the inkee may
do
anything
. Ink is for sale at various
markets
in the land.
You may have only one tattoo on each body part, until you have risen to the
rank
of skilled in tattoos at which
point
you will be able to ink a second
tattoo on your torso. At the rank of expert you will be able to ink a second
tattoo on your head, a second on your left leg at fabled, and a second on your
right leg at mythical. Once you receive the
ability
of Superscribing at
transcendent, you will be allowed to have two tattoos on each body part.
II. Removing a Tattoo
---------------------
A tattoo may be wiped off your body by:
WIPE OFF <tattoo>.
III. Activating or Using a Tattoo
---------------------------------
For most tattoos, TOUCH in order to use or activate. Some are continuous in
effect after activated, such as
moon
or boar. Some, such as
shield
or
web
,
perform a single function when TOUCHed. TOUCH again when you
wish
to use again.
IV. How Long Do Tattoos Last?
-----------------------------
Continuous effect tattoos, such as moon or boar, may wear off at any
time
.
The average duration is several
Achaean
days
of active, continuous use.
Instantaneous, single function tattoos, such as shield or web, have a
limited number of uses. When those are gone, the tattoo fades.

VI. Getting Tattoos from Vinci
------------------------------
The leading tattooist in the realms is Vinci. His parlour is in
Delos
. It can
be reached from the Delosian bridge by
going
northeast, northeast, east, east,
and north. It can also be reached by
newbies
by using
PORTALS
, then
moving
WEST and NORTHWEST.
VII. Artefact (or Permanent) Tattoos
------------------------------------
For the truly discriminating taste we also offer the ultimate in tattoos:
artefact tattoos, also known as permanent tattoos. See HELP ARTEFACT TATTOOS
for more info.
