8.2.13 The Somatikos
"Kindle the
fire
, be ruthless; be a
paragon
of inspiration to those you
protect, and a moonlit dagger to those you oppose."
Fiercely protective of the
city
of
Hashan
, the
Somatikos
believe strongly in
the importance their home plays upon the
world
stage, and will
go
to any
lengths to keep the Hashani way of life secure. To an extent they
consider
the
realm at large a part of their protectorate against the dangers of fanaticism
and tyranny, and are ever working to further the interests of the Court of
Shadows.
Guardians
of equality, experts of reconnaissance, masters of
engineering and craftsmanship, they are a varied team but one that works
together seamlessly.
