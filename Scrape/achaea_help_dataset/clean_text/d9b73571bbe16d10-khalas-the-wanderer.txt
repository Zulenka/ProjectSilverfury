11.8.15 Khalas, the Wanderer
Khalas
, the Wanderer, was an Elder God and
member
of the
Triumvirate
during the Chaos Wars.
Following
the defeat of the Triumvirate, Khalas was transformed into a statue, serving as a
reminder
of His
attempt
to overthrow the
Logos
.
During the Age of Black
Woe
, Khalas was freed from His Logosian prison by Pazuzu. He continued to wage the
war
He struck in eons past, before He was ultimately overwhelmed by the combined forces of His
Divine
siblings and Sycaerunax, Father of
Dragons
.
His last breath was ended at the hands of His student, Han-Silnar, an act of both betrayal against the Elder God and
justice
for the suffering of Enheduanna.
