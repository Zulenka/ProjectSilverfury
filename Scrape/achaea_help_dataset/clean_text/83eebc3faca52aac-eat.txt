5.9 HUNGER
When You Get
Hungry
-------------------
When you
reach
level
15, you will start growing hungry and require
food
.
Typing
STATUS
will
show
you how hungry you are. If you allow yourself to get
too hungry, you can get sick, and eventually pass out.
Where the Food is
-----------------
In order to
eat
, you will need to find yourself a
shop
that sells food.
Generally
speaking
, the more expensive the food, the more sustenance value it
has, though there is not an exact one-to-one correspondence.
Hunger
By
Name
--------------
How hungry are you? The
following
is a brief review of the
messages
you'll see
when you type
STAT
, and what they mean:
utterly
satiated
-
eating
won't
help
at all!
reasonably full        - eating probably won't help much
not hungry             - eating would help a little, but is unnecessary
feeling a bit
peckish
- hungry; no risk yet, but eat soon
weak from hunger       - very hungry; you better get to the food stall
starving to
death
- at this
point
you will start passing out and
taking damage.
