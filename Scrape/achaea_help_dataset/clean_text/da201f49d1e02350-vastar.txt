11.7.19 Vastar, the Skylord
Born of Ayar at the dawn of Creation,
Vastar
stands as one of the last remaining Elder
Gods
, a dwindling assembly
Who
existed at the
Genesis
of All That Is. Unwilling to countenance the lesser claims of His
Divine
Siblings, it was from Ayar that Vastar claimed
dominion
of all the Skies of Creation, anointed
sovereign
over infinite heavens teeming with boundless potential. His authority over this domain is limitless and absolute, and all that
moves
beneath the Sky is subject to His scrutiny.
In the tumult of the Chaos Wars, the
Skylord
saw providence in the myriad mortal
races
revealed to Him. And yet, revelations most bitter soon became manifest as He realized that humanity, when offered the breath of life, the
flame
of choice, and set loose beneath His firmament, would choose to squander their divine
bounty
, clinging instead to comfort and wallowing in mediocrity. With each passing epoch, the limits of the Skylord's forbearance were tested, countless realms and worlds devoid of greatness and scarce punctuated by
sparks
of potential.
Though His Necessity is never complete, Vastar has turned His transcendent and unbridled attentions back towards
Achaea
, manifesting as the absolute amalgam of the Storm to
command
calamity as both judge and
crucible
. No longer willing to stand idly by while mortals falter beneath the
weight
of their squandered promise, Vastar wields His
power
as Divine ultimatum.
Ambition is His demand, stagnation a sin that demands immediate rebuke. To worship the Skylord as one of His Farethi is not mere
devotion
but a sacred obligation. His faithful prepare to unleash His Reckoning upon the complacent masses with relentless Fury and unyielding Conviction, to summon forth His Storm that Achaea
might
one
day
rise, tempered and worthy, beneath the majesty of His Sky.
