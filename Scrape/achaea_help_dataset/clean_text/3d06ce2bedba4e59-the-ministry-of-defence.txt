10.4.2 THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE
(For
questions
relating to personal
defences
, see
HELP
DEFENCE
)
The
Minister
of Defence is responsible for all military matters within a
city
. Their primary functions include protection of its
citizens
, enforcement of the law,
army
recruitment, and declarations of
war
.
The Minister must be an enlisted soldier to be appointed to this position. Upon appointment, they will automatically be promoted to the highest
rank
in the city's army.
Aides
in this ministry are responsible for managing city security and combative readiness; the Minister handles management of the military forces and the filing of
bounties
.
The
commands
associated with the Ministry of Defence are:
CITY
FINANCE
REPORT DEFENCE [<year>]
- The detailed report for this ministry for the given year.
CITY REPORTS DEFENCE [<year>]
- Non-financial general report for this ministry.
ARMY
SHOW
- Show the
status
of your army.
ARMY CONSCRIPT <person>
- Induct a
citizen
into your city's army.
ARMY PROMOTE <person>
- Promote a soldier to the next rank.
ARMY ASSIGN <rank#> <power>
- Assign a
power
to a rank in your forces.
Powers are:
ARMYTELL  : Use of the army
channel
.
ARMYWHO   : List online soldiers.
MEMBERS   : List all enlisted soldiers.
DISCHARGE : Dismiss a soldier from the
ranks
.
ARMY LISTPOWERS <rank#>
- List all assigned powers for a specific rank
FUNDS DEFENCE
- See how much
money
your Ministry has. This must be done from the Council Room.
CITY
ENEMY
/UNENEMY <person> <reason>
- Brand a person an enemy of your city.
CITY
ALLY
/UNALLY <person> <reason>
- Brand a person an ally of your city.
CITYWITHDRAW DEFENCE <amount>
-
Withdraw
funds from the Defence coffers.
- Available to Admiral only.
For City
Guard
commands, see
HELP GUARDS
For
Crimes
and
Charges
see
HELP CRIMES
Please also see
HELP IMPROVEMENTS
and HELP CITY DESTRUCTION for more responsibilities and commands that the Ministry of Defence has.
(See also:
HELP ARMY
, HELP CITY DESTRUCTION, and HELP CRIMES)
