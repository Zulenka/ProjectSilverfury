8.1.6 THE INFERNAL CLASS
(See also:
HELP OPPRESSION
,
HELP MALIGNITY
,
HELP WEAPONMASTERY
,
HELP
8, HELP
CLASS
)
Since the origin of the
Infernal
class in
Ashtan
under the leadership of
Ekary Lucoster - known as the Sinistar - these terrible warriors have
plagued
Sapience
. Destroyed in a
war
between their very
Guild
and the
city
of Ashtan that was their home, in
service
to Evil the Guild refounded
in the new-made city of
Mhaldor
in the west of the
continent
.
With the demise of the Maaldathi
House
, the
Infernals
live in service to
Evil before all else. They are the iron arm of Mhaldor, clad in hell-forged
steel as they
wield
their
weapons
with unshakeable
devotion
to their Lord
and God. A
vision
of the hellish lower realms as they bestride the
battlefield with their daemonic companions at their side, one and all
the Infernals eschew weakness in pursuit of naught
other
than the
supreme, unchallenged
strength
promised through adherence to the Seven
Truths of Evil.
In the year 837, by the will of the Malevolent One, Infernal
Knights
cast
aside the
practice
of
Necromancy
in
favour
of the arts of
Oppression
.
The
skills
of an Infernal are
Malignity
,
Weaponmastery
, and Oppression.
A
fledgling
Infernal gains Oppression and Weaponmastery, while Malignity
is gained upon
embracing
class.
** Please be advised that this class comes with restrictions on your actions.
The
power
of an Infernal to wield hellforged weapons or wear hellforged
armour
is dependent upon the agreement and oaths in service to Evil; if
you choose this class yet act against Evil or Mhaldor, you may find that
your
ability
to use these skills is revoked. As a result this class will
require some
roleplaying
.
(See also:
HELP OPPRESSION
,
HELP MALIGNITY
,
