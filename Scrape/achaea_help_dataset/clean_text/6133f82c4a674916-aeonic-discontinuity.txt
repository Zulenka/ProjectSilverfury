18.1.1 Aeonic Discontinuity
(See also:
HELP TIME
,
HELP CALENDAR
,
HELP GODS
,
HELP
AEONIC
TURBULENCE
)
Time
Warps (Aeonic Discontinuities, Aeonic Disturbances, Aeonic Anomalies)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sometimes it happens. You are
walking
along and you feel
something
odd. Perhaps
you faint, or enter some sort of fugue. When you awake, or when your
consciousness returns, it seems to you as though time itself has changed. And
in a way, it has.
Your memory is clear - you lived, you fought, you explored, you bought, you
sold, and yet somehow, you are back to an earlier time. It may be a quarter of
an
Achaean
day
, or maybe half. Either way, you feel as though you've gone back,
and
everything
is as it was.
How It Happens
--------------
Aeon
, Lord of Time, acts in a variety of mysterious ways to work His will upon
time itself. Mortals and
immortals
alike are subject to His whim in these
matters. He does not often warp time to His will, but when, or how often, are
both unpredictable.
What It Means
-------------
In these rare situations where Aeon acts, and reality returns to a previous
time, then all is exactly as it was from that precise moment.
Did you
rescue
a damsel in distress during that now-vanished
period
? Then the
damsel was never rescued and is still in need.
Did you write and send a
letter
? The letter is then unwritten and unsent
(though the contents still remain in your memory, and even in the memory of
the one to whom you sent it).
Did you gain a
level
of
experience
, or two? Then it is as though you never
gained them.
Did you
die
several
times
,
losing
multiple
levels
of experience? Voila! They
are back.
Did someone
steal
huge mountains of
gold
from you? Huzzah! The
theft
never
occurred, and your riches are restored to you: you have another chance!
Did you
leave
your conscious activity in the realms from "North of New Thera"?
When you return to activity, you will not return to "North of New Thera" again,
but to wherever you left on the previous occasion.
What Can Be Done About It
--------------------------
There is little any being, mortal or
immortal
, can do to affect the acts of the
Lord of Time. On some rare occasions, suffering mortality is granted mercy
by the
Gods
and may give some small gift as recompense for the sorrow of living
time and having it essentially unlived for them! This is not frequent either,
since the loss due to Aeonic disturbances is rarely very long or large.
