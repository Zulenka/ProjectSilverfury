12.17.5 Myrinia
Surrounded on all sides by lush, tropical jungle, the village of
Myrinia
rests
beneath a pair of sweltering suns on the
Plane
of Drauger. Matriarchal society
reigns supreme within, all males consigned to lives of domestic servitude as
blacksmiths, cooks, and cleaners.
Hunting
features prominently within Myrinian
culture
, and huge jaguars roam the town as the favoured companions of both
maidens and maids.
