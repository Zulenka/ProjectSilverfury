19.24.12 Contender's Talismans
The "Contender's"
talisman
set:
-------------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Promotions
(this will always be announced via the relevant
news
when the
pieces are currently available)
-
Trading
between players (pieces only)
- Whole talismans bought from the Delosian
shop
(when in stock)
- Partial completion (see below.)
The Contender's set contains the following talismans:
-----------------------------------------------------
A funereal bouquet                 - TALISMAN INFO
CONTENDERSBOUQUET
- Purchasable in
Delos
for 250
bound
credits
.
- With this emblem of mourning in your
inventory
, whenever you
die
and
leave
behind a body, the
corpse
will be covered in flowers themed to
your
city
.
A porcelain piglet
bank
- TALISMAN INFO
CONTENDERSPIG
- Purchasable in Delos for 50 bound credits.
- This trinket starts as a mere piglet and grows into a fully fledged
piggy bank as it is filled with
gold
!
- To encourage
saving
, gold cannot be removed once put in (and is entirely
safe from
theft
).
- SMASH the
piggybank
to destroy it permanently and retrieve your savings.
- SHAKE it to see how much is inside.
An adventurer's
journal
- TALISMAN INFO
CONTENDERSJOURNAL
- Purchasable in Delos for 700 bound credits.
-
READ
this journal and
learn
the details of a
quest
you have never before
completed!
- It will change once per
Achaean
month
, and will never
show
newbie
or
Elemental
Planes
quests
.
An odious jug of chum              - TALISMAN INFO
CONTENDERSJUG
- Purchasable in Delos for 200 bound credits.
- CHUM JUG when in an appropriate location for deep sea
fishing
, and your
strike chance shall be increased for thirty
minutes
.
A featureless battle figurine      - TALISMAN INFO
CONTENDERSFIGURINE
- Purchasable in Delos for 400 bound credits.
- TURN <figurine> <player> and this battle figurine will change to resemble
the player and
fight
according to their
abilities
!
- This can be done once every 15 minutes.
A prismatic stud                   - TALISMAN INFO
CONTENDERSSTUD
- Purchasable in Delos for 1250 bound credits.
- Once per Achaean
day
, your prism
tattoo
will lock on to the
target
of a
contract
or a
bounty
, following them even if they
move
from the initial
location.
- If the target changes area or you move, the prism
attempt
will still be
cancelled.
A crimson
ribbon
bar               - TALISMAN INFO
CONTENDERSRIBBON
- Purchasable in Delos for 1500 bound credits.
- With this bar in possession, whenever you
score
a player
kill
, you begin
to accrue a kill streak!
- After five successive kills, you will be fully healed in
mana
.
- After ten successive kills, you will be fully healed in both
health
and
mana.
- After fifteen successive kills, your
afflictions
will be cured entirely,
and your health and mana pools will be restored to max.
- After twenty successive kills, in addition to a full refresh of health,
mana, and afflictions, your health will be temporarily doubled until
lost
through damage.
- This does NOT function while Mayology is active.
- If you die, the streak is reset to zero.
- Upon
reaching
twenty kills, or after fifteen minutes without kills
elapses, the streak is reset to zero.
A bronze-handled magnifying glass  - TALISMAN INFO
CONTENDERSGLASS
- Purchasable in Delos for 400 bound credits.
-
CONSIDER
will now
tell
you if the
denizen
in
question
has any resistances.
Partial Completions
--------------------
If you have two or three pieces of a talisman in the historical set you can
TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL <talismanname> to complete it for the remainder
of the bound
credit
value of the item as it is in the Delosian Talisman shop.
DOING THIS WILL COMPLETE THE TALISMAN AND
BIND
IT TO YOU.
Talismans completed in this manner will have an altered
tradein
that
reflects its credit worth, including the credit purchased completion.
I.E
You have: cruciblebase and cruciblebowl.
You use TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL
CRUCIBLE
and pay 500 bound credits.
This is half the cost of the crucible in the shop because you already
have half of the pieces.
You get: a completed soulfire crucible talisman.
This item has a tradein value of: 346 bound credits.
This is
2
/3rds of 520 credits (The 500 credits paid above, plus your 2
pieces valued at 10 credits each.)
