12.11.13 Wegava Valley
The ancestral home of the gnoll, ice-
bound
Wegava
Valley, lies hidden
amongst the peaks of a mighty volcanic mountain range bordering the eastern
coast of northern
Sapience
. Glittering white snow blankets the hot, bubbling
springs that warm the numerous caverns laced throughout the underlying
mountain pass.
In a constant state of hostility and warfare against the gnolls threatening
to enslave their young are the reptilian razahr, managing to survive only by
virtue of their physical size, cunning, and usefulness as the indentured
servants
of the gnoll people.
