12.5.11 The Dungeon of the Beastlords
Inside of the Great Rock, east of the road which runs through the
Savannah
from
North of
Thera
, you will find the dungeon of the
Beastlords
. Within it are a
number of strange creatures, such as the ophidians, a
race
of noble snake-men,
and the Fortress of the Beast, filled with the cultists and their cerberi.
Beware the stirges, and give Herbie the Giant Rhinocerous Beetle a
mushroom
. He
likes that.
