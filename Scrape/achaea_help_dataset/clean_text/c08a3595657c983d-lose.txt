What's the
Point
, or How Do I
Win
, or
Lose
?
-------------------------------------------
You win by
choosing
your
goals
and attaining them. You win by being true to
your
vision
. You win by
moving
in the
direction
you deem best.
In
other
words: there is no victory for you except the one of your own
choosing, and no satisfaction except that of knowing that you did what you
thought was right, and gained what you thought worth gaining.
If you are unsure what you should be doing in
Achaea
,
read
on!
What
Might
Be Important To YOU - Possible Goals or Attainments
--------------------------------------------------------------
We can't
tell
you what is important to you. We can give you some
ideas
of goals
and attainments
others
have sought after in Achaea. Then it's up to you to
decide what you want to do. And make sure you have fun too, because that's what
it's really all about.
First we'll list some personal goals, then some competitive goals related to
rankings
, or organisations. Then we'll
talk
about how to get there, with or
without help.
Personal Goals (
HELP POLITICS
,
HELP HONOURS
,
HELP LICENSES
, HELP MIGHT)
--------------
Politics
- gain position in your
House
,
City
, or Order, and more.
Honours
- complete
famous
quests
.
Wealth      - can your mountain of
gold
rival
Prospero's?
Possessions - owning land, a home, and
artefacts
of unsurpassed
power
.
Licenses
-
Tailoring
,
Jewellery
,
Heraldry
,
Bookmaking
, etc.
Reputation  - gain
world
renown
, whether admired, loved, or feared.
Might       - transcend
skill
after skill.
Dragonhood
- the ultimate attainment in
experience
(HELP
DRAGON
).
Rankings Goals (HELP RANKINGS)
--------------
Artisanal
- prove your artistic prowess in our regular
contests
.
Bardic
- prove your bardic puissance in our regular contests.
Combat
-
arena
excellence has its benefits.
Experience - pass your peers as you rise in
level
of experience.
Explorer
- be the first to find every nook and cranny.
Fishing
- catch the most, the biggest, the priciest!
Riddle
- solve the most demanding
riddles
.
Organisational
Rank
-------------------
City    \   gain the respect (and
favours
) of your peers,
House   /   in City or House, rising in rank.
How To Get There: With Help
---------------------------
Many of the goals above require the aid of others. This is how it should be.
You are not alone in Achaea! You are one of many thousands
who
travel our
lands.
Reputation, what others
think
of you, obviously requires others, though perhaps
not their aid! Become known as a double-crossing traitor, or as a loyal
friend
.
Become feared as a mighty combatant, devastating thief, a supportive healer, or
just a good friend. Cast your shadow long across the realms by your deeds of
good or evil,
loyalty
or betrayal!
City and House rank require the recognition and
favour
of fellow
citizens
or
House members.
Influence
them by your acts of loyalty. Dazzle them with your
defence
of your organisation's aims. Prove how worthy you are, and you will be
rewarded with the rank you seek.
How To Get There: WithOUT Help
------------------------------
Some
things
can be done without anybody's help at all, if that is your
preference. Gaining rank in the Explorers'
Guild
can be done entirely alone.
Same with Riddles, Fishing, Artisanal, and Bardic attainments. Experience
ranking
, even up to gaining Dragon, can be done with or without the aid of
others.
You may gain the recognition of numerous honours lines by acting alone, if you
prefer. It's all up to you.
Getting There
-------------
Choose where you want to
go
and what you want to attain. Decide on what you
want your story to be, and make it happen. We give you all the options, and
it's up to you to decide which ones you like.
Choose one or choose many, and welcome to them, with our blessings.
