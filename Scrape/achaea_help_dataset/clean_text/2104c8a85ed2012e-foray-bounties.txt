14.8.1 Foray Bounties
A
Foray
Bounty
is a 24
hour
speedrun contest. A specific foray will be announced and there is a prize for the
group
who
can complete this bountied foray in the fastest
time
(with deaths tracked in case of ties.)
Each person may
attempt
the foray up to three
times
. This restriction applies to the player, not the group, so you can either attempt the foray three times with a single group, or one time each with three separate
groups
of players.
Signing for the bountied foray during this span will enter you into the contest for the bounty.
The timer will start counting when you enter the foray, but if you sign for the foray and do not enter, it will count as an attempt.
If you have attempted
3
times during the contest
period
you will not be able to sign for the foray again for the duration.
If you are in the bountied foray when the 24 hour period ends, you will be able to complete that
run
and it will count.
You will be able to see your attempt count by
READing
the foray
board
.
Your group will be given their completion time when the foray ends.
There is not a public leaderboard for these
bounties
, so use all your
attempts
and push!
We will be monitoring the attempts. Any attempts to get around the
spirit
of these rules will result in the disqualification of the person and any groups that they are in. This is at administrative discretion.
