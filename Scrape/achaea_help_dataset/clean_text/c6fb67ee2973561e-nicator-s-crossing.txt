12.12.10 Nicator's Crossing
Nicator's Crossing is a site on the eastern side of the north
Vasnari
Mountains
where, centuries ago, Nicator is said to have travelled in his legendary journey
to found
Seleucar
. In the past, the fortress was an important stop for those
beginning or ending the difficult journey across the mountains; after the fall of
Seleucar, however, access was cut off and the keep was abandoned.
Rediscovered by the
Fellowship
of
Explorers
600 years after the fall of Seleucar,
the keep was restored and is now presided over by the current bearer of the Staff
of Nicator.
