9.90 VALOUR
Valour
is a
skill
that may be thought of as having three distinct parts.
The first part involves the bond shared with a golden eagle, a lifelong
companion that shall aid you in all manner of endeavours.
To bond your eagle,
go
to the
island
of Anost within
Targossas
and
EAGLE REQUEST.
The second part of Valour is archery. By gaining the
abilities
to assess
wind
direction
and
speed
and use different kinds of bows (longbows, crossbows,
darkbows) you may
attack
from afar, or shoot at a cowardly opponent
who
has
fled from you.
The third and final part of Valour involves an assortment of aggressive
abilities ranging from howling a Battlecry, to Engaging your unlucky
opponent, to boosting your prowess with Fury.
In addition you may use EAGLE SANCTUARY <eagle#> to send the eagle of
another
Paladin
to safety.
- The eagle's bonded must not be logged in.
- You must be of the same
house
or
city
as the eagle's bonded.
- You must be of a higher
rank
than the bonder in said organisation.
- You must have uninterrupted concentration for a short
period
.
