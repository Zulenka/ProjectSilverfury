Artisanal and Bardic Rankings
-----------------------------
Points of Bardic or Artisanal Distinction may be gained in our regular
Artisanal and Bardic competitions. In addition to the glory of rankings of this
kind, prizes are also granted with the awards, to the tune of hundreds or even
thousands of
credits
every
time
the awards are given.
Combat Rankings
---------------
Beat your foes in the arena in ranked duels! See
HELP COMBAT RANKING
and HELP
ARENA for more details.
Experience Rankings
-------------------
Your
rank
is solely a function of your experience
level
. The RANKINGS
EXPERIENCE
command
allows you to list the rankings of other adventurers in the
Realms, and to see what a particular adventurer is ranked, you may type HONOURS
<adventurer>.  Your own
ranking
will
show
when you type
SCORE
.
The reason you do not see the level
name
for people over level 80 (Logosian) is
that the
names
are longer, and do not display well on the rankings output.
Seamonster
Rankings
-------------------
Based upon the number of seamonsters killed, the monster-
hunting
fleet of
Achaean
ships
are listed with the number of monsters slain, the average level of the seamonsters, and W/S/G to denote the type of
ship
.
