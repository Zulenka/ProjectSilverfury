9.37.11 Design Handlers
Achaea
uses a small number of players to assess
designs
based on a criteria of common mistakes, these
handlers
can
reject
a
design
based on the criteria they have, or they can escalate a design on to our design lead for approval or more
help
than the criteria can handle.
Design handlers earn
2
bound
credits
per design handled (this is tallied and awarded at the start of the next
day
), up to a maximum of 20 bound credits per day.
Design handlers will also need to maintain reliable access to the Achaea
Discord
server.
If you are interested in
applying
to be a design handler, please contact us via
ISSUE
ME
and include which
crafting
licenses
you actively use, and list the
NDS
ID for the design you own that you believe to be your best.
We will
look
for proven reliability and adherence to crafting standards and game rules in any
who
apply
.
