We have a lot to celebrate in
Achaea
! Many organisations
hold
festivals
and
celebrate various
events
in their histories. A few such occasions are celebrated
throughout the
Achaean
world
and are considered "official"
holidays
:
-
Logosmas
-
Mayaween
-
Quinquagenary
anniversary
of the Fall of the
Seleucarian
Empire (a.k.a. the
year 500, year 550, and so on)
- The appearance of the
aurora
supernalis.
Though the celebrations for each
holiday
vary, special
games
and
contests
for all
adventurers are guaranteed happenings!
