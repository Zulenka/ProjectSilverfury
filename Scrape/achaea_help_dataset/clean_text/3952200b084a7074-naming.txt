7.4.1 Naming Your Loyals
(For
help
on
selecting
proper
character
names
, see HELP NAMES)
You may
name
pretty much any
denizen
that is loyal to you. To do so, have one
thousand
gold
in your hands and type:
NAME <loyal> <newname>
For example:
NAME horse13124 Silver
If the denizen is indeed loyal to you, and meets certain
other
restrictions,
then you will be relieved of one thousand gold, and the denizen will have a new
name!
This
command
is not designed for
pets
, which are a special type of loyal.
Please see
HELP PETS
for more details on what you can do with them.
(For help on selecting proper character names, see HELP NAMES)
