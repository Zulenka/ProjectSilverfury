6.30 DIGGING AND BURYING
You can
BURY
items and
DIG
for items in many places across
Achaea
.
BURY
----
To bury, get a shovel. Some
classes
have special powers that allow
digging
as well, such as some morphs in the
Metamorphosis
Skills
. Make sure that
you have the item (or
gold
) you want to bury in your
inventory
, then
BURY <item/GOLD>
There is always a chance you will break your shovel, if using one. And
you can't bury
things
just anywhere. Use common
sense
. Or try it and see.
DIG
---
If you can bury
something
, you can dig it up. Just like with
burying
, you'll
need a shovel or a special
power
permitting you to dig.  Then just
DIG
That's it! If something is buried in that location, you'll probably find
it and it will be there on the ground for you to pick up.
