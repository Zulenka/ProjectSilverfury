8.7 THE POSITION OF HOUSE PATRON
A
House
Patron is a God
who
has been asked by a House to watch over them and,
if they request, provide guidance to the House. Further, a House may need a
Patron to protect them from the machinations of
Gods
of different ideals.
The
Divine
Patron also acts as the
voice
of the House in the
Garden
. The
formal
choosing
of the House Patron is the sole province of the House Lord
or Lady, though of course the
wishes
of the House as a whole shouldn't be
ignored.
HELP HOUSE
<house shortname> will display the patron of that House.
