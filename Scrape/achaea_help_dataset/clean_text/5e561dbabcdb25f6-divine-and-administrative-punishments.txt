11.6.1 Divine and Administrative Punishments
(see HELP DIVINE FAVOUR,
HELP ADMINS
,
HELP POLICIES
,
HELP
PENALTIES
)
Divine
Punishments
------------------
Divine punishments may be meted out in a variety of forms including:
cripple   - a sudden change in perspective.
disease   - hope that you never
experience
so many
afflictions
at once.
disfavour
- see HELP DIVINE FAVOUR.
maggot
- a different
race
with a different sort of appetite.
softvoice - removes your
ability
to
shout
or yell.
toad      - a different race. Imagine how it
moves
!
zap
- a range of powerful acts, mostly not fatal to the recipient.
Administrative Penalties
------------------------
Administrative penalties are reserved primarily for
resolution
of
ISSUES
,
violation of
Achaean
policies
, and
other
OOC
(out of
character
) situations:
describe
- loss of your ability to set your own
description
.
exp
loss  - loss of experience.
marketoff
- loss of
market
privileges, temporary or permanent.
newbie
- loss of your ability to use the newbie
channel
.
notitling
- loss of the ability to
title
others
.
pacifism
- loss of the ability to
fight
.
returnmsg - loss of the ability to set your return
message
.
shrubbing
-
changing
to a new, leafier form, temporary or permanent.
time
- a place from which there is usually no
exit
.
voice
- loss of ability to use a voice or
accent
.
warcry
- loss of ability to choose a warcry or
motto
.
Admin
Penalties Mostly for OOC situations
-----------------------------------------
Since these penalties cover OOC situations, the place where violations may
occur is not entirely confined to
Achaea
, potentially including other forms of
communication
at our discretion, including (but not limited to)
emails
,
forum
posts
, or instant
messages
.
What Punishments or Penalties Am I Under Now?
---------------------------------------------
You may see what punishments or penalties you are currently under by typing
either PUNISHMENTS or PENALTIES. Either
command
will
show
the same output.
Administrative Appeal
---------------------
Appeal of any of these administrative penalties should be sent via
email
to
support
@achaea.com.
(see HELP DIVINE FAVOUR,
HELP ADMINS
,
HELP POLICIES
, HELP ISSUES)
