15.17 Administrative Penalties
For guidance on how we
apply
Achaea's rules and
policies
, and what you can expect if you fall foul of them, please
read
on:
Please be aware that these are our guidelines. When there are situations that deviate from the norm, we may apply
penalties
higher or lower than listed, the reason for this will be explained to the person receiving.
General note 1,
If an offence is within
6
months
of a previous punishment/warning, the response will escalate to the next
level
. If a prior offense of the same type has not occurred within the prior 6 months we begin at level 1. This may be waived for habitual offenders.
General note
2
,
If an offence continues while an action is in place (i.e
other
alts
made and breaking rules,
ignore
circumvention, etc) the current
penalty
will be removed, we will escalate to the next level, include any new actors, and the duration will start over.
General note
3
,
The warning in any penalty progression may be bypassed if a player has been previously warned about the
policy
on any of their other characters.
General note 4,
This is not every rule in
Achaea
, simply the most common ones that we are asked to rule on via
issue
.
Charactersharing
=================
When a
character
created by one player is then given to or shared with another player to play.
See HELP CHARACTER SHARING for guidance.
Permanently
shrub
the character that was shared/given away.
Multiplaying
=============
When a player is logged on with two or more characters at the same
time
.
See
HELP MULTIPLAYING
for guidance.
If
seconds
abuse is involved, skip to Seconds Abuse.
If no interaction is made between the characters:
1st time - Warning.
2nd time - 3
day
disfavour
.
3rd time - 3 day shrub.
4th+     - increased
periods
of
shrubbing
. (7
days
, 30, 90, 180, 1 year+)
Seconds Abuse (with or without multiplaying)
=============================================
When a player uses one character to benefit another of his or her characters.
See
HELP SECONDS
for guidance.
1st time - Warning and
deletion
of shared goods/
xp
/
gold
/etc.
2nd time - 30 day shrub on both accounts,
anything
shared is deleted.
3rd time - 365 day shrub on involved accounts, anything shared is deleted.
4th+     - Permanent shrub on involved accounts/account wide at administrative discretion.
Harassment
===========
In general, an IGNORE must be in place before a charge of harassment can be put forth. If someone then tries to bypass that by having
others
pass
messages
,
logging
onto alts to send
tells
, etc, then it becomes harassment.
If other rules are broken in the course of harassment (seconds,
language
rules, etc), we will assess the harshest penalty from across the rules and apply that. All relevant rules violations will be noted as having been escalated to the next level for future reference.
See
HELP IGNORE
and
HELP HARASSMENT
for guidance.
1st time - Warning.
2nd time - Shrub 30 days.
3th time - Shrub 180 days.
4th+ time - Shrub 365 days to permanently, depending on severity of harassment.
AFK
Auto
-generation
====================
Being AFK while automatically generating goods, information, gold,
fish
,
rats
,
herbs
, etc.
See
HELP AUTOMATION
for guidance.
1st time - Warning if obviously new,
remove
generated goods where possible.
2nd time - 7 day shrub, remove generated goods.
3rd time - 30 day shrub, remove generated goods.
4th+ time - 180 day shrub to permanent.
QQ
from
combat
/
theft
=====================
This includes journalling, force
disconnect
, and qqing.
See
HELP HONOUR
for guidance.
1st - Warning
2nd - 1 day tdf
3rd - 3 day tdf
4th+ - 7+ day shrub
Language Infractions
=====================
Achaea is a PG13
environment
, so excessive/unnecessary profanity or explicit language in a public environment or near someone
who
has not agreed to that kind of content is not appropriate. We do take into account context, especially during
PK
, feelings can
run
very high.
See
HELP LANGUAGE RULES
for guidance.
This does not include hatespeech or discriminatory language.
1st time: Warning (1 day mute if extremely inappropriate language used)
2nd time: 7 day mute
3rd time: 7 day shrub
4th+ time: 30+ day shrub
Hatespeech/Discriminatory Language
===================================
OOC
prejudice and discrimination is not acceptable anywhere in Achaea.
See HELP DISCRIMINATORY LANGUAGE for guidance.
1st time - 30 day shrub.
2nd time - 90 day shrub
3rd+ time - 180 to permanent shrub.
PK-related offenses
====================
These include improper
contracts
, egregious PK without reason, and more.
See
HELP PK
for guidance.
1st time - warning
2nd time - 3 day tdf and xp removal.
3rd time - 7 day tdf and xp removal.
4th time - 3 day shrub.
5th+ time - 7+ day shrub.
