13.5 ARMOUR AND SHIELDS
(For the
House
of the Vashnarian
Shield

Normal
armour
is
something
you wear that protects you from both types of
physical damage (cutting and blunt).

Armour
Statistics
-----------------
Non-magical armour and shields have two armour-specific statistics: protection
vs. cutting damage and protection vs. blunt damage. The WEAPONPROBE
ability
in
the
Weaponry
skill
will allow you to see these statistics on a
piece
of armour
or on a shield.
