6.8.1 Selling Things
(See
HELP MARKET
,
HELP ADS
, HELP CREDIT TRANSFER, HELP CREDIT MARKET,
HELP COMMS
,
HELP RATS
,
HELP FISHING
, HELP SELLING VIALS, and
HELP
SHOPS
)
You can
sell
just about
anything
if you know where or to whom! Have a sword you
no longer need? One of the land's many practitioners of
forging
would be
willing to take it off your hands. Did you just milk
5
vials
of curare? One of
the land's many warriors will surely
buy
them.
Things
You Can Sell Direct
--------------------------
rats
- SELL RATS TO <
ratman
denizen>        - see
HELP RATS
fish
- SELL FISH TO <baitshop denizen>      - see
HELP FISHING
commodities
- SELL <howmany> <commodity>           - see
HELP COMMODITIES
your
SHOP
- SELL SHOP TO <whom> FOR <amt>
GOLD
- see
HELP SHOPS
credits
- CREDITS SELL <howmany> AT <goldeach> - see HELP CREDIT MARKET
vials       - SELL
VIAL
[#] TO <vial buyer>         - see HELP SELLING VIALS
Mostly Adventurers, Not Shops
-----------------------------
Most shops don't buy, but Adventurers do: the trick is to find them! The
easiest ways can be on the
market
channel
or with
ads
.
Other
options relate to
credits and becoming a
shopowner
.
MARKET  - after
level
11 or so you can use the market channel to
look
for
adventurers to buy things from you. (HELP MARKET)
ADS     -
Advertisements
are an alternative to MARKET. (HELP ADS)
CREDITS - Transfer/sell credits to other adventurers (HELP
CREDIT
TRANSFER)
or use the credit market. (HELP CREDIT MARKET)
RATS    -
Ashtan
,
Hashan
,
Targossas
,
Cyrene
, and
Mhaldor
each have a
denizen
that will buy rats from you. (HELP RATS)
FISH    -
Tasurke
,
Shastaan
, and
Minia
have fish
buyers
(HELP
FISHING
).
COMMS
- Commodities may be bought and sold at comm shops (HELP COMMS).
SHOPS   - You could become a shopowner. This can be extremely expensive.
Shops may be available from their current owners, or their
cities
.
(HELP
SHOPKEEPING
)
Some Things That Should be Pretty Easy To Sell (And To Whom!)
-------------------------------------------------------------
weapons
- try asking a forger
who
can smelt.
armour
- try asking a forger who can smelt.
Persons - Slaves and Otherwise
------------------------------
You cannot
auction
, sell, or offer for
rent
,
lease
,
loan
, or any other form of
use or transfer, persons or people.
Services are fine.
Slave
auctions
happen, but they must always clearly and explicitly mention that
services - not persons - are for sale or being offered for whatever purpose -
lease, rent, loan, etc. That means clearly and explicitly EVERY
TIME
it is
mentioned, not just once at the beginning of the auction.
(See
HELP MARKET
,
HELP ADS
, HELP CREDIT TRANSFER, HELP CREDIT MARKET,
HELP COMMS
,

,
