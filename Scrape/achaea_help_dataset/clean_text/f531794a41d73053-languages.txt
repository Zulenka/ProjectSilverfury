4.13 LANGUAGE
(See
HELP SAYS
about how to
say
things

language

COMMANDS
--------
SPEAK
- to see what languages you can speak.
SPEAK <language> - to switch into a certain language.
LANGUAGE         - to see what language you are
speaking
now.
Upon your arrival in
Achaea
, you will have the
ability
to speak Achaean, the
common language, plus at least one racial language.
To see what language(s) you can speak, type SPEAK.
To speak a different language, type SPEAK <language>.
To see the language you are currently speaking, simply type LANGUAGE.
Language comes into play in
tells
, yells,
says
, and
shouts
. All
other
forms of
communication
are language-independent.
Each
city
in Achaea also has its own special dialect usable only by
citizens
,
and only for the duration of their
citizenship
. This special dialect is granted
by the
gods
directly to citizens, and is withdrawn automatically by their
power
when citizenship ends. This has obvious advantages for discussing city business
or coordinating with city members.
Those advantages are magnified by the fact that the necklace of comprehension (an item produced by those with the
Conjuration
trade
skill
), does not allow
others
to understand these special dialects.
