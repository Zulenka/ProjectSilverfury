17.3.1.1 Inquisitor - Master of Ceremonies for Quizzes
As the
Inquisitor
, you are responsible for keeping the
quiz
going
in an
orderly manner. The
following
commands
are yours:
INQUIZ <adventurer>       : Adds an adventurer to the quiz.
OUTQUIZ <adventurer>      : Kicks an adventurer out of the quiz.
NEWQUESTION               : This must be done before asking a
question
.
QPOINTS                   : Sets the amount of points the question is worth.
Q <your question>         : Ask a question.
TIMER <seconds>           : Set the timer countdown.
CA <answer>               : Display the correct answer to your question.
ANSWERS                   : List the answers that have been given.
CORRECT <adventurer>      : Declare an adventurer correct and award him the
number of points you set with QPOINTS.
ADDPOINTS/REMOVEPOINTS    : Use these commands to add or
remove
points from a
adventurer's
score
. These are to be used primarily
to rectify any errors you
might
have made. The
syntax
is: ADDPOINTS/REMOVEPOINTS <adventurer>
<points>
WINNER <adventurer>       : Declare a adventurer the winner of the quiz. This
command
also officially ends the quiz.
SAYBALANCE                : This will increase, to about 30
seconds
, the
length of "
say
balance" in the Quiz room. It's
good for keeping people slightly quieter in a busy
Quiz.
