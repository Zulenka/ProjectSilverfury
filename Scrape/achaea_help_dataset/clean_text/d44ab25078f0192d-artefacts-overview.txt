19.10.1 Artefacts Overview
(see also
HELP ARTEFACTS
, HELP ARTEFACT TRADEINS,
HELP
ARTEFACT
TRANSFERS)
Artefacts
are special items that can be purchased only with
Bound
Credits
.
Unless otherwise stated, artefacts can be purchased in Merentesh's series of
shops
in
Delos
. They will reset into your
inventory
frequently, which means
they cannot be loaned out, or stolen(!), for very long. Some only work for
the owner. They never
decay
. They cannot be changed to reset to anyone
else's inventory, so do not
buy
artefacts of any kind if you intend to
sell
them.
The specific artefacts available for purchase are all listed in the
following
help files:
- HELP OFFENSIVE ARTEFACTS
- HELP DEFENSIVE ARTEFACTS
- HELP STATS ARTEFACTS
- HELP MISCELLANEOUS ARTEFACTS
- HELP [<classname>|
KNIGHT
] ARTEFACTS
- HELP TRADESKILL ARTEFACTS
- HELP SHIP ARTEFACTS
- HELP SHOP OF WONDERS
-
HELP PETS
- HELP ARTEFACT ARMOUR
Commands
--------
ARTSEARCH <artefact>
- Search the artefact helpfiles for matching artefacts.
HELP ARTEFACT SEARCH <artefact>
- Alternative
syntax
to ARTSEARCH.
ARTEFACT LIST
- View a list of all of your artefacts
ARTEFACT LIST <type>
- View a list of a specific type of artefact that you possess, like
vials
,
armour
, or
weapon
.
ARTEFACT LIST EXCLUDE <type1> [<type2>]
- View a list of your artefacts, excluding certain types.
How To Purchase
---------------
Many artefacts are available to you in shops throughout the realms, primarily
in Delos, plus the artefact
vial
shop
in
Moghedu
. Consult the above help files
for any additional details on purchasing specific artefacts, such as bows or
Earrings of Sinope.
For any
other
requests or special needs,
email
artefacts@
achaea
.com. Please be
very clear as to what you want to purchase and what you believe it will cost.
This is especially true in cases where you may be
claiming
a discount, or other
special consideration.
Artefacts as Gifts (see HELP ARTEFACT GIFTS)
--------------------------------------------
Artefacts may be given as gifts, but with a
critical
restriction: to buy an
artefact as a gift, you MUST pay with UNBOUND credits. If your credits are
bound, you must use them on yourself. You may not buy a gift with bound
credits.
Beyond that, though this is not unique to gifts, please be aware that
an artefact gift is not transferrable or returnable, though if it is
normally
something
that could be traded in (which excludes
pets
) then
it can still be traded in by the person you gave it to.
Artefacts and Loaning
---------------------
Some artefacts may be used by individuals other than the owner. We
consider
this a perk, and you are free to lend your artefacts to
others
to use. This
functionality may change for some or all artefacts in the future.
Artefacts that must be
wielded
to be used will be useless to anybody but the
owner. This has been true since at least 2005. So don't bother trying to
loan
out an artefact you have to
wield
to use.
Note About Stacking
-------------------
Artefact effects (for similar artefacts) don't combine or stack. If you have,
say
, a Collar of
Ceylon
and a Collar of
Agatheis
, both of which would normally
boost damage of a magical
nature
, then only one will boost damage of a magical
nature. If you have a Logosian
Ring
and a Mayan Ring, then only one will
increase the value of sipping a
health
elixir
. In general, the best item rules,
but there may be exceptions. So don't count on having two artefacts and somehow
stacking their effects.
