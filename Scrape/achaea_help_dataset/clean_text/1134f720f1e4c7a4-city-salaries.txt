10.17 City Salaries
The
city
salary
allows a government to pay the various members of the city
who
work daily to keep the city
running
!
Your city leaders can set the amounts each position earn per
month
and then the
gold
is automatically distributed from city coffers directly to the
bank
in your city on the 1st of Sarapin each year.
If you are removed from the city during a salary year, what you had earned will still
deposit
to that city's bank, but you will need to retrieve it if you
wish
to use it.
COMMANDS
FOR ALL
CITIZENS
:
--------------------------
CITY SALARY - Will
show
most citizens what they are currently earning.
COMMANDS FOR
TREASURER
, AND COUNCIL:
---------------------------------------------
CITY SALARY POSITIONS - The positions for which a salary can be assigned.
CITY SALARY LIST      - The positions which have a salary assigned.
CITY SALARY OWED      - The sum of how much your city owes for
salaries
.
- This total will build until the end of the year.
COMMANDS FOR CITY COUNCIL/LEADERS:
----------------------------------
CITY SALARY OWED ALL       - List each salary accrued.
CITY SALARY OWED <person>  - List the salary accrued to a specific
citizen
.
CITY SALARY SET CUMULATIVE <YES/NO>
- Set whether or not someone with multiple positions can earn multiple salaries.
- If no, the highest of their salaries will be paid.
CITY SALARY SET <position> <monthly gold amount>
- Set the monthly gold amount to be earned by a position.
- This is capped at 50,000 gold.
