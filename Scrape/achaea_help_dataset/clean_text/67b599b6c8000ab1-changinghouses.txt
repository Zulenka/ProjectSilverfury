8.2.2 Changing Houses
(See
HELP
8 for much information on
houses
,
HELP QUITTING
, and
HELP JOINING A HOUSE, HELP
CHOOSING
A
HOUSE
)
To change from one House to another, you must first
quit
your current House.
Let's
say
your current house
name
is the
Virtuosi
:
QUIT VIRTUOSI
(after the QUIT, put whatever your house name is)
Now you are no longer a
member
of any House, you should seek out the next House
which you
wish
to
join
. Then, inquire with those listed in
HELP HOUSE
<name>.
For a list of the current Houses in
Achaea
, simply enter: HOUSE LIST.
For most houses, young applicants will be able to complete the
city
tasks
they
are given, which will allow them to join a house. Otherwise, you will need to
seek out the House leadership. To find someone in the House leadership, enter
HELP HOUSE
<housename> and
look
for
names
after the
title
"Able to induct".
(See HELP 8 for much information on houses,
HELP QUITTING
, and
HELP JOINING A HOUSE, HELP CHOOSING A HOUSE)
