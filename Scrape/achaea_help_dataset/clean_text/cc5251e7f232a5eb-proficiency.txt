13.4.2 PROFICIENCIES
(See
HELP
PROFICIENCY
TEACHERS
)
With this
ability
, you specialise in certain
classes
of
weapon
such as the
longsword, broadsword, javelin,
halberd
, flail, and
others
. In order to gain
proficiency, first seek out someone with the knowledge to
teach
you that
specific weapon (HELP PROFICIENCY TEACHERS).
Proficiencies
cost 100
lessons
each.
To
learn
a weapon proficiency:
LEARN PROFICIENCY IN <weapon name> FROM <proficiency-teacher>.
After you have given your lessons to the
teacher
, you must
go
out and
practice
with the weapon you are seeking proficiency in. Simply go out and use the
weapon as it was intended and eventually you will master it.
To
show
what you are
practising
, or proficient in, use
PROFICIENCIES
Proficiencies based on
Class
----------------------------

Weaponmastery
specialisation
