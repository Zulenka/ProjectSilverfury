19.13.10 Item Reactions
Item
reactions
can add some personality and flair to your
day
to day
inventory
!
These are simple
emote
style
reactions without secondary conditions or delays or effects.
Any
reaction
will automatically have a 30 second cooldown, so that it cannot be spammed.
You will need to
fill
out both the first and third person view of a reaction.
The item must be non-
decay
and resetting before a reaction can be added.
Reaction
customisations
cost 50
bound
or
customisation
credits
.
CUSTOMISATION NEW FOR <item#> REACTION
- Create a reaction customisation for your item.
You can add responses for WORN, REMOVED,
WIELDED
, UNWIELDED, SHEATHED, DRAWN*, TOUCHED, TURNED, PULLED, PUSHED.
*
Drawing
a
weapon
will also
fire
any wielded reaction. Wielded first and then drawn if both are present.
As with any customisation, any magic referenced needs to make
sense
for your
character
and the item/action. Reactions that imply a God is working through your item/self will require
IC
permission
from that God.
If you want a reaction more complex than the
system
permits, please
email
customisations@
achaea
.com with all of the specifics and they will do their best to
assist
.
