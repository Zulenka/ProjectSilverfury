Found for sale every twenty-six
Achaean
years or so,

decorations
are

rage
.  These include:
Candy Canes          -
speed
230, to-hit 250 - these are
weapons
of choice!
Uses the same
proficiency
as swords!

Trees
- Drop 'em anywhere. They can't be moved thereafter.

LIGHT
