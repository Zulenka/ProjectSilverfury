19.10.29 Caskets
The
Tomacula
Graveyard
has a number of derelict
caskets
and these can only be opened with
keys
.
Keys can be purchased from the
website
or found in the
renown
shop
! (If you have partial keys from a previous
Delos
Bank
Vault
promotion
, you can still
buy
the
key
pieces
on the website to create a full key for the caskets too.)
When you have a key you can visit the graveyard in Tomacula where a set of 64 caskets wait to be unlocked (in an 8x8 grid).
The prize list that is hidden behind the locked caskets is unchanging and listed below, but which door they are behind will be subject to change.
Before you choose which tomb to open you can even see what has been won from
already opened coffins!
When there are no prizes left in the graves, the caskets will reset the contents to full once more! The
board
will also reset when the special prize is claimed, as well as every in game
month
on the 1st.
CASKETS                       - view the layout
CASKETS OPEN NUMBER <#>      - open a specific unopened casket
CASKETS OPEN <x> <y>         - open an unopened casket by coordinates
CASKETS OPENED               - list all opened caskets
CASKETS CLOSED               - list caskets still to be opened
CASKETS <number> / <x> <y>   - view a specific casket
5
caskets will each conceal 25 scintillating gems,
3
caskets will each conceal a silver giftbox,
5 caskets will each conceal 75
customisation
credits
,
10 caskets will each conceal 65 credits (
bound
),
5 caskets will each conceal 3
artefact
discount vouchers,
10 caskets will each conceal a decorated sleeve,
5 caskets will each conceal a page from the Tome of
Muses
,
1 casket will conceal 150 credits (unbound),
1 casket will conceal 3 Mayan
crowns
,
1 casket will conceal a dagger of
reincarnation
,
5 caskets will each conceal a supreme scrapper trainer's token,
1 caskets will conceal
2
decorated sleeves,
5 caskets will each conceal a bronze giftbox,
5 caskets will each conceal a pair of artefact
vials
,
1 casket will conceal a
phial
of shimmering
ink
,
and,
1 casket will conceal the special prize*
* The special prize varies and changes when the board resets, it will always be one of the
following
:
- 800 credits
- A golden giftbox
- A platinum giftbox
- A token that can be exchanged for any
talisman
piece
