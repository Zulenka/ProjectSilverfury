6.11.3 Shop Signs and the Licensed Master Shopkeeper
(see
HELP SHOP POLICIES
and
HELP
BINS
)
MASTER
SHOPKEEPERS
ONLY:
SHOP
SIGNS
-----------------------------------
Licensed master shopkeepers, and no one else, may purchase shop signs. Shop
signs may only be purchased in
shops
owned by master shopkeepers. If you are
standing in your shop and do not yet have one, you will be notified that you
can make the purchase, and the cost of it.  Shop signs are non-refundable and
non-returnable, so
consider
carefully before purchasing. No more than one shop
sign may appear in any shop.
HOW TO USE THE SHOP SIGN
------------------------
Simply enter your shop and then WRITE SIGN. The rest is just like any
other
writing
session (writing a
letter
, posting a
news
item, etc).
LIMITATIONS and
LANGUAGE
------------------------
The content of the shop signs is subject to the language rules of our realms
(HELP LANGUAGE RULES). Violating shop signs may result in removal of the sign
and/or loss of
license
, possibly permanently.
CONFIGURING THE SHOP SIGN
-------------------------
You may
control
the colour of your SHOP SIGN as a
configuration
option. Type
CONFIG
COLOUR and you will see the setting there: CONFIG COLOUR SHOP_SIGN
<colour>.
Everyone that enters your shop will see the sign at that colour, no matter what
their config settings may be for viewing 'things' in rooms. If the person
entering does not have
ansi
colour enabled, then the sign will not be coloured
differently from
anything
else.
(see
HELP SHOP POLICIES
and HELP BINS)
