4.7 TITLES
Titles
: What are They?
----------------------
Titles in
Achaea
are up to 50 characters before and after your
name
. The part
before your name is called the
prefix
. The part after is the
suffix
. This means
you could have a 20
character
prefix and 30 character suffix, or any
combination
thereof to max of 50 characters.
For instance: The Bakhu,
Rider
of the Apocalypse.
PREFIX: The
NAME  : Bakhu
SUFFIX: , Rider of the Apocalypse
How do You Get a
Title
?
-----------------------
To gain a title, someone powerful must grant it:
house
leader, a high-
ranking
member
of your
city
, or a
Divine
Patron.
DO NOT
ISSUE
ASKING FOR A TITLE!
You may, however, politely submit an issue requesting a title be removed. This
may or may not be done for you, depending on the title and why you have it.
Can I Ask any God for a Title?
------------------------------
You can do
anything
you want. For those of you
who
wish
a long life,
read
on.
DO NOT ASK JUST ANY
IMMORTAL
or GOD for a TITLE!
If you ask a God for a title or title change, be quite sure that that Immortal
is a patron of some organisation of which you are a member! If that Immortal
is not a patron of yours in any
sense
, then it's
time
to notify next of kin
and set your worldly affairs in order.
Fees
----
Changing
someone's title costs 50
gold
per suffix or prefix changed. Everyone
gets the first prefix and suffix free.
You can see an adventurer's titles by typing
HONOURS
<adventurer>.
Rules for titles:
1) A title is just that, a title. It is not a sentence.
2
) No acronyms in titles (TGO, OJB, etc).
3
) Overly silly titles (our discretion) are bad.
4) Titles that imply you are immortal, or a god, are bad
("The Goddess of...," "The mighty
Celani
," etc.)
5
) Titles that purposely
attempt
to obscure your name are bad.
("Jack Nimby, Proficy Valaria, Ralph, Silvaria" for example)
* The only exception here is Squires who may be required to wear a title accordingly.
6
) Make sure that references to
immortals
are OK with them.
("Danniko, Maya's minion": be VERY SURE that
Maya
is OK with it.)
Good titles:
------------
Lancelot, the Lion-hearted.
Druid
Lancelot Fionuaidh.
Freya Xanne Kaer-Leigh
Lord Buckthorn of
Ceylon
Bad titles:
-----------
The Lancelot
Project
Smiling sweetly, Beth the Pouncer
Generally, shorter is sweeter. Trying to cram all sorts of information into
your title just makes you
look
silly. In any case, most of you have perfectly
acceptable titles. It's just a few that don't, and you will likely be hearing
from us.
