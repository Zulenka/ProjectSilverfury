19.24.25 Conclave Talismans
The "Conclave"
talisman
set:
-------------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Trading
between players (pieces only)
- Whole talismans bought from the Delosian
shop
.
- Partial completion (see below).
NOTE: The
Conclave
set is no longer used in
promotions
(
other
than generic "all talisman"
offerings
) and exists in the Delosian Talisman Shop in order to facilitate partial completion of otherwise unfinishable
artefacts
, as well as the Grand
Magi
Robes, but not including sprites or top hats unfortunately!
In addition, purchasing Conclave talismans will not adhere to your faction from the Conclave
event
and this cannot be changed.
The Conclave set contains the following talismans:
---------------------------------------------------
A magical hourglass           - TALISMAN INFO HOURGLASS
- Priced at 75
credits
- This functional hourglass will allow its owner to TURN HOURGLASS <x>
MINUTES
and its sands will slowly
pour
through. When the last grain has passed, it will alert those in the room.
A Brooch of the Tempest       - TALISMAN INFO TEMPESTBROOCH
- Priced at 150 credits
- This brooch will prevent the
wind
from blowing you off course when
flying
.
Prospero's Lesser
Vault
- TALISMAN INFO LESSERVAULT
- Priced at 350 credits
- A talisman version of Prospero's Lesser Vault, doubling the capacity of the owner's
rift
.
A
Ring
of Flying              - TALISMAN INFO FLYINGRING
- Priced at 400 credits
- Allows the owner to FLY.
A buckawns amulet             - TALISMAN INFO BUCKAWNS
- Priced at 800 credits
- Enchanted to allow its owner to dodge the sticky strands of a
web
tattoo
, much like its
artefact
equivalent.
An Armband of Celerity        - TALISMAN INFO CELERITY
- Priced at 500 credits
- Gives you an extra
move
/second before the "Don't be so hasty"
message
kicks in.
A Grimoire of Adaptation      - TALISMAN INFO ADAPTGRIMOIRE
- Priced at 1000 credits
- This grimoire will allow you to reset your
traits
up to once per
RL
day
.
A Stygian Pendant             - TALISMAN INFO STYGIANPENDANT
- Priced at 1200 credits
- This pendant increases your chance of
critical
hits by
6
%.
A pair of atavian wings       - TALISMAN INFO ATAVIANWINGS
- Priced at 2000 credits
- An enhanced version of eagle's wings, much like its artefact version.
Robes of the Grand Magi       - TALISMAN INFO
GRANDMAGI
- Priced at 3000 credits
- An upgraded version of the Robes of the Magi, this confers a reduction in both the
mana
and
willpower
cost of many spells.
Partial Completions
--------------------
If you have two or three pieces of a talisman in the Conclave set you can TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL <talismanname> to complete it for the remainder of the
bound
credit
value of the item as it is in the Delosian Talisman shop.
DOING THIS WILL COMPLETE THE TALISMAN AND
BIND
IT TO YOU.
Talismans completed in this manner will have an altered
tradein
that reflects its credit worth, including the credit purchased completion.
I.E
You have: cruciblebase and cruciblebowl.
You use TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL
CRUCIBLE
and pay 500 bound credits.
This is half the cost of the crucible in the shop because you already have half of the pieces.
You get: a completed soulfire crucible talisman.
This item has a tradein value of: 346 bound credits.
This is
2
/3rds of 520 credits (The 500 credits paid above, plus your 2 pieces valued at 10 credits each.)
