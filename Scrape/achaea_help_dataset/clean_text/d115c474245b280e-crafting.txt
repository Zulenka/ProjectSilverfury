9.37 Crafting
Crafting
in
Achaea
is one of the best opportunities for self
expression
and creativity, as well as a chance to
leave
a permanent
mark
on the game
world
through your wonderful
designs
.
All adventurers have the opportunity to take up a craft and become licensed in either the
ARTISTRY
,
BEVERAGES
,
COOKING
,
FURNISHING
,
JEWELLERY
,
TAILORING
skills
, allowing the
design
, creation, and (if desired) sale of unique and interesting items of
clothing
, furniture, jewellery, or foodstuffs.
To get started you'll want to choose a craft and get yourself a
license
. See
HELP
<craft> for details!
From there you're an officially recognised crafter and can get to work on creating wonderful
things
! See HELP <craft> DESIGN for information here!
If you are not a licensed crafter but want to find a design that best suits you, our
NDS
system
is the place to
look
!
HELP NDS
will
guide
you!
