I. Why We Ask You to Register
-----------------------------
Registration helps us and it helps you. Registration helps us by giving us an
idea
of the demographics of our player
base
. Registration helps you by giving
you:
- a way to keep your account safe (see (a) below),
- an automated way to reset your password (see (b) below), and
- an easy way to manage your multiple accounts (see (c) below).
PRIVACY
: We respect, and will protect, your privacy. We will not
sell
,
loan
, or
share your registration information. You can
read
more about our privacy
policy
at www.
ironrealms
.com/privacy.
(a) Problems and Keeping Your Account Safe
------------------------------------------
Registration information is the primary method we use for verifying your
identity in case there are any problems with your account. If you
lose
your
password, or believe your account has been
hacked
, we will ask you for the
name
, birthdate, and
email
that you registered with. Obviously forged or
falsified information will be of little value in such cases, for you or us. If
there are any
other
irregularities with your account, accurate registration
information is the best possible safeguard for you.
(b) Automated Password Reset
----------------------------
Additionally, with proper registration information it is possible to reset your
own password by visiting http://www.
achaea
.com/game/recover and follow the
directions
presented there.
(c) Easy Changes!
-----------------
If your registration information changes (change of email address, legal name
change, relocation to a different country), you can simply REGISTER again.
Please keep your registration information current!
We appreciate you coming to play, and hope you enjoy the realms of Achaea. Be
assured that your registration is safe and easy and beneficial to all
concerned.
II. Confirming your Registration
--------------------------------
Once you have completed your registration an email will be sent to the address
you supplied. In order to finalise your registration you will have to click on
the URL provided in the email, or cut and paste it into your browser. This
process allows us to verify that you are the owner of the email address.
(a) Problems With Registration Confirmation - some report that they are not
receiving our registration confirmation email. A few
things
you can try:
- your email
service
may be treating our email as junk/
spam
email.
To rectify this, you may
wish
to check your junk or spam email folder to
to see if our email (from
support
@achaea.com) is there.
- Another possibility would be to add our email address: support@achaea.com,
to your address
book
, which should make sure that your email service does
not treat
emails
from there as junk.
III. Multiple Characters
------------------------
If you have more than one character, registering is even easier. Once you give
your email address you will be prompted to verify ownership of the email using
the confirmation process detailed above. When completed, all of your characters
will share the same registration details. This means, when you update your
registration on one character, all of your characters will automatically be
updated at the same
time
.
IV. Registration Problems
-------------------------
Some have problems with registration, usually because their telnet/
mud
client
doesn't handle symbols as expected. This is most often true with the "@" sign
in email addresses. We've had some
experience
with this sort of problem and can
provide the
following
examples of how to put in the following generic email
address -
me
@myemail.com.
Client          How to put in an email address
---------       ------------------------------
zMUD
me~@myemail.com
GoSClient       me\@myemail.com
Other
clients
(e.g. GMud, MUSHClient,
Portal
GT, Savitar) work without
problems. Just enter the email address normally.
V. About Registering and Inactivity
-----------------------------------
Adventurers may be erased after a
period
of inactivity which varies from a few
days
to a few
months
. The more play time you have, the longer we wait before
counting you as
inactive
and removing your character.
Hours
Played |  Days before
deletion
-------------------------------------
< 1       |         30
< 10      |         90
< 30      |        360
< 100     |        720
If you've played over 100 hours, or have ever purchased
credits
or
lessons
,
your character will not be deleted for inactivity.
We'll send you an email to your registered email address 7 days before your
character is scheduled for deletion, so you'll have time to
log
in and make
sure your character is safe.
VI. Dormant Adventurers and Vacations/
Holidays
----------------------------------------------
We're sorry but there is no provision for making an adventurer dormant, or any
way to protect your adventurer from removal due to inactivity excepting to
connect
from time to time, except for the next section. If you must be away for
an extended period, due to being on military duty,
holiday
/
vacation
, extended
travel, or the like, there is little else you can do besides visit every now
and then, even if only for a few
seconds
- or else take advantage of the
protections noted in VII below.
VII.
OOC
Purchases
------------------
Because of accounting recordkeeping and reporting regulations, adventurer
accounts will not be erased if any purchases have ever been made for that
account from Iron Realms, Inc. This includes
web
site purchases or
mailing
payment, but does not include purchasing credits from other adventurers
directly or via the credit
market
. Another side-effect of making OOC purchases
in this way is that your adventurer may not be suicided or erased.
