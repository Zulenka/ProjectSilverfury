17.7.2 Logosmas
(See also:
HELP VAULT
,
HELP
LOGOSMAS
DECORATIONS
)
The
Season
of Logosmas is celebrated across
Achaea
and is a
time
of joy and
gift-giving. Adventurers take great pride in outfitting their
cities
and homes
with Logosmas wreaths,
trees
, and
other
decorations, while the magnanimous dwarf,
Ironbeard
, is said to bring gifts to lucky adventurers.
EXCEPT!
Ironbeard may have gotten a little
drunk
and scared off all of his helpers. He needs your help to wrap and send the presents!
During the Festive
Event
(
running
through the
month
of December) you can send anonymous gifts with
custom
messages
to players across all of the Iron Realms
games
.
- If you send at least one gift during the event, you become eligible to receive gifts from
others
.
- You can send one gift per real
day
.
- Each gift costs
5
wrapping papers.
- You can earn up to 10 wrapping papers per real day.
Step 1: How to find wrapping paper
----------------------------------
- Wrapping paper is earned via all activities that award
renown
!
Step
2
: Choose a gift type
--------------------------
- View all gift types with:
FESTIVE CATEGORIES
- Decide which category you want to send, then start your present with:
FESTIVE WRAP <category>
Step
3
: Add a
message
---------------------
- See all message
groups
:
FESTIVE MESSAGES
- See messages in a specific
group
:
FESTIVE MESSAGES <group>
- Each message has 1 to 3 blanks that you can customise.
- Once you have chosen, select your message with:
FESTIVE MESSAGE <group> <message>
Step 4: Customise the message
-----------------------------
- See available word groups:
FESTIVE WORDS
- See words in a specific group:
FESTIVE WORDS <group>
-
Fill
the blanks in your chosen message with:
FESTIVE WORD <word> [word 2] [word 3]
Words are inserted into the blanks in order. If your message has fewer blanks than words provided, extra words are ignored. If it has more blanks than words provided, remaining blanks stay empty.
Step 5: Send the present
------------------------
- Check your current present:
FESTIVE WRAP
- Happy with it? Send the present:
FESTIVE SEND
- Not happy? Reset and start again:
FESTIVE RESET
- Want to let fate decide? Randomise parts of your present with:
FESTIVE RANDOM <ALL|TYPE|MESSAGE|WORDS>
Once sent, 5 wrapping papers are consumed and your anonymous present is added to the pool to be delivered.
Presents are delivered in waves roughly once every
hour
, and as you all send gifts, you will fill a global "cheer" meter (see FESTIVE
STATUS
!). Every present nudges it higher, and when it finally hits 100 percent, a notably irascible dwarf will descend upon
Sapience
, rewarding buffs and renown to anyone
who
defeats him.
Senders will also earn special milestone rewards as they help Ironbeard with his deliveries, receiving special prizes when they
reach
1, 3, 5, 10, 15, 20, and 25 presents sent:
- 1 present: a Logosmas chocolate
- 3 presents: a
promo
XP
lantern
- 5 presents: a festive
stocking
- 10 presents: a custom Logosmas hat
- 15 presents: An
orb
vision
of Ironbeard, including a glass orb filled with
writhing
smoke
if you don't have one
- 20 presents: a Logosmas sleigh
mount
- 25 presents: an
honours
line!
Every present you send also grants you a ticket in the Logosmas
lottery
. At the end of the event, three lucky winners from each
world
will receive
credit
prizes:
- 1st prize: 1000
credits
- 2nd prize:  500 credits
- 3rd prize:  250 credits
Command
reference
-----------------
General:
FESTIVE STATUS
-
Show
your daily status and current progress.
FESTIVE SENT
- Show presents you have sent.
FESTIVE RECEIVED
- Show presents you have received.
Presents:
FESTIVE CATEGORIES
- List available present categories.
FESTIVE MESSAGES
- List message groups.
FESTIVE MESSAGES <group>
- List messages in the given group.
FESTIVE WORDS
- List available word groups.
FESTIVE WORDS <group>
- List words in the given group.
Wrapping and
building
:
FESTIVE WRAP <category>
- Begin building a present of the given category.
FESTIVE PRESENT
- Show the present you are currently building.
FESTIVE RESET
- Reset the present you are currently building.
FESTIVE MESSAGE <group> <message>
- Select a message for your present.
FESTIVE WORD <word> [word 2] [word 3]
- Insert one or more words into the next blank space or spaces.
FESTIVE RANDOM <ALL|TYPE|MESSAGE|WORDS>
- Randomise parts or all of your present.
FESTIVE SEND
- Send your finished present.
