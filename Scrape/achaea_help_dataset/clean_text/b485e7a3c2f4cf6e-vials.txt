18.10.13 Phials and Vials
(See also
HELP REMEDIES
,
HELP TOXICOLOGY
,
HELP
FILLING
)
Vials
and
phials
are small
containers
, usually for liquids, often with a
stopper or some way in which it may be closed.
Vials
hold
salves
,
elixirs
,
venoms
, and the like. Vials hold 200 sips.
Phials hold the various compounds crafted by an
Alchemist
skilled in
Formulation
.
Vial
contents can be consolidated with the CONSOLIDATE <fluid>
command
, which
will
pour
all of your elixirs into the fewest number of vials. CONSOLIDATE VIALS
will consolidate all fluids in all of your vials at once.
You can POUR from or into a vial:
POUR [# of sips] <
container
|fluid> IN|INTO <container|RIFT>
It is also possible to
FILL
a vial from the
rift
or a
tun
:
FILL <vial> WITH <fluid> FROM <tun|RIFT>
(See also
HELP REMEDIES
,
HELP TOXICOLOGY
, HELP FILLING)
