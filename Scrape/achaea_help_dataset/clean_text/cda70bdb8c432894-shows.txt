4.1 SHOW - to see just about anything about your adventurer
(See also:
HELP STAT
,
HELP
SCORE
)
The
SHOW
command
is a single place to
look
at
things
about your adventurer.
Three sorts of options are available to SHOW:
- Things that are available elsewhere in exactly the same form,
(
AB
,
ALLIES
,
BANKACCOUNTS
,
BLOODLINE
,
CREDITS
, DEF, DIAG,
ENEMIES
, FEELINGS,
HONOURS
ME
,
IH
,
II
, ME,
NEWS
, QSC,
QW
RIFT
, SCORE,
SKILLS
,
STAT
, and
WHO
)
- Changed or combined versions of things available elsewhere, and
(
CON
,
DEX
,
INT
,
STR
,
GOLD
, and especially ORGS)
- Information that you can't get elsewhere.
(
LESSONS
IN
skill
, LESSONS LEFT IN skill)
The current SHOW options are:
AB [skill]             -
abilities
you have in a given skill.
AGE                    - your age.
ALLIES                 - who you
consider
allies.
BANKACCOUNTS           - where your stash is.
BLOODLINE              - your relatives and
family
.
CON|DEX|INT|STR        - your con/dex/int/str stat.
CREDITS                - various
credit
information.
DEF[ENCES]             - **
bal
/
eq
** your
defences
and effects.
DIAG                   - **bal/eq** diagnose your
afflictions
.
ENEMIES                - who you consider your enemies.
FATIGUE                - how tired you are currently.
FEELINGS
GODS
|VILLAGES - feelings from
Immortals
or villages.
GOLD (or
MONEY
)        - the gold in your hands. Check BANKACCOUNTS too.
HELP
- this list, in full detail.
HUNGER
- how
hungry
you are currently.
INFO HERE [IH]         - what is near you, in great detail.
INFO
INV
[II]          - what you are carrying, in great detail.
LESSONS                - how many lessons you possess for
learning
skills.
LESSONS LEFT IN skill  - how many lessons till trans in a given skill.
LESSONS IN skill       - how many lessons you have learnt in a given skill.
NEWS                   - the various news sections.
ORGS                   -
city
,
clan
,
house
, oakstone, order.
PORTALS
- how many uses of portals remain to you.
QSC (or QUICKSCORE)    - shorter score.
QW [match]             - quick who list, optionally matching 'match'.
RAGE
- your current
battlerage
levels
.
RIFT                   - what is in your rift.
SCORE                  - your
health
,
strength
, and
other
useful info.
SKILLS                 - your skills and skill
rank
.
STAT [
STATS
]           - hunger, rest,
warcry
/
motto
, and other
statistics
.
WHO [HERE]             - who is active, either in the realms, or with you.
(See also:
HELP STAT
, HELP SCORE)
