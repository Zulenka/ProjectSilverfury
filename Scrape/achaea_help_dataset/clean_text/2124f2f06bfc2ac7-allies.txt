6.5 ALLIES
PERSONAL
ALLIES
-----------------
By typing
ALLY
<adventurer> you may officially add someone to your list of
allies. ALLIES will list
who
your official allies are, and UNALLY <adventurer>
will
remove
an adventurer from that list. You may have, at most, 20 officially
declared allies. If you
wish
to unally all of your allies at once, simply do
UNALLY ALL.
The significance of official allies lies in the way some
abilities
work and in
the way your loyal
mobiles
, and
other
mobiles, will treat your allies. We will
leave
it up to the individual adventurer to discover the particulars of this,
however.
CITY
ALLIES
-----------------
If you are recognised as a formal ally of a city the
following
is important:
- You can use the city
language
.
- Your deaths count for
tank
charging when
defending
.*
- Your kills count for tank charging when
raiding
.*
- Your kills and deaths will count towards a
war
effort.*
- You will not
lose
experience
when in a sanctioned raid situation.*
- You can use call for
help
in the allied city.
ADMIN
STANCE - while you can
show
up to defend/
assist
your ally city in a raid situation, you can be BOUNTIED for participating. This is once per raid, not per
attack
or per
kill
.
* These are obviously referring to actions involving your allied city.
