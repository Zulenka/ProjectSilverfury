6.8.4 Selling Vials
(See also:
HELP SELLING
,
HELP VIALS
,
HELP
SEASONE
)
Rufio
, the Receptacle Representative will
buy
your
vials
. He is generally
wandering somewhere around
Thera
, which is quite close to
Ashtan
, to the south,
and not far from Seasone's hut either!
Rufio, the Receptacle Representative will buy your old, used, or just unloved
vials. The more
months
of usefulness a
vial
has remaining, the more he'll pay,
up to 150
gold
.
SELL
VIAL[#] TO RUFIO
With that, you'll get some gold and help keep
Achaea
clean. For example:
SELL VIAL123 TO RUFIO    for a specific one
SELL VIAL TO RUFIO       if you want to sell whatever's in your hands
Rufio is rather excited by the
idea
of
buying
artefact
vials. Rather, he was,
until he realised that an artefact vial will disappear and he'll
lose
out. Now
he just doesn't take any suspicious vials, which would include
artefacts
,
resetting, or non-
decaying
vials.

HELP SELLING
,

, HELP SEASONE)
