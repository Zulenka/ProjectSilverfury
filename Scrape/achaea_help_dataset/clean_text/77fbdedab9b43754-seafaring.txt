9.47 Seafaring
(See
HELP MARQUE
,
HELP
SEAFARING
SPECIALISTS
)
Just about anybody can exist on a
ship
on the sea. It is entirely possible
to
sail
aboard a ship without any
skill
in Seafaring at all.
To truly excel and prosper at sea, aboard ship, requires Seafaring.
Something
New
-------------
Seafaring has an exciting new approach which changes the options usually found
in
other
Achaean
skills
where
abilities
are always gained in the same order. As
with other skills, you
learn
Seafaring by spending
lessons
to increase your
overall capacity in the skill. The new approach relates to Seafaring
specialisations
, which means that any two practitioners of Seafaring are likely
to have a different set of abilities.
Seafaring
Promotion
Points
--------------------------
Each skill
rank
(HELP SKILL
RANKS
) you gain in your
study
of Seafaring grants
you one or more SPPs - Seafaring Promotion Points. These SPPs may be used to
increase rank in one of the five Seafaring specialisations: deckhand,
weapons
,
helm, watch, and
command
. The grand total of SPPs you may gain is about thirty
two if you study Seafaring all the way to Transcendent. This would allow you to
reach
the maximum rank of five in two specialisations, with a couple of SPPs
left for other specialisations.
The Five Seafaring Specialisations
----------------------------------
1) Deckhand - stability, repairs, and boarding
enemy
ships
2
) Weapons  - aiming, firing, and fixing ship weapons
3
) Helm     - ship handling,
bearings
, manoeuvres, and whirlpools
4) Watch    - crow's nest, bearings, ship scanning, and ship
combat
powers
5
) Command  - more effective operations, and several powerful
seaspells
Each
specialisation
has ranks one through five. Increase your rank in a
specialisation by spending SPPs as follows:
To Gain     Will      Grand Total SPPs
Rank       Cost     Spent to Gain Rank
I      one SPP           one
II
two SPPs         three
III    three SPPs         six
IV      four SPPs         ten
V      five SPPs       fifteen
General Abilities
-----------------
Some abilities are gained by having any specialisation to rank II or above. If
you get any rank II in any specialisation, you'll gain one of the Seafaring
General Abilities. If you get any rank III in any specialisation, you'll gain
another, and so forth.
How to Gain a Rank in a Specialisation
--------------------------------------
Find the proper specialist
who
is able to grant your promotion. See HELP
SPECIALISTS. Make sure you have enough SPPS for the promotion with
SHOW
SPPS,
then
SPECIALISE IN *
(* is deckhand, weapons, helm, watch, or command)
If it works, you spend some SPPs and gain a rank in the specialisation. Check
your current ranks in specialisations by SKILLS.
How to Give up a Rank in a Specialisation
-----------------------------------------
For at least a little while after Seafaring is made available, you will be able
to give up a rank in a specialisation at no cost. That is, you'll reduce your
rank and regain all of the SPPs you had expended to get it. Simply:
UNSPECIALISE *
(* is deckhand, weapons, helm, watch, or command)
If it works, you regain some SPPs and
lose
a rank in the specialisation. Check
your current ranks in specialisations by SKILLS.
