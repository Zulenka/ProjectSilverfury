15.19 Insight Into Events
Insight
is a
system
that allows us, the
Garden
as a whole, to easily convey information of an out-of -
character
nature
about in-character
events
. We understand that in a role-playing game there can often be situations where you need certain information to play your character that can not easily be acquired in-character and insight is meant to address that by allowing us to have a sort of
event
guide
for major events.
Things
to keep in mind:
- Insight is OUT OF CHARACTER. That means that your character should not be verbatim quoting from it and much of it will be knowledge your character will not directly have. Generally we will try to not reveal any information that is meant to be puzzled out so as to keep this simple.
- INSIGHT by itself allows you to see the current event's guide. Your Order can also have a section here but it will not
show
up unless your
Divine
chooses to add
something
. Order insight
might
be related to global events or it might just be something related to your Order.
Deucalion
might
wish
his Order to know that they're all currently burning from the inside out because he had an accident, for instance.
- INSIGHT QCC lets you submit a
Question
, Comment, or Concern about the event (or your Order. You do not need to specify in the
syntax
, we'll handle
who
receives the information). These are
OOC
in nature but should still be respectful to the team behind the scenes. Do not use this
command
to rant or
rage
or you will find your
ability
to use it quickly taken away.
- When it comes to QCC, also keep in mind that
anything
you require an answer for will ideally be answered by adding to the INSIGHT text itself. If your question is of a personal nature, then, or would generally not be answered by the global command, you should instead try something like an
issue
or e-
mail
.
We hope that this system, overall, helps us
communicate
with you in a more fluid and easily -referenced manner for global events. We still expect you to find actual lore and figure out the  event itself in-character, and INSIGHT will only aim to give you context to aid your
roleplay
, not answers or information that could lead to metagaming.
