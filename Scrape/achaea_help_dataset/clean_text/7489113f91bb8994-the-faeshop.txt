19.25 The FaeShop
The
Faeshop
has a number of
artefacts
on offer, some of which are sold only in the Faeshop and
others
that are specially customised versions of existing artefacts themed for the
Fae
!
Shining teardrops are the currency used to purchase artefacts from the FAESHOP!
You can access the Faeshop with the
following
commands
:
FAESHOP LIST
FAESHOP
SHOW
<item id>
FAESHOP
BUY
<item id> [FOR <person>]
FAESHOP GIVE <number of tears> TO <person>
FAESHOP
TRADEIN
<number of tears to tradein for credits>
Artefacts for sale in the Faeshop include:
An amulet of the
Unsidhe
:                                     160 Tears
- Equivalent to a buckawns amulet.
- Lets you avoid the
web
tattoo
attack
from players.
- This also makes you immune to web bombs that are thrown (not those that detonate in your hands).
A
Sidhe
diadem:                                               200 Tears
- Equivalent to an Aldar diadem.
- This diadem speeds up your
equilibrium
regain by about 15%.
A coral staff of
Illusions
:                                   100 Tears
- Equivalent to a staff of
illusion
.
- This staff gives you the
ability
to CONJURE ILLUSIONs.
- The maximum illusion length using the staff is 500 characters. This also increases the maximum length for any
class
abilities
that allow you to CONJURE ILLUSION.
- You may specify one newline
character
in your illusion with the character sequence
- Requires
balance
and costs 1 second of equilibrium.
- Just having the staff is enough: it is not needful to
wield
it.
A magnificent kelpie:                                         10 Tears
- Equivalent to a legendary
steed
.
- Much like their kin in
Annwyn
, this kelpie will change
description
and behaviour with nightfall.
A satyri cornucopia:                                          50 Tears
- Equivalent to a horn of plenty.
- This horn will periodically
fill
with various
food
from the Unsidhe and Sidhe kitchens.
A hollowed length of selkie
bone
:
3
Tears
- BLOW <blowgun> AT <target> to
apply
a random effect similar to Seasone's miscellaneous flasks.
- This item is consumed on use!
A jagged fae sword:                                           100 Tears
- When a player is beheaded by this sword (via
WEAPONRY
BEHEAD), the newly severed head will
speak
, reporting all that the initial owner
says
for 15
minutes
or until the head's owner meets another
death
.
A tear-stained
cauldron
:                                      200 Tears
-
WISH
FOR <damage type> to gain resistance to that type and lower resistance to
other
damage types.
- 10% bonus to the one you choose, -
5
% to all the rest.
- Can switch at any
time
for a small balance cost.
- No cooldown.
- Persists until death or
logout
.
A black fang:                                                 20 Tears
- With this fang in your
inventory
,
wishing
yourself out of Annwyn becomes a much more exciting (and dangerous) affair.
- Simply place it in a
container
if you want safer passage.
A smoked glass sundial:                                       180 Tears
- Grants a
critical
hits and
XP
bonus against the Unsidhe during the
day
and against Sidhe during the night.
- Approximately 5% bonus to both.
A silvery spark:                                              100 Tears
- Detonating a
tank
imbued with this
power
will create a vortex that periodically pulls people in from adjacent rooms, similar to BECKON
style
abilities.
- See
HELP SPARKTANKS
on how to use this power.
