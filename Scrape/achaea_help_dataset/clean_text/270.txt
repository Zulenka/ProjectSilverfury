4.25.2 Joining a House
(See also:
HELP HOUSES
,
HELP TASKS
,
HELP
CERTIMENE
)
There are up to two routes to
joining
a
house
. To see which options are available to you, use HOUSE LIST. You'll see a list
something
like the
following
:
Short
Name
In Which
City
Long Name
--------------  -------------  ----------------------------------------------
Scions
*
Eleusis
The Scions of the
Ithmia
Merchants       (no city)      The Grand Merchant Collective
Somatikos
Hashan
The Somatikos
A * by the short name indicates that you are able to
join
that House by
speaking
with the relevant
denizen
, after completing a few
tasks
for the city (see HELP TASKS). Certimene speaks for those
houses
lacking a city affiliation.
If you do not see an asterisk for a House that accepts your
class
, you will need to
speak
with someone in that House to find out if the house is willing to induct you.
To find someone to speak with in the
Virtuosi
, for example, use HELP HOUSE VIRTUOSI. You'll see something like the following:
The Virtuosi
. . .
[CURRENTLY IN THE REALM]
Able to induct: Meelg, Enihs, Wolg, and Thgirb
Then you could try
TELL
Wolg Excuse
me
: I'd like to join your house. Could you please help?
You are on your way to joining the house of your choice!
