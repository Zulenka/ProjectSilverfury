15.3.2 MULTIPLAYING
(See also
HELP SECONDS
, HELP CHARACTER SHARING, and
HELP
CREDIT
TRANSFERS)
Multiplaying
, which is having more than one
character
logged in at once, in
Achaea
is strictly forbidden. While we do not mind second characters (for more,
see HELP
SECONDS
), having two (or more) characters logged in at once is not
allowed.  This also applies to having any of your characters be involved with,
or helping each
other
in any way.
The punishment for violation of multiplaying can be quite severe, often
involving permanent loss of one or more of the characters involved.
When two characters
connect
from the same internet address at the same
time
,
and if the registrations match or either is falsified, then that's
multiplaying. Don't
tell
us you are brothers, or pals in an internet cafe -
prove it by
registering
. Until then, you are violating this rule.
(See also
HELP SECONDS
, HELP CHARACTER SHARING, and HELP CREDIT TRANSFERS)
