12.1.1 YGGDRASIL
Yggdrasil
is the single largest living entity in existence, its branches stretching forth to touch lands and realms innumerable as they span all worlds and none across
planar
barriers and planetary confines uncharted by even the most
intrepid
Gods
, lesser
immortals
, and adventurers. Although it
might
take the form of a mighty oak tree to the
vision
of mortals, it is believed this is simply an interpretation of the mind, an
attempt
to perceive and comprehend an infinite spiralling
web
of trails across all Creation that may never be fully understood by any
save
the
Logos
Himself.
Supporting
Achaea's highest mountains - the Pillars of Heaven - the massive trunk of Yggdrasil acts as a metaphysical highway, its known tiers comprised of behemothic branches large enough to
support
entire nations, for such is theimmensity of the
World
Tree's span. Within each burns the unending, eternal
Flame
, each extrusion of the blazing
beacon
offering
a gateway to a distant
plane
with its
embrace
.
The Pilgrimage of Yggdrasil is not for the faint of heart. From the lowest depths of Frysta to the highest reaches of Mannamot, every step upon the planar pathway offered by the World Tree is a step further away from your ownreality towards a distant world, its unknown and potentially dangerous inhabitants, and the risk of a far worse fate than simple
death
.
The Flame of Yggdrasil is in many ways the
key
to extraplanar travel for those incapable of willing themselves into the Beyond. Simply offer it your EMBRACE and let your mind wander while it carries you away...
