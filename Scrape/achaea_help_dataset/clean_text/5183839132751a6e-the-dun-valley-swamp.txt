12.9.4 The Dun Valley Swamp
Filled with twisted creatures and malevolent spirits, the southern swamps of
Dun
Valley are a cesspool filled with dark magic. To enter these swamps, one
must find their way through the thick vines in the northern swamps.
Kloreme, twin sister to Haag the Swamp Witch in the
bog
near
Ashtan
, lives
within the swamps and is the reason for the perverse, warped animals that
wander her domain. Unable to keep herself alive through her own magic, she
uses the
lost
souls that wander near her hut to feed her own life force.
It is also rumoured that she keeps quite a dangerous
pet
that at
times
breaks
free of her
control
.
