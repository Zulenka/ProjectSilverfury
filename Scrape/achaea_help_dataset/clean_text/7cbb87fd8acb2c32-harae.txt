12.9.6 The Isle and Village of Harae
A small
island
in the Western Ocean just off the Northern
Vashnars
, the mountainous Isle of
Harae
harbours
a small volcano, and at the
base
of it, the ashen remains of a now ruined village. Once home to tribe of people known as the Haraekanewa, the village of Harae used to worship the volcano as a God,
offering
yearly sacrifices to appease the burning peak.
Other
inhabitants of the isle once included vicious wasps, and mindless, savage
mud
-men called the poharaehe.
In the year 973 AF, the plotting of the mage Gaderian led to the volcano's eruption and the subsequent awakening of a paramount
fire
elemental which ultimately caused Harae and its inhabitants to be buried in magma, despite the warnings of adventurers.
