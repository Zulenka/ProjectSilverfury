17.4.4 LOTTERIES
In order to participate in an
Achaean
lottery
, type
LOTTERIES
to see what
lotteries you can enter.
If there is one available, you can LOTTERY INFO <lottery #> to get more
information, including the prizes, the ways to
buy
and the ticket costs.
There can be three different types of ways to buy tickets:
1. You can buy tickets from the person
who
is
running
the lottery.
2
. If the
runner
has decided to allow it, you can buy tickets at the Lottery
Office in
Delos
.
3
. And finally, if the runner has enabled it, tickets can be bought at a
ticket box, the location specified in LOTTERY INFO <lottery #>.
The
syntax
for
buying
tickets is BUY TICKET IN <lottery #>. To buy more than
one ticket, you can do BUY <amount> TICKETS IN <lottery #>. You want to buy
tickets for someone else? Just do BUY TICKET IN <lottery #> FOR <person>.
The Lottery Office can be found in Delos, next to the Crafters Union.
When the lottery is over, the
winning
tickets are drawn by the lottery runner.
Gold
prizes will have to be claimed with the runner or at the Lottery
Office in Delos.
In case the lottery had alternative prizes (i.e. non gold), these will have
to be claimed at the lottery runner personally.
Commands
:
LOTTERIES
List all lotteries you can participate in.
BUY TICKET IN <lottery#>
Buys
a ticket in the specified lottery number for yourself. If you want to
buy a ticket for a
friend
, append FOR <friend's name>. Your friend will be
notified of your generosity. Some lotteries may be restricted to let you buy
tickets for yourself only.
BUY <amount> TICKETS IN <lottery#>
Buy more than one ticket. Please note, some lotteries may have a maximum
amount of tickets per person.
LOTTERY INFO <lottery #>
Get information on a lottery. This will also
show
your ticket numbers.
LOTTERY
CLAIM
<ticket #>
Use this to claim gold prizes only. This can be done at the Lottery Office
in Delos or from the lottery runner. Obviously you will have to have the
winning ticket.
LOTTERY <#>
TARGET
ADD <org>
Use this to target your lottery towards a specific organisation.
This will automatically exclude everyone except those added.
LOTTERY <#> EXCLUDE ADD <org>
Use this to exclude members of an organisation from purchasing tickets.
Exclusion to an org will overrule
membership
in a target org.
e.g. LOTTERY 1 TARGET ADD
ASHTAN
LOTTERY 1 EXCLUDE ADD
SAVANTS
These settings will mean that all
citizens
of Ashtan who aren't members of the
Savants of the Wheel will be eligible to purchase tickets or have tickets
purchased for them.
