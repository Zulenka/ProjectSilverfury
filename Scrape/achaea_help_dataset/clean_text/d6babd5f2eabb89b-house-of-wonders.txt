19.10.24 House of Wonders
(see also:
HELP ARTEFACTS
, HELP ARTEFACT TRANSFERS,
HELP
ARTEFACT
TRADEINS
)
Artefacts
are special items that can be purchased only with
Bound
Credits
. Unless otherwise stated, artefacts can be purchased in Merentesh's series of
shops
in
Delos
. They will reset into your
inventory
frequently, which means they cannot be loaned out, or stolen(!), for very long. Some only work for the owner. They never
decay
. They cannot be changed to reset to anyone else's inventory, so do not
buy
artefacts of any kind if you intend to
sell
them.
Note About Stacking
-------------------
Artefact effects (for similar artefacts) don't combine or stack. If you have,
say
, a Collar of
Ceylon
and a Collar of
Agatheis
, then only one will boost damage of a magical
nature
. If you have a Logosian
Ring
and a Mayan Ring, then only one will increase the value of sipping a
health
elixir
. In general, the best item rules, but there may be exceptions. So don't count on having two artefacts and somehow stacking their effects.
All items purchased in the
House
of Wonders with Mayan
Crowns
are resetting and non-decay. They also have no
trade
-in
credit
value and cannot be refunded.
The House of Wonders can be found among the rest of the artefact shops in Delos.
Gifting items from the House of Wonders
-----------------------------------------------
To purchase an item as a gift for another
character
, use this
syntax
: BUY <item> AS GIFT FOR <person> [
MAIL
]. If you include 'MAIL' at the end, it will be delivered via the
postal
service
.
A miniature display
arena
:
3
Mayan Crowns
- Place this display arena in your home and stash up to 25 of your battle figurines inside.
From
time
to time the appearance of this arena
might
shift
to display a different notable location.
PLACE <arena>   - Owner only, in a location that you own
PUT <battle figurine> IN <arena>   - Owner only
TAKE <battle figurine> FROM <arena>   - Owner only
LOOK
<arena>   - For everyone
LOOK <arena> <item id|item keyword|position> - For everyone
I.E:
LOOK ARENA 12345 will
show
item #12345.
LOOK ARENA
IRONBEARD
will show the Ironbeard figurine's
description
(if one is placed within.)
LOOK ARENA
5
will show the 5th item.
GET <arena>   - Owner only, retrieve your display arena
Note: Battle figurines will not
fight
in this arena.
A sturdy display case:
2
Mayan Crowns
- Place this display case in your home and put up to 25 of your treasures inside for all to admire.
PLACE <case>   - Owner only, in a location that you own.
PUT <item> IN <case>   - Owner only.
TAKE <item> FROM <case>   - Owner only.
LOOK <case>   - For everyone.
LOOK <case> <item id|item keyword|position>   - For everyone.
GET <case>   - Owners, retrieve your display case.
A banded chest:                                               3 Mayan Crowns
- This chest holds up to 50 items, and is open/closeable by its owner only.
Putting/getting items from this chest can only be done when the item is in a
housing
room.
This item can only be dropped in housing rooms.
Once dropped PUSH CHEST to secure it in place.
This item can be unsecured with PULL CHEST, then take the chest and drop it elsewhere as desired.

other

An
Orb
of Minor Confinement:                                  15 Mayan Crowns
- This orb offers a number of security features for a home.
DROP this item in a house you own.
The owner of the orb and house can GET ORB# provided that it is deactivated.
ACTIVATE/DEACTIVATE orb to block track and flight in and out of your whole property.
With this active you can still pierce the veil out of the home.
Airlord lightning will not be able to pierce the orb's protection.
Traverse, the Yuthka
Legenddeck
card, and falcons will not be able to pass through the barrier.

or drink stored inside.
A gilded invitation:                                          10 Mayan Crowns
- Extend an invitation to all mutual
allies
to visit your home!
Stand in your home and POST INVITATION to send an invitation to all mutual allies currently in the realms!
The invited have 30
seconds
to ACCEPT INVITATION and must be in same area as property to accept.
If they accept they will be transported to your location!
This item cannot be used during a sanctioned raid.
This item will not function for allies that are enemied to the city if your home is in a city.
This item respects
monolith
sigils
on the accepting party's end but not the sender.
This has a 5
minute
cooldown.

With the bucket of rocks in your inventory, head to an outdoors room in your home that does not already have a pond and CONSTRUCT POND!

An elaborate gargoyle knocker:                                5 Mayan Crowns
- Use this knocker to set a
password
for a door in your house.
DROP this gargoyle in a house that you own.
Owner of house/gargoyle can PUSH GARGOYLE <direction> <password> to set/change a password on a door
direction

When someone
says
the password in the room with the gargoyle the door will open.

PERMISSIONS
SET.
<direction> should be a full word: northeast, west, down, out.
<password> can only be one word.
A gargoyle can only handle one door and password.
There must be a door in the direction.
