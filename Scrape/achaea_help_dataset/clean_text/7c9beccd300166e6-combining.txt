7.13 "GROUPING" ITEMS
(See also
HELP GROUP
for
grouping
with
other
adventurers)
Command
Summary:
-----------------
COMBINE <item> AND <item>
COMBINE <item> [FULLY]
COMBINE ALL
SPLIT <item> <# of ways> [WAYS]
SPLIT <item> <num>/<num>
SPLIT <item> INTO
GROUPS
OF <num>
GET/GIVE/DROP/PUT/FALCON DELIVER/VEILDELIVER
GROUP
<groupeditem> TO <person>
Some item types in
Achaea
can be grouped and treated as a single item. For instance, if you're carrying 16 red
inks
, it's helpful to group them so you can manage them more easily and
move
them all at once. Once combined, they are treated as one, and giving, dropping (but not
inking
, obviously), etc., manipulate the 'group' as a whole. To create and manage groups, you can use the
following
commands
:
COMBINE <item> AND <item>
-or-
COMBINE <item> [FULLY]
-or-
COMBINE ALL
The first will
join
two items into a single group (if they are the same type and can be grouped, of course). The second will combine all of the same type into a single group, regardless of how many you have. Note that this can work both in your
inventory
andon the ground (but it will check your inventory first). The third will
attempt
to combine all items in your inventory that can be grouped into a single group.
Example:
COMBINE RED
INK
(Combines all of the red ink in your inventory into a single
item representing all of them.)
SPLIT <item> <# of ways> [WAYS]
This splits a grouped item into the specified number of groups. It will try to separate them evenly, but if it can't, one group will
hold
the difference. For example, a group of 8 can be split
3
ways, yielding two groups of
2
and a single group of 4.
SPLIT <item> <num>/<num>
This splits the grouped item into two groups with the specified fraction, e.g., 2/3 or 50/50.
Example (while
holding
a group of 10 red inks):
SPLIT RED INK 50/50
(Results in two groups being formed, each with
5
inks.)
SPLIT <item> INTO GROUPS OF <num>
Split the item in to as many groups as possible, each group having a specified number in it. Note, that you can only split into 250 groups at a
time
this way.
GET/GIVE/DROP/PUT/FALCON DELIVER/VEILDELIVER
--------------------------------------------
Dealing with items in groups is a little different than most items. Having a group of items requires the GROUP addative to interact with the entire group and not just one item in the group.
DROP GROUP REDINK
PUT GROUP REDINK IN CHEST
VEILDELIVER GROUP
COHOSH
TO
TECTON
FALCON DELIVER GROUP COHOSH TO TECTON
(See also
HELP GROUP
for grouping with other adventurers)
