8.3.6 House Challenges - How Successors Move Up
(See HELP HOUSE GOVERNMENT,
HELP DICTATORSHIP
,
HELP OLIGARCHY
, and
even
HELP ARENA
for
arena
challenges
and
combat
.)
A designated
successor
to a current
Dictator
or
Oligarch
may
attempt
to
move
ahead (taking over the reins of
power
in the place of the one
who
designated
this successor) by means of a
challenge
.
The challenge simply notifies the Dictator/Oligarch, and the
House
, of the
successor's desire to take over as soon as possible. There is no
election
, or
referendum
.
If the Dictator or Oligarch responds "yes" to the challenge, or does not
respond  at all (within a reasonable
period
), then the Successor takes the
reigns of power and becomes the new Dictator/Oligarch. If the response is "no"
then the challenge ends with no change.
HOUSE CHALLENGE <person>   : Challenge the current leader
HOUSE CHALLENGE
REJECT
: Current leader declines to step aside
HOUSE CHALLENGE SURRENDER  : Current leader agrees to step down
