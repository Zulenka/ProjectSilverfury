4.27 Adventures
While you have
adventures
each and every
day
,
Achaea
offers more than many have a chance to
experience
! With the adventures
system
, Phileo is rewarding people
who
complete his varied
adventure
tasks
with
renown
which can be redeemed for a number of different rewards, even
bound
credits
!
Renown
-------
- Renown is rewarded for completing
quests
and adventures, by taking on leadership roles within your organisations, and by being a
City
Guide
!
- Your current amount can be seen on your
SCORE
RESOURCES and with the RENOWN
command
.
- You can earn up to 2000 renown per day.
- Your daily earned renown resets at
Serenade
, but your total will stack up!
- Renown cannot be transferred or traded, and cannot be dropped.

Adventures
----------
- Adventures are a variety of activities and
challenges
that are part of daily playtime in Achaea! Completing these will earn you renown.
- Adventures automatically complete and reward you with renown as you do the
task
listed!
Commands
are:
ADVENTURE LIST [
seafaring
|conflict|
exploration
|
hunting
]
List the adventures, as you complete an adventure it will be removed from the list.
ADVENTURE THEME
Show
the current theme for a sweet double renown adventure.
Quests
------
- Renown (up to the cap) will be awarded based on completion of
quest
system quests.
- Each quest completed will only count once for renown per person, per day.
-
Questing
is not considered part of any adventure theme.
- Questing in excessively low
level
areas will not reward higher level players, AREAS is a good guide of whether or not an area will be appropriate for you.
- Quests completed in areas that are suitable for
levels
90 and above will reward renown for all.
Adventure Themes
----------------
- There are currently four adventure themes: Conflict, Seafaring, Hunting, and Exploration.
- Adventures are offered in a cyclical system, with the theme
changing
every four Serenades.
- While a theme is active, the first completion of an adventure on theme will reward with double renown!
Organisation Leadership
-----------------------
- City and
House
Leaders (the actual leader, not the council) will earn 200 renown per hour while logged in, up to 800 renown per day or until they hit cap.
- City and House Councils, Order Heads, and High
Clan
leaders will earn 200 renown per hour while logged in, up to 400 renown per day or until they hit cap.
- City
Ministers
and Heads of
Newcomers
will earn 100 renown per hour while logged in, up to 400 renown per day or until they hit cap.
- Soldiers AR3+ and Newcomer
aides
in
Houses
will earn 100 renown per hour logged in up to 300 renown per day or until they hit cap.
Important Note: No matter how many hats you wear in your orgs, only one of these will tick for you, it will always be the most rewarding. These passive renown gains will also not tick up if you are on duty as a City Guide.
City
Guides
-----------
These chosen
newbie
helpers can earn up to 1200 of their daily renown by being "on duty" at a rate of 300 renown per hour.
See HELP CITY GUIDES for full details!
