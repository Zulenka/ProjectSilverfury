17.7.5.2 Race Around The World
HELP RACE AROUND THE WORLD
----------------------------
This is a
Championship
Games
team
race
that will require competitors to fully complete three
ship
racing
courses taking them on several trips in various parts of the
continent
.
Championship points will be awarded to captain of the
winning
ships
, averaged out from the placings in all three
races
.
Teams
may use any ship type!
If you want to be a team of one, that is absolutely fine!
SHIPRACE
SPECIFICS
------------------
The
World
Race will use the formal ship race
system
and courses and tracks and mechanics therein. See
HELP SHIPRACE
for all of the relevant information here.
Additional rules:
- WAVECALL will not be available
- Whirlpools will have a much shorter duration (10 second duration,
5
minute
cooldown on usage)
- Use of ship
weapons
, offensive sea spells*, and
attacking
other
ships directly is not permitted**
* Wavescythe is the main relevant
spell
here, but
anything
that interferes with another team qualifies as disallowed.
** Ships in the race cannot be fired on or
fire
weapons of their own
** Use of ship weapons and attacking other ships directly is not permitted. **
POINTS AND
SCORING
-------------------
Championship points and
credits
will be awarded to the top six finishers:
1st wins 200 credits and 7 Championship points
2nd wins 175 credits and 5 Championship points!
3rd wins 150 credits and 4 Championship points!
4th wins 125 credits and
3
Championship points!
5th wins 100 credits and
2
Championship points!
6th wins 75 credits and 1 Championship points!
