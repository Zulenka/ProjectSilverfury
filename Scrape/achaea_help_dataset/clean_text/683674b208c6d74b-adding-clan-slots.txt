21.4 Adding clan slots
The number of
clans
you may have is initially limited to 10. You may add up to
20 additional
clan
slots, one at a
time
.
To do so, at cost of 25
credits
per slot, enter
BUY
CLANSLOT
PERMIT.
If you have room to add another clan slot, and 25 credits, then you will
increase your limit!
Clanslots
are not refundable, transferrable, or
trade
-in-able.
