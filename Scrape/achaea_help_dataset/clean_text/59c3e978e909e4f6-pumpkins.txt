19.10.31 Pumpkins
Two varieties of
pumpkins
are available in
Achaea
!
Magnificent Pumpkins can be acquired from the
renown
shop
or bought for
credits
in the
Tomacula
graveyard
during October when the store is open.
See
HELP RENOWN
for all information on the renown shop.
The second variety is the Magnificent carved
pumpkin
, one of these is awarded for every
5
pumpkins purchased from the Tomacula graveyard (the renown shop pumpkin does not count.)
- SMASH PUMPKIN to see the options.
- SMASH PUMPKIN <option> to open it and retrieve your prize!
- These can be smashed by anyone (and the prize will belong to whoever does), but pumpkins will always reset to the original owner.
Magnificent pumpkins smash open for: a card sleeve, a blood-stained shard, or a page from the tome of inspiration.
Magnificent Carved Pumpkins smash open for: card sleeves, blood-stained shards, pages from the tome of inspiration, a
custom
-themed
Mayaween
vial
, a cache that can be opened for any promotional
talisman
set (except for the
Logosmas
and Memory sets), or a Mayan
crown
.

Iron Realms Entertainment.
