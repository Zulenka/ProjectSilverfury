4.4.1 Lessons
(See also:
HELP LEARNING
,
HELP
LEVELS
)
Lessons
are the currency of
learning
in
Achaea
. In order to raise or improve
your
skills
, you must spend lessons learning them.
Gaining lessons can be done in these ways:
1)
Reaching
a new
experience
level
.
Every
time
you gain a level, you will gain
5
lessons.
Conversely, if you
lose
that level, you will lose 5 lessons.
Simply gain the level back to get the lessons back.
2
) Obtaining
Bound
Credits
and then converting them to lessons.
See
HELP CREDITS
for information on obtaining Bound Credits. Once you
have any Bound Credits, you can type CREDITS CONVERT # TO LESSONS and
you will receive
6
lessons for each Bound
Credit
.
3
) Bonus lessons. You will be gifted with 2.5 bonus lessons for every Credit
you choose to convert to a Bound Credit, for the first 1000 Credits you
thus convert, totalling a possible 2500 bonus lessons. See
HELP CREDITS
.
Clarification: you receive these bonus lessons at the time you convert
Credits to Bound Credits, NOT at the time you convert Bound Credits into
lessons!
4) ** NEW** The No-Brainer
Lesson
Packages. These get you 1000 lessons for $10,
and are bought from the
website
- http://www.achaea.com/credits
Note that this lesson package may only be purchased for any particular
character
twice.

HELP LEARNING
, HELP LEVELS)
