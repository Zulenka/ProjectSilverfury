8.2.1 CHOOSING A HOUSE
(See
HELP
8 for more information on
Houses
.)
A
House
is a
group
of people
who
will
control
many aspects of your life in
Achaea
for a long
time
. If you can't get along with them, then
think
very
carefully about
joining
.
Selecting
a House
ranks
among the most important choices you'll make throughout
all of your
days
in Achaea. Make it wisely. Get to know the
other
members of
this House, at least by reputation. Ask around. Become familiar with the
classes
(
HELP CLASSES
, HELP
CLASSLIST
) and the Houses of Achaea (
HELP HOUSES
,
HELP HOUSESYSTEM
, HOUSE LIST). If you make the right decision, you'll have a
much better life in our realms.
A great way to see a bit more about the people who
run
a particular House, and
to see what it actually takes to rise in
rank
in that group -
HELP NEWCOMERS
<house>. This is a document written by the leadership of each House and can
reveal a great deal about how they think, and how hard (or easy) it is to get
ahead in the House. And of course, you will want to view the House Charter and
the list of accepted classes in
HELP HOUSE
<house shortname>.
Beyond that, meet the members.
Talk
to them. Ask about them. See what sort of
reputation they have.
Look
for their
names
in the
Achaean
combat
rankings
. See
if they are helpful and responsive, or reclusive and self-centred.
Houses are a great way to get ahead in Achaea. While you can gain
class
skills
without joining a House, you will not be able to benefit from the wealth of
knowledge about the class skills and the ways of the land that a House can
offer, not to mention the individual House
culture
, community, friendships, and
political clout.
(See HELP 8 for more information on Houses.)
