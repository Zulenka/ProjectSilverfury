15.3 Your Adventurer(s)/Character(s) in Achaea
Your adventurer or
character
in
Achaea
are VERY important to us. With certain
restrictions, you may create and play more than one.
Please see:
HELP MULTIPLAYING
,
HELP SECONDS
, HELP CHARACTER SHARING,
HELP CROSS CHARACTER,
HELP AFK
,
HELP PK
, and
HELP AUTO
.
(summarized briefly below).
HELP MULTIPLAYING
-----------------
Never have more than one of your adventurers playing at any
time
. This includes
systems
that allow
others
to pilot your character remotely.
HELP SECONDS
------------
No adventurer of yours may ever
help
another adventurer of yours in any way. No
transferring of
equipment
or
gold
, etc. The only exception is that if you
purchase
CREDITS
with
money
, they can be transferred among your adventurers.
HELP CROSS CHARACTER ACCOUNTABILITY
-----------------------------------
What you do on one character may affect your
other
characters from an
administrative
penalty
standpoint. If you get warned for breaking a rule on one
character, for example, then you
log
on to anther character and break the same
rule, don't expect to get off with just another warning.
HELP CHARACTER SHARING
----------------------
Do not share,
sell
, or transfer any adventurer at any time.
HELP PK
-------
Playerkilling
, or
PK
, is heavily restricted in Achaea, and especially so for
newbies
. Just don't get involved in PK until after your newbiehood.
PUNISHMENTS
-----------
All of these are extremely serious. Most violations of these very important
rules are punished by permanent
deletion
of all related adventurers, with no
second chances.
HELP INACTIVITY
---------------
If your adventurer is absent from Achaea, it may be removed for
inactivity
.
Please refer to
HELP INACTIVITY
for more details. There are plenty of ways
to reduce your risk.
A FEW MORE
THINGS
NOT TO DO
---------------------------
Other very serious things include:
-
AFK
(
HELP AFK
- being away from keyboard while playing)
-
AUTO
(
HELP AUTO
-
automating
various activities)
-
HARASSMENT
(
HELP HARASSMENT
- various forms of
harassing
behaviour)
-
CREDIT
CARD FRAUD (don't do it!)
