9.67 Toxicology
Toxicology
is the creation of deadly
toxins
from a variety of sources. These
toxins can be envenomed upon
weapons
to deliver ailments and
afflictions
to an
enemy
.
Based on what you're making these toxins from, there are two methods for
formulating them:
BREWING     - For
plant
-based toxins.
COMPOUNDING - For mineral-based toxins.
Each
AB
help
file will list both the plant and mineral ingredients for each
toxin
.
Brewing
-------
Have the ingredients and a pot in your
rift
or
inventory
and:
BREW <# batches> <toxin> IN <pot>
You may brew multiple batches of a toxin at once. Multiply the number of each
ingredient by the number of batches you want up to fifty batches.
Compounding
-----------
Have the ingredients and an alembic in your rift or inventory and:
COMPOUND <# batches> <toxin> IN <alembic>
You may compound multiple batches of a toxin at once. Multiply the number of
each ingredient by the number of batches you want up to fifty batches.
Batches
-------
Each batch contains 40 sips. Those
who
have reached the Transcendent
rank
and
gained the Economy
ability
will produce 50 sips in each batch.
Pouring
Toxins
--------------
To transfer toxins from your pot or alembic, simply:
POUR
[# of sips] <
container
|fluid> IN|INTO <container|RIFT>
