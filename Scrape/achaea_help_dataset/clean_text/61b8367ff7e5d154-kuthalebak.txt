12.17.10 Kuthalebak
The town of
Kuthalebak
is found in the shadow of the mountain from which it
takes its
name
, nestled in a secluded valley on the
plane
of Bjarndyr. An enclave
of
Vertani
, much of the lowlands are given over to agriculture, both arable and
livestock.
Exploration
of the peaks surrounding the settlement is fraught with
danger, home as they are to predators and prey alike. Recent efforts to expand
the catacombs beneath the temple have uncovered a fungus-infested network of
tunnels deep under the mountains.
