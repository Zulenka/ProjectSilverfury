10.4.4 TREASURER
The
Treasurer
is responsible for maintaining the operating budgets of the
city
, including all of the
Ministries
. The Treasurer also takes receipt of some major revenue sources, such as
banks
.
It is imperative that the Treasurer be someone of unquestioned
loyalty
, for otherwise, the financial
health
of your city will be in doubt.
The Treasurer does not have
aides
.
The
commands
associated with the Ministry of
Finance
are:
CITY FINANCE SUMMARY [<year>]
- Summary of all city ministries
gold
movement
.
CITY FINANCE REPORT <ministry> [<year>]
- Line by line expenditures and income for a particular ministry.
CITY FINANCE REPORT
CREDITS
[<year>]
- Line by line expenditures and income for
credit
movement.
CITY ECONSTATUS
- See some basic info about your city's economic
status
.
ALLOCATE <amount> TO <ministry>
- Transfers funds from the city coffers to a ministry. Must be done from the Council Room.
ALLOCATE <-amount> FROM <ministry>
- Transfers funds from a ministry back to the city coffers. Must be done from the Council Room.
FUNDS
TREASURY
/ FUNDS COFFERS
-
Shows
you how much is in the city coffers. This must be done from the Council Room.
FUNDS <ministry>
- See the funds that each ministry possesses. This must be done from the Council Room.
READREVLOG/REVLOG
- Works the same as READLOG in
HELP CITYCOMMANDS
, except that it is a a
log
of ingoing and outgoing city revenue. This must be done from the Council Room.
WRITEREVLOG
- Append
something
to an organisation's revlog (if you have
permission
).
APPROPRIATE
BANK
- Take the withdrawal fees from your city's bank and transfer them into the coffers. Must be done from the Council Room.
