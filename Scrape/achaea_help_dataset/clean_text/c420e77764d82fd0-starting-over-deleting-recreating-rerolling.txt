19.8 Starting over: DELETING / RECREATING / REROLLING
If you
wish
to permanently end your adventurer's life in
Achaea
, use the
DELETE
command
. You will be asked for confirmation that this is, indeed, what
you wish to do, and, if you answer AGREE then your adventurer will be erased
from reality * PERMANENTLY *. Be careful in doing this as the decision to
delete is not reversible and you will not be able to use this same
name
for
awhile.
WHAT YOU CANNOT DO
------------------
It is not possible to force someone else to delete their
character
.
* IF YOU HAVE EVER PURCHASED
CREDITS
FOR
MONEY
: you cannot delete your character.*
ALTERNATIVES
------------
Perhaps you simply wish to stop playing. If so, why not just stop? Or, if you
do not trust yourself to stay away, you may try the
following
.
If you wish to limit your own use of your character, one possibility would be
to set the
password
to some reasonably random string of characters that you
will probably not be able to remember. Then your character will persist, should
you wish it back again.
In any
event
, make sure your
registration
information is accurate before you
change the password: this is the only way we can verify that you are the
rightful owner of the character in case you decide to come back later and
request assistance in recovering/resetting your password.
