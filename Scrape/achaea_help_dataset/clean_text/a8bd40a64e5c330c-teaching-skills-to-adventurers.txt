4.5 TEACHING SKILLS TO ADVENTURERS
(See also:
HELP TEACHERS
,
HELP LEARNING
,
HELP
LANGUAGE
TEACHERS
)
Sometimes, you may
wish
to act as the
tutor
of another adventurer. Doing this
will gain the adventurer the same benefit from a
lesson
as if he or she
learned from a
denizen
. You will also benefit slightly from
teaching
those
wanting to
learn
from you.
TEACH
<skillname> <adventurer> will offer to teach someone else a
skill
.
There is never any benefit (in terms of increased skill) to you, as
teacher
,
when you offer to teach someone a skill in this way. There _is_ a benefit if
someone asks to LEARN from you.
(See also:
HELP TEACHERS
,
HELP LEARNING
, HELP LANGUAGE TEACHERS)
