11.7.0 God-King Proteus, the Logos
God-King
Proteus
, the
Logos
reigned in the eras before mortality and humanity as unquestioned ruler of the Elder
Pantheon
. Creation's brightest and most beloved star, Infinite Proteus was the greatest among all the
Divine
children
of Omnipotent Ayar. For this the God of
Gods
was given Lordship of all that Was, Is, and Will Be in the era before
Time
was Time, forming a sacred
contract
betwixt Father and Son. In a hallowed bond to resound down all the long corridors of Eternity, Their ancient words were forever immortalised within the
City
Forever by the patient hands of His most favoured Aldar. Taking
Lorielan
as His wife and
naming
Her Queen of Creation, Proteus' love for His lesser sibling was matched only by the passions with which He delved the world's peoples and the wrath He delivered upon those
who
defied the Ultimate.
From first His Twin Throne and later the very
Font
of All
Things
did Proteus
command
Creation for eons with just, guiding hand. Beneath His
eyes
all was bounded to His Realm of Rule, whether Divine or mortal. Within His mighty wings did He offer shelter from catastrophe, and at His uttered Word even
Yggdrasil
would kneel before His Star-brightened supremacy to quell the grandest of torments. And as the Logos His unconquerable Will moved Worlds in accordance with His Father's grand
Design
.
This story is a lie.
He whom
Divinity
and Creation knew as their God-King was in truth one infinite half of Ayar's own Transcendence. As the means by which Ayar would
experience
not just the love of Creator for Created but also Created for Creator, Proteus' true
name
and eventual reconstitution with Ayar was concealed in His command of not one but all three of the Supreme Realms: Kingship,
Stars
, and Logos. Yet with Their memories twisted at the hand of Ayar to conceal the reality of Proteus, with Their minds forcibly turned from the mysteries of Beginning, the Elder Gods and indeed all the multiverse saw Their King of Kings only as the simultaneous Youngest and
Eldest
, He Who Will Be Forever.
At the close of the Chaos Wars and the revelations they brought, it was Ayar who chose to disperse Entropy and
Discord
from the pre-written
assault
upon Proteus that Ayar had known would come. That which passed between the two in the
events
that followed still remains unknown even to the Gods, yet at its end it was Proteus who chose to return to that Indivisible Unity within which They, now He,
might
continue the work of Ayar's uninterrupted Design. Thus did the Infinite Father and Omnipotent Son take on identity anew,
choosing
for Himself the moniker of
Sarapis
, the Logos.
