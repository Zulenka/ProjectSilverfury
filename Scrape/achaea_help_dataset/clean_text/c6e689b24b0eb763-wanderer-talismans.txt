19.24.19 Wanderer Talismans
The "Wanderer"
talisman
set:
--------------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Promotions
. This will always be announced via the relevant
news
when the
pieces are currently available.
-
Trading
between players (pieces only)
The Wanderer set contains the following talismans:
------------------------------------------------
A large slice of
waybread
- TALISMAN INFO WAYBREAD
- Priced at 50
credits
- You may
eat
this waybread once per
Achaean
day
to fully satiate yourself and
remove
any vestiges of tiredness.
A globe of the aimless              - TALISMAN INFO
AIMLESSGLOBE
- Priced at 200 credits
- If you
THROW
this globe at someone, they will suffer from poor accuracy with their bows for the next five
minutes
.
- Can only be used once every 30 minutes.
A tattered
parchment
- TALISMAN INFO
TATTEREDPARCHMENT
- Priced at 400 credits
- CONSULT PARCHMENT <area> will
tell
you how many non-player loyals are currently alive in that area.
A wanderer's
starchart
- TALISMAN INFO WANDERERSTARCHART
- Priced at 600 credits
- This starchart will increase the radius of your visible
map
when on
board
a
ship
.
A Khalasian
compass
- TALISMAN INFO
KHALASIANCOMPASS
- Priced at 800 credits
- Possessing this compass will increase your celerity when not upon the Prime Material
Plane
.
A featureless sculpture             - TALISMAN INFO SCULPTURE
- Priced at 1000 credits
- Declare that your foe is a mistake (MISTAKE <target>), and you will begin a channeled action which, if it resolves, will slay them outright and
leave
a stone statue of them behind.
- This sculpture will also change to be the last person you slew with it.
A bloody
knife
- TALISMAN INFO
BLOODYKNIFE
- Priced at 1200 credits
- With this knife in your
inventory
(not
wielded
), you may BETRAY <a mutual ally> and plunge it into their chest.
- While this does deal some damage, it will also let you balancelessly farsee that person (requires but does not consume
equilibrium
) for the next thirty minutes.
- You may only betray someone every ten minutes in this way.
Some woven hand wraps               - TALISMAN INFO
HANDWRAPS
- Priced at 1400 credits
- These unassuming hand wraps convey truly mighty
power
upon the wearer.
- You may OVERPOWER <target> <HIGH|HIGHER|HIGHEST> and send them rocketing into the sky after a
channel
(longer for each grade of
strength
).
- You may only overpower someone in this way every five minutes.
Robes of the wanderer               - TALISMAN INFO
WANDERERROBES
- Priced at 2000 credits
- These robes serve as robes of the
magi
and cut all
mana
usage, but also cut
willpower
usage to a similar degree as well.
