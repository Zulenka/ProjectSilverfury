4.4.2 THE ACHAEA SKILL SYSTEM
(see also
HELP CLASSES
,
HELP
HOUSES
)
Commands
:
SKILLS
,
AB
,
SCORE
RESOURCES,
LEARN
.
The
Achaean
skill
system
is based on
groups
of skills. SKILLS will list the
various skills you possess. Most of skills you start out with have no
abilities
in them, but are instead defensive skills. As you
train
in these, your
ability
to defend against the various forms of damage will increase. Use HELP
<skillname> to get information on each of these skills. Some of the skills will
have abilities in them; for instance
Vision
. To see a list of the abilities
that you have in these skills, type AB <skillname>. Remember, a skill is
generally a set of abilities, while an ability is
something
you will
individually do.
If you type SCORE RESOURCES, you will notice, among
other
things
, the number of
lessons
you have. Lessons are the method by which you increase your skills.
(see also
HELP CLASSES
, HELP HOUSES)
