19.24.23 Elemental Talismans
The "Elemental"
talisman
set:
-----------------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Promotions
. This will always be announced via the relevant
news
when the   pieces are currently available.
- Whole talismans bought from the Delosian
shop
.
-
Trading
between players (pieces only).
- Partial completion (see below).
The Elemental set contains the following talismans:
---------------------------------------------------
A miniature air elemental - TALISMAN INFO AIRELEMENTAL
- Purchasable in
Delos
for 60
bound
credits
- A
minipet
in the domesticated
collection
A warblefish's
jawbone
- TALISMAN INFO JAWBONE
- Purchasable in Delos for 200 credits
- OPEN this underwater and the next
thing
you
say
will be heard by all
who
share the underwater environs with you.
- The jawbone will then close for 30
seconds
before it can be used again.
A tiny
phial
filled with the Tears of the Sea - TALISMAN INFO SEATEARS
- Purchasable in Delos for 300 credits
-
POUR
from this phial into a
vial
of fresh or salt water to convert it to the
other
!
A mineral-crusted shovel - TALISMAN INFO EARTHENSHOVEL
- Purchasable in Delos for 375 credits
-
DIG
those pesky burrowers up from deep in the earth (more than 1
level
of
burrow
), none shall
escape
your wrath!
A dripping
mudbomb
- TALISMAN INFO MUDBOMB
- Purchasable in Delos for 700 credits
-
THROW
this in a room and everyone (including you!) will be hit with an effect that increases the
balance
time
on
flying
by a nontrivial amount.
- This item will return to you five
minutes
after being thrown.
- The
mud
effect will fade naturally on its own after a couple of minutes, or can be scrubbed off in a body of water.
A seared glyph - TALISMAN INFO SEAREDGLYPH
- Purchasable in Delos for 850 credits
- This glyph must be ATTACHed to either a
shield
of absorption or a suit of
artefact
armour
containing an absorption
paragon
.
- Every five
times
the armour absorbs a hit from a
denizen
, it will lash out with
flame
, dealing damage to the attacker.
- Note that this item CANNOT be removed once attached.
A stratospheric
harness
- TALISMAN INFO HARNESS
- Purchasable in Delos for 1700 credits
- SHAKE <harness> when in an associated region and of sound body in order to capture the far flung windstreams. These immense forces will allow you to travel to nearby isles.
- Western windstream encompasses
Tuar
,
Tapoa
, and
Ageiro
.
- Eastern windstream encompasses
Zanzibaar
,
Clockwork
,
Colchis
, and
Umbrin
.
- Northern windstream encompasses
Karbaz
,
Suliel
, and
Ilyrean
.
An armoured charge
tank
- TALISMAN INFO FIRETANK
- Purchasable in Delos for 1350 credits
- This item is controlled with the DEPLETE
command
.
- This tank
charges
up as you slay
denizens
. Higher level denizens convey more charge, and the max charge the tank can
hold
at any one time is 7500.
- You may spend charge on various actions.
- For 1500 charge, you may destroy an item similar to a
mushroom
sigil
, but you will not take damage to do so. DEPLETE EXPLOSION <item>
- For 1500 charge you may burn away ropes or webs that
bind
you immediately for a short balance. DEPLETE WREATHE
- For a minimum of 7000 charge (though it will consume all charge on the tank), you may grant
experience
to all that stand in your location, similar to a bard's Chorale. DEPLETE GLORY
- This final effect may only affect you once every
2
hours
and may only be performed by you once every
18
hours.
A sceptre of aqueous mastery - TALISMAN INFO SCEPTRE
- Purchasable in Delos for 2000 credits
- You may
POINT
this sceptre in a
direction
to part water in said direction.
- It additionally conveys the
ability
to CAST
FLOOD
.
A shackle of
Garash
- TALISMAN INFO GARASHSHACKLE
- Priced at 600 credits
- This shackle will convey the
mass
defence
to you for as long as you continue to wear it.
- This is identical to the artefact of the same
name
.
A
fire
pendant - TALISMAN INFO FIREPENDANT
- Priced at 200 credits
- This pendant increases your
critical
hit rate by 2% when worn.
- This is identical to the artefact of the same name and does not stack.
Partial Completions
--------------------
If you have two or three pieces of a talisman in the elemental set you can
TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL <talismanname> to complete it for the remainder
of the bound
credit
value of the item as it is in the Delosian Talisman shop.
DOING THIS WILL COMPLETE THE TALISMAN AND BIND IT TO YOU.
Talismans completed in this manner will have an altered
tradein
that
reflects its credit worth, including the credit purchased completion.
I.E
You have: cruciblebase and cruciblebowl.
You use TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL
CRUCIBLE
and pay 500 bound credits.
This is half the cost of the crucible in the shop because you already
have half of the pieces.
You get: a completed soulfire crucible talisman.
This item has a tradein value of: 346 bound credits.
This is 2/3rds of 520 credits (The 500 credits paid above, plus your 2
pieces valued at 10 credits each.)
