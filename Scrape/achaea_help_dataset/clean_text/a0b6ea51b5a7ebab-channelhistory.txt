3.7.5 Channel History
(See also:
HELP
CHANNELS
)
Channel
History
is available to all on
Newbie
, Deathsight, and
Market
channels
if they have access to those channels normally. Simply HISTORY MARKET, HISTORY
DEATHSIGHT, or HISTORY NEWBIE and you will see the last 60
messages
on the
channel. This could include messages from before you were present in the
realms.
Optionally
----------
Some
cities
may have chosen to acquire history for their
CT
, in which case
HISTORY CT will work. The same goes for
Houses
, in which case HISTORY
HT
and
HISTORY
HNT
will both work and provide two different channel histories.
Clans
and
Orders
may also have history.
It is also possible to specify the number of lines of history you would like to
see. For example HISTORY CT 15 would
show
only the last 15 lines in the channel
history.
A Quicker Way to View History, Maybe
------------------------------------
HISTORY <anyorgname> will also work if you are a
member
of that org, and
the org has paid for channel history.
How To Get History
------------------
This costs 500,000
gold
.
Divine
Orders that
wish
to activate channel history should contact their Divine.
Houses that wish to activate channel history will need to file a Patronage
Request via their Patronage
Denizen
. For this single fee a
House
will get both HT and HNT history.
Clan
leaders can use CLAN HISTORY ADD [CONFIRM] with 500,000 gold in hand to purchase history for the clan they have selected in their CLAN LIST.
Limits
------
HISTORY only records
things
said by adventurers on the prime material
plane
,
plus
Immortals
, whose precise location transcends such designations in any
event
.
Those
who
are astralformed, blackwinded, phased, off of the prime material
plane, otherwise unable to use the channel, or have it disabled, will be unable
to review their organisation's history.
You should not be able to see historical entries from people you have ignored
(this is only the case for clans).
Tell
History
------------
You can check the history of
tells
that you have received using the TELLS
command
. This will list a brief list of the last twenty tells. You should use
SHOWTELL (#) to see the full
message
of a specific tell.
Personal History
----------------
Adventurers are able to write their own histories about their personal lives.
For more information on this see
HELP BACKGROUND
.
