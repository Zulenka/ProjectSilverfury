9.37.6 Cooking
(See also:
HELP
COOKING
DESIGN
)
The cooking
tradeskill
enables you to prepare a wide variety of
food
products
from basic ingredients. With advanced knowledge in cooking you will be able to
design your own recipes, enhance your creations to make them more
filling
, and
preserve
them to make them last longer.
You must be above
level
10 to access the cooking tradeskill.
In order to
learn
cooking, you must purchase a permit for 200
credits
from the
Culinary Institute of
Delos
with
BUY
COOKING PERMIT.
Following
that, pay a
visit to Amarisse, Secretary of the
Crafting
Council and ASK AMARISSE GAIN
COOKING.
BUY COOKING PERMIT
- Use this
command
at "The Culinary Institute of Delos".
- This will cost 200 credits.
- If you have a bronze
trade
seal, please
READNEWS
ANNOUNCE
4247
- Once purchased, this permit will
show
up on
SCORE
LICENSES
.
- This will never expire and cannot be traded in.
GAINING THE COOKING
SKILL
- Make sure you have Bought your Cooking Permit as per above!
- Make sure you have a tradeskill slot empty
-
TRADESKILLS
will show your tradeskill slots
- SCORE LICENSES will show how many slots you have in full.
- Visit Amarisse, Secretary of the Crafting Council
- ASK AMARISSE GAIN COOKING.
If you are in possession of a gilded spoon, you may COOK any item in
your personal
NDS
list without needing the Cooking tradeskill.
These do not have a
tradein
value.
Cooking stoves
--------------
For the low cost of only 200000
gold
, you may REQUEST STOVE from Fronck in
Delos, gifting you a miniature stove that can be dropped in any
housing
room
that you have
permission
to place furniture.
Cooking stoves may also be found in all the major
cities
as well as the Delos
Culinary Institute.
(See also: HELP COOKING DESIGN)
