17.2.5 IBOTG / IBOG - the Irregular Battle of the Genders
This is a worldwide game of
EGGHUNT
(see
HELP
EGGHUNT) with
SHARING
and NOPK
options. In it, the contestants share their
eggs
in order to demonstrate their
gender's
abilities
in planning and coordination or, in later
days
, participate
in a 'noshare' variety of Egghunt (in which case
selling
eggs or aiding the
other
team is impossible).
Selling eggs, or aiding the team of the opposite gender during the
IBOG
will be
met with extremely harsh treatment. Don't do it!

dates
