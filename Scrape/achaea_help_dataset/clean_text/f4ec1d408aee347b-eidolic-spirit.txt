10.8.5 Eidolic Spirit
Spirit
is the resource tithed from areas via Eidolic
Influence
.
Spirit of Fear is generated via conquering areas. Spirit of Gratitude is generated via the
liberation
acts. Both funnel into the
city
via the
font
and can be used as Spirit.
Each area conquered will generate Spirit monthly; this will be tithed to the conquering state at the end of the
month
. There are diminishing returns, so bigger isn't always better on paper. If you
conquer
an area that you have laid out in the
Doctrine
as a
conquest
objective
, that area will generate a bonus amount of Spirit.
The various
quests
that aim towards liberation/morale boosting will generate Spirit of Gratitude. This is cached in the area until it is liberated and then awarded to the liberator at the end of the month. Liberation itself also generates Spirit from a liberated area. If you
liberate
an area you have laid out in the Doctrine as a protection objective, that area will generate a bonus amount of Spirit for you.
Spirit can be spent on a variety of rewards for your city. Some of these rewards are gated by the number of areas held in conquest.
When the city has gained enough Spirit, the City Leader or
Defence
Minister
can use the
following
commands
to spend it.
CITY SPIRIT
INVEST
<powername>
- To use a
power
.
CITY SPIRIT INVESTMENTS
- To see the power list.
CITY SPIRIT INVESTMENTS <powername>
- To see the specifics of a power.
- You must have reached the required areas held at a minimum before you can see a power's details.
CITY SPIRIT RENEW <investment>
- This will cause the
investment
to renew itself when it fades automatically.
- This can be used with the
EMPOWER
, SLAUGHTER, and MAINTAIN investments.
- In the case of MAINTAIN, the power is reactivated when the repair happens (if the power is off cooldown), or when the cooldown ends if a maintain is not pending.
- To toggle the
renewal
OFF, simply repeat the
command
for the investment currently being renewed
(i.e. If MAINTAIN is being renewed, to turn it off, use CITY SPIRIT RENEW MAINTAIN)
CITY SPIRIT RELINQUISH
- This will cost all of your accumulated spirit, and at your next Eidolic Phase, your spirit will
return to its corporeal form, and all conquered areas will be relinquished.
- This command can only be executed by the City Leader in the
War
Room.
- Using this command means that the spirit of your
Guardian
will need to be resummoned from scratch if you
wish
to venture forth again.
CITY SPIRIT
STATUS
- This will
show
the current spirit amount, any active investments, and any investments that are on cooldown.
