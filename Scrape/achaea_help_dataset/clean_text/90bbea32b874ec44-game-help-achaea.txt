15.3.5 Deletion for inactivity.
To minimise the amount of resources spent on
inactive
players,
Achaea
institutes a
system
for
purging
dormant
players after a certain amount of
time
.
The
following
table will give an
insight
into the
policy
in place for these
activities:
Hours
Played |
Days
before
deletion
-------------------------------------
< 1       |         30
< 10      |         90
< 30      |        360
< 100     |        720
If you've played over 100 hours, or have ever purchased
credits
or
lessons
,
your
character
will not be deleted for
inactivity
.
We'll send you an
email
to your registered email address 7 days before your
character is scheduled for deletion, so you'll have time to
log
in and make
sure your character is safe.
