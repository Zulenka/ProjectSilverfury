19.22 Banners
Want to
show
off your
Achaean
character
around the internet? Well now's your
chance! We provide a series of
banners
for use on websites and as
forum
signatures
for your use!
To get started, simply type BANNERCODE and you'll be given the various options
(HTML and BBCode) all ready to copy and paste. You'll notice that the link also
has a
referral
link on it, which means you get rewarded for people clicking on
your
banner
! See
HELP REFLINK
for all of the details.
If you
wish
to hide your in-game affiliations, you may HIDEBANNERORGS, which
will make your
House
and
City
appear as "hidden". You can reveal this
information again with SHOWBANNERORGS.
