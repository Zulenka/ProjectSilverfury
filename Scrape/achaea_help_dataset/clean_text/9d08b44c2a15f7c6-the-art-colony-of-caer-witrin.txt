12.8.8 The art colony of Caer Witrin
Caer Witrin, high in the
Vashnar
mountains off the
Cyrene
road, is a settlement
of
artists
and crafters from all over
Sapience
. Sheltered beneath the mountain
from which it takes its
name
, it is a site of stunning natural beauty, although
the residents are too aware also of the hazards of life high in the mountains.
The bitter
winds
can
kill
the unwary, and the heavy snow that makes the town so
picturesque also blocks off the pass in winter,
leaving
the town isolated. The
locals are friendly and helpful, more so to visitors
who
have proven their
goodwill over
time
. The town
guards
are well drilled, and form a cooperative
and organised unit
defending
the town against any threat.
