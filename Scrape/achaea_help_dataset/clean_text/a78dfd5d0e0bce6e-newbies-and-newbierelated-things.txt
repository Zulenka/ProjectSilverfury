What is a
Newbie
?
-----------------
A newbie is any adventurer in
Achaea
that has not yet reached the 21st
level
of
experience
. If you create a new adventurer in Achaea, then you are a newbie.
What do
Newbies
get?
--------------------
* To take the introduction to Achaea (see HELP
NEWBIEINTRO
)
* Special newbie
attacks
to help you
fight
(see HELP
NEWBIEATTACK
)
*
Godly
rescues
, or
PORTALS
, to help you get around (see HELP PORTALS)
* An easy way to
join
the
class
or
House
of your choice (see HELP
CERTIMENE
)
* The NEWBIE
channel
for
questions
about Achaea (see HELP CHANNEL)
* Access to the
GUIDES
,
Romeo
and
Juliet
(see HELP GUIDES)
* Special places to
explore
, where non-newbies may not remain. These are
Minia
,
Lodi
, and
Mount
Gheladan
. (see
HELP MINIA
,
HELP LODI
, and
HELP GHELADAN)
More about the Introduction
-------------------
The Introduction to Achaea contains two parts:
Escape
from
Miba
&
the Cultist's Conspiracy. This is especially helpful, as it allows
for a new adventurer to seamlessly integrate within Achaea.
What DON'T Newbies get?
-----------------------
The
market
channel for
buying
and
selling
, at least to begin (see HELP MARKET)
and the
ability
to SHOUT (see HELP SHOUT). Newbies also cannot join
arena
events
or
attack
other
players until level 11.
