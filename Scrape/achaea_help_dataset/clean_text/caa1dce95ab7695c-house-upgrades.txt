20.4 HOUSE UPGRADES
(See also:
HELP HOUSING
, HELP HOUSING SUMMARY,
HELP
SUBDIVISIONS
)
Once you've purchased your
house
there are a number of different upgrades you
can purchase for the rooms in your house. These upgrades tend to be fairly
expensive compared to rooms as they represent either serious structural changes
to your house from a
roleplaying
sense
or powerful magic.
Houses
and Rooms: No Sale or Transfer
-------------------------------------
Houses may not be sold, transferred, or traded in, although the individual
rooms and
doors
may be destroyed and some value may be recaptured in that way.
Destroying the rooms and doors of a house returns approximately half
credit
.
Plots
may not be sold if they have houses on them.
UPGRADES LIST
-------------
All upgrade costs* in houses built upon
ships
are doubled.
(*) That's costs, not refund value at
time
of
destruction
.
See
HOUSING
DESTROY ROOM UPGRADE, below, for further information.
Health
regeneration
(keyword: health)
- You and your
allies
will
regenerate
extra health while in the room.
- 40 room
credits
.  (80 on a
ship
)
Mana
regeneration (keyword: mana)
- You and your allies will regenerate extra mana while in the room.
- 40 room credits.  (80 on a ship)
Endurance
regeneration (keyword: endurance)
- You and your allies will regenerate extra endurance while in the room.
- 60 room credits.  (120 on a ship)
Willpower
regeneration (keyword: willpower)
- You and your allies will regenerate extra willpower while in the room.
- 60 room credits.  (120 on a ship)
News
access (keyword: news)
- Anyone may access the news from that room.
- 40 room credits.  (80 on a ship)
Outdoors (keyword: outdoors)
-
Remove
the roof from that room.
- 10 room credits.
- NOTE: not available on ships.
Environment
(keyword: see below)
- Change the environment to one of the available choices. You may only
have one environment per room - for example, if you upgrade a room to
garden
, and it was formerly freshwater, it is no longer freshwater.
- 20 room credits.
- NOTE: not available on ships.
- Environments that may be selected are:
* Garden (keyword: garden)
-
Plants
will grow here.
-
Trees
are added to the room.
* Freshwater (keyword: water)
- Plants will grow here.
- You will not be able to
fish
from this room.
* Beach (keyword: beach)
* Grassland (keyword: grassland)
- Plants will grow here.
* Natural Underground (keyword: nunderground)
- Plants will grow here.
- Alchemical
minerals
may be extracted here.
-
Diamond
dust may be gathered here.
* Constructed Underground (keyword: cunderground)
- Alchemical minerals may be extracted here.
Security (keyword: security)
- Applies
key
sigil
,
monolith
sigil, and
eye
sigil protections to the room.
-
5
room credits (10 on a ship)
Orb
(keyword: orb)
- blocks track and flight in and out of a room.
- You can still pierce the veil out of the room.
- Airlord lightning will not be able to pierce the orb's protection.
- Traverse, the Yuthka
Legenddeck
card, and falcons will not be able to pass
through the barrier.
- This cannot be deactivated, it is always on.
- Not available for ships.
- 5 room credits
No Prism (keyword: noprism)
- For those desperate for
privacy
, this will prevent prisming into that
room.
- 40 room credits.
- NOTE: not available on ships.
Privacy (keyword: privacy)
- Veils your room in a cloak of privacy. People will not be able to
listen in on conversations there. Various
things
are stopped, including
eavesdropping, listening, mind listen,
vodun
/
puppetry
listen,
angel mindread, and forms of overhearing
tells
.
- 80 room credits.  (160 on a ship)
- NOTE: An option to this is a
bed
, which is a much more powerful version
of a privacy room. See
HELP BEDS
for more details.
Stables (keyword: stables)
- Allows you to STABLE <LOYAL> (be it
mount
,
pet
, or
minipet
.)
-
Mounts
will no longer need or use
food
while stabled.
- If the loyal resets, it will now reset to that room instead of your
inventory
.
- You may UNSTABLE <LOYAL> to remove it.
- 80 room credits.  (160 on a ship)
Gates (keyword: gates)
- Prevents stabled mounts from being summoned with WHISTLE <mount>
or the platinum whistle
artefact
.
- Requires the stables upgrade to be purchased first.
- 20 room credits.  (40 on a ship)
Observatory (keyword: observatory)
- Allows you to
LOOK
OUTSIDE and see the
subdivision
map
.
- Not available in out-of-subdivision houses.
- 40 room credits.
Furniture Stasis (keyword: fstasis)
- Prevents furniture
decay
in the room.
- 80 room credits.  (160 on a ship)
Humgii
(keyword: humgii)
- Your own pet humgii! Will
eat
almost
anything
given to him.
- Can have his
description
customised (HELP
CUSTOMISATIONS
).
- 50 room credits.  (100 on a ship)
Chess
board
(keyword: chess)
- A permanently installed chessboard for your house.
- 40 room credits.  (80 on a ship)
Fountain (keyword: fountain)
- A bubbling fountain that supplies a stream of fresh water.
- Can have its description customised (HELP CUSTOMISATIONS).
- 20 room credits.  (40 on a ship)
Plaster (keyword: plaster)
- Prepare a room for a mural!
- See
HELP ARTISTRY
-
3
room credits. (
6
on a ship)
Cellar (keyword: cellar)
- A room upgrade that allows the
placing
of Kegs/Casks/Barrels from
furnishing
to age and store
beverages
!
- 10 room credits. (20 on a ship)
Plot
sign (keyword: sign)
- A small wooden sign outside your subdivision home.
- Must be purchased/traded-in from the keyroom of your house.
- Not available for ships.
- 20 room credits.
Crystal Ornament (keyword: ornament)
- Allows the home owner to set up to three ambience
messages
that will be displayed randomly.
- 40 room credits.  (80 on a ship)
-
Syntax
:
PUSH ORNAMENT <message>   Add a
message
.
PULL ORNAMENT <#>         Remove a message.
TOUCH ORNAMENT            List current messages.
TURN ORNAMENT             Toggle ornament's visibility.
COMMANDS
--------
HOUSING
BUY
ROOM UPGRADE <keyword>
-
Buys
a specified upgrade for the room you are standing in. All upgrades
cost TWICE as much for houses on ships.
HOUSING DESTROY ROOM UPGRADE <keyword>
- Destroys a specified upgrade in the room you are standing in and
refunds you half the room credits that the upgrade currently costs.*
(*) NOTE: the refund value is half the room credits that the upgrade
costs appropriate to where it is located. If you own a home on a ship,
where upgrades cost double, you will be refunded half of the doubled
purchase cost.
HOUSING
SHOW
ROOM UPGRADES
-
Shows
the upgrades for the room you're in, if you own it.
(See also:
HELP HOUSING
, HELP HOUSING SUMMARY, HELP SUBDIVISIONS)
