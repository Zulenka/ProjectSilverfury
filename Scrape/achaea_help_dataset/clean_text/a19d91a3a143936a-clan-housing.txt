20.7 Clan Housing
Ownership of a built
house
can be transferred to democratic
clans
!
HOUSING
TRANSFER TO <clanalias>
***** This is irreversible, and non-refundable *****
***** This is irreversible, and non-refundable *****
***** This is irreversible, and non-refundable *****
- This costs
2
,000,000
gold
and the housing owner and
clan
leader must be
standing in the house, or on the
plot
the house is built on.
- The house owner must initiate this process, and the clan owner must be
present with the fee to accept the transaction.
***** This is irreversible, and non-refundable *****
***** This is irreversible, and non-refundable *****
***** This is irreversible, and non-refundable *****
Clan-owned housing will not be transferred under any circumstances. Should the
house on the plot be destroyed, plot ownership will revert to the
city
to be
sold at their discretion. Ownership of non-city
plots
will be removed
entirely.
Housing owned by a clan will work much like normal housing, with all
permissions
being granted to the owner of the clan to build and destroy as
they see fit.
HOUSING SET ROOM OWNER <person>
- This is not available to private homes and allows the clan leader to set a
temporary room owner.
- This costs 10,000 gold and the person must be a
member
of the clan that
owns the property.
- For as long as a person remains room owner, they can place furniture as they
see fit, edit door permissions for
doors
coming into and out of their room,
and are granted a few
other
liberties.
-
Leaving
the clan will
remove
any room ownership assigned.
- The assigned room owner cannot edit their rooms in any way, that still
falls
to the clan head, should they
wish
to do so.
- Room owners cannot upgrade
beds
to
artefact
beds, they may place these and
the clan owner can
apply
the upgrade as per
HELP BEDS
.
Clan members will have access to any upgrades such as
regeneration
rooms, stables, and the like.
Housing upgrades can only be placed by the clan leader. See Stables,
Willpower
regen
, Newsroom, and similar.
As these are communal upgrades, a room owner cannot be appointed for a room with these upgrades.
CLAN
HOUSES
- Will list houses owned by your current clan.
Please do not give
OOC
clans housing, these will be removed where found.
