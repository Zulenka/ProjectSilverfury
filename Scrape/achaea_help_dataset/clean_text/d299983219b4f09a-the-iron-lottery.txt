17.4.4.2 The Iron Lottery
The Iron
Lottery
is a special
event
typically
run
with larger prizes consisting
of
credits
or
artefacts
. To participate in the Iron Lottery you must collect
iron tickets from
denizens
throughout the
world
. Some denizens will drop these
tickets upon
death
,
other
tickets could be scattered about on the ground (or
beneath it!), so be sure to
hunt
them down. Once you have collected some
iron tickets they must be taken to the lottery office in
Delos
and exchanged
for tickets in the Iron Lottery.
Commands
:
LOTTERIES
: List all lotteries you can participate in.
LOTTERY EXCHANGE IRON TICKET: Exchange iron tickets for lottery tickets.
LOTTERY INFO <lottery #> : Get information on a lottery. This will also
show
your ticket numbers.
(See also
HELP
LOTTERY)
