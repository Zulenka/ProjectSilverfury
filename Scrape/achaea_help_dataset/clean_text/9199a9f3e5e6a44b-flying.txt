6.2 FLYING
If you have the
ability
to fly, whether by virtue of a
mount
of some sort or
magic then you will be able to fly from any outdoor location. The
exits
in the
skies will be the same as the exits on the ground. From the skies, you can
GLANCE GROUND to see what's on the ground below you.
Naturally, those
who
are
following
you when you fly will not be able to follow
you into the skies. Furthermore, if you drop an item that cannot fly while in
the skies, it will soon fall to the ground, and break if it is breakable.
If you find yourself unwillingly dragged into the sky, and you do not have the
ability to fly, you will have but a fleeting amount of
time
with which to make
yourself
levitate
(through a feather
tattoo
or a
levitation
elixir
, for
instance) or you will be gravely damaged upon hitting the ground.
In order to return to the ground, you may simply LAND.
