6.15 BANKING AND BANKS
Keeping your valuable golden
sovereigns
in your hands is not always the wisest
course of action. While
leaving
the realms will not cause you to
lose
the
gold
in your hands (see
HELP
GOLD), there are
other
ways in which gold may be
lost
.
Certain
abilities
possessed by adventurers may force you to hand over gold, and
dying
will cause you to drop it as well. Keeping your gold in a backpack is
certainly safer, but for true safety, the place to keep your gold is in a
bank
.
You may also donate
credits
to a
city
or
House
at a bank.
In order to use a bank, you must first find a branch of a bank.
Banks
can be
found in each of the major
cities
:
Ashtan
,
Hashan
,
Cyrene
,
Mhaldor
,
Targossas
,
and
Eleusis
. You can also find a bank in the southeast of the
island
of
Delos
.
Once there, you may
DEPOSIT
<however many golden sovereigns>, provided you are
holding
that many in your hands.
WITHDRAW
<amount of gold> will withdraw gold
from your account. For other options, check the
Commands
section below.
To provide an optional reason on deposits and transfers, you must be working
with an amount greater than 1000 gold sovereigns otherwise the clerk will not
make any such
log
recording (though the transfer/deposit will still be made).
Fees
----
Banks will exact a 0.
5
% (
2
% for Delos) withdrawal fee every
time
you withdraw
or transfer gold. The fee will always be at least 1 gold.
Multiple Accounts
-----------------
If you have wealth rivaling
Prospero
, you may have spread your accounts among
various banks.
BANKACCOUNTS
will
show
you a listing of your various accounts,
as well as how much gold you have in them. These are all separate accounts, and
have
nothing
to do with each other.
Personal
Banking
Commands
-------------------------
BANKACCOUNTS
Displays the current amounts you have deposited in the various banks.
BANK TRANSFER <amount> GOLD TO <person> <bank>
Stand in the bank where your gold is.
Moves
money
between your two
accounts. E.g. BANK TRANSFER 1000 GOLD TO ELENTARI HASHAN.
BANK TRANSFER <amount> GOLD <organisation> <bank>
Stand in the bank where your gold is. Moves money between the two
accounts. E.g. BANK TRANSFER 1000 GOLD CIJ ASHTAN.
DEPOSIT <amount> GOLD
Deposits the amount specified into your bank account.
DEPOSIT <amount> GOLD TO person
Deposits the amount into someone else's bank account.
DEPOSIT <amount> GOLD|CREDITS <organisation> <optional reason>
Deposits the amount of gold into the account of the
city/house/order/
clan
etc.
WITHDRAW <amount>
Withdraws the amount of gold specified from your account.
Minus the 0.5% withdrawal fee.
WITHDRAW <amount> <organisation> <position> [reason]
Withdraws the amount of gold specified from the account of the
house/order/clan etc minus the 0.5% withdrawal fee.
BANKFEES <amount>
Gives an approximate amount for the fee associated with
withdrawing the specified amount from your bank account.
NOTE: This is an approximate only.
Banks are located in all of the adventurer-
run
cities, and in Delos.
For City Coffers please
speak
to your
Treasurer
.
