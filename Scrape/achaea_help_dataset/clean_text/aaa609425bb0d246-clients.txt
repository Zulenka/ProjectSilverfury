19.3 Clients / MUD Clients / Telnet clients
A
client
is
software
that connects to
Achaea
to let you play it. It can be either browser-based, downloadable, or a
mobile
app.
The best options to play Achaea with are:
* The
Nexus
client, which is our official client. It runs in a browser and will
work on all PCs/laptops (Windows, Macs, Linux), as well as tablets. It even
runs on mobile, though not with full functionality.
You can access Nexus at http://nexus.achaea.com
HELP NEXUS
will
tell
you more.
* The Mudlet client, which is a third-
party
downloadable client for PCs.
You can find Mudlet at http://www.mudlet.org
To
connect
you'll need to tell it to connect to achaea.com, and either port
23 or 2003. (Both work.)
Mudlet allows for
custom
GUIs to be created. One of our favourites was made
by Zulah. All instructions and links for it can be found at:
http://
forums
.achaea.com/discussion/5061/zulahgui-free-easy-gui
* A mobile client for
Android
phones (see
HELP
ANDROID)
* If you're visually-impaired and using a
screenreader
, we recommend
checking out http://www.gmagames.com/vipmud.shtml
There are many
other
third-party
clients
(some free, some require purchase) that can be
run
. Some of these are listed on our
website
:
https://www.achaea.com/how-to-connect/
If you are having problems with
registering
, there are some notes in
HELP REGISTERING
related to various
MUD
clients.

Iron Realms Entertainment.
