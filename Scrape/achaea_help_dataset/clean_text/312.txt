5.19 COMMON SENSE
Common
sense
is an attribute that everyone should strive to develop, though it
is sadly lacking in some.
Here are some general guidelines for behavior in the
light
of common sense:
1. If it looks dangerous, guess what? It
might
be.
2
. If it is dangerous, it might
kill
you.
3
. If it kills you, you will
lose
xp
.
4. If you lose xp because
something
killed you that
looked
dangerous and was,
in fact, dangerous, that's just part of
roleplaying
in a virtual
world
.
5
. Don't spit into the
wind
.
