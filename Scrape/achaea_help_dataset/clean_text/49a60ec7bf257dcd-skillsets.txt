4.14.1 Skillsets
(See also:
HELP
SKILLS
)
Achaea
has skills. Each
skill
is comprised of many
abilities
. You
might
even
call it a set of abilities.
The term "skillsets" is sometimes used erroneously in place of "skills". A
skill is
something
like
Subterfuge
, or
Telepathy
, or
Devotion
. These are not
skillsets
!
A
skillSET
is a set of skills connected to a certain
class
. For example, the
skillset of the
Monk
class is Telepathy,
Kaido
, and
Tekura
.
(See also: HELP SKILLS)
