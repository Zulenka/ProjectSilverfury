19.24.4 Mark Talismans
The "Marks"
talisman
set:
-------------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Promotions
(this will always be announced via the relevant
news
when the pieces are currently available)
-
Trading
between players (pieces only)
- Whole talismans bought from the Delosian
shop
(when in stock)
- Partial completion (see below.)
The
Marks
set contains the following talismans:
-----------------------------------------------
A Warhorn of Heroes                  - TALISMAN INFO
IVORYWARHORN
- Purchasable in
Delos
for 1000
bound
credits
.
-
Challenges
another adventurer to a valorous duel.
- Simply DECLARE
CHALLENGE
<person> to broadcast your challenge to the
world
.
- The intended
target
can then ACCEPT or DECLINE your CHALLENGE.
A Champion's medallion               - TALISMAN INFO
IVORYMEDALLION
- Purchasable in Delos for 350 bound credits.
- This medallion will confer a bonus to
experience
for slaying members of
the
Quisalis
Mark
.
- This only applies to adventurer kills.
A Sangrean spyglass                  - TALISMAN INFO
IVORYSPYGLASS
- Purchasable in Delos for 200 bound credits.
- Granting enhanced sight of the Ivory Tower, this spyglass will allow
adventurer to see the
ranks
of the Quisalis Mark in the Realms.
-
MARKWHO
will reveal the list.
An Ivory pennon                      - TALISMAN INFO
IVORYPENNON
- Purchasable in Delos for 250 bound credits.
- A sign of affiliation with the Ivory Mark, this pennon will confer a
small experience bonus to the wearer when
hunting
in the the Quisalis
Den, under New
Thera
.
A spiralled silver
arrow
armband     - TALISMAN INFO
IVORYARMBAND
- Purchasable in Delos for 500 bound credits.
- As a loyal
supporter
of the Ivory Mark, this item bestows a
custom
deathsight
message
upon the owner while worn.
- For kills attributed to the wearer, adventurers will see: As a
beacon
of
the Ivory Mark, Killer has vanquished Victim.
An armed Atlantia dummy              - TALISMAN INFO
IVORYATLANTIA
- Purchasable in Delos for 50 bound credits.
- Perfect for your next performance, or just to
tell
a horrible joke, this
ventriloquist's dummy has been fashioned to
look
like Atlantia, leader
of the Ivory Mark.
- Wielders can DUMMY <dummy>
SAY
<whatever> to make the dummy
speak
.
A Champion's
Training
Dummy          - TALISMAN INFO
IVORYDUMMY
- Purchasable in Delos for 50 bound credits.
- Howl with righteous fury as your wail upon this training dummy.
- You can drop it anywhere and then
attack
it for up to one
Achaean
day
.
- All
attacks
that work on
denizens
may be used on this item.
A miniature ivory tower              - TALISMAN INFO
IVORYTOWER
- Purchasable in Delos for 500 bound credits.
- A perfect replica of the Ivory Tower, this item may be TURNed to transport
the adventurer to the
Sangre
Plains and the
halls
of the Ivory Mark.
- This item will only work on the
continent
of
Sapience
, and requires the
user to be in good general
health
.
An Atlantian bowstring               - TALISMAN INFO
IVORYBOWSTRING
- Purchasable in Delos for 1200 bound credits.
- Crafted for the famed cloudbow of Atlantia, this bowstring will ensure
that
arrows
will not
lose
accuracy, regardless of how far the arrow is
shot.
- Simply ATTACH it to your bow to reap the benefits.
- Should you
wish
to change the bow it is attached to, simply DETACH it.
Anima's Thirst                       - TALISMAN INFO
QUISALISDAGGER
- Purchasable in Delos for 2500 bound credits.
- The fabled tool of a long-dead
assassin
, this
weapon
will effortlessly
pierce to the very soul of the target, severing the safeguards against
death
.
- To unleash this
power
, simply STAB <target> WITH <dagger> and their
starburst
defence
, and any inked starburst
tattoos
, will be annihilated.
- This weapon may only be used once per Achaean day.
A notched
bone
- TALISMAN INFO
QUISALISBONE
- Purchasable in Delos for 350 bound credits.
- This macabre trophy will convey upon the owner a small experience bonus
for slaying adventurers in the Ivory Mark.
A Callibian medallion                - TALISMAN INFO
QUISALISMEDALLION
- Purchasable in Delos for 250 bound credits.
- Replicated from the one worn by the famed Quisalis recruiter, Callibius,
this token will bestow an experience bonus to all
denizen
kills inside the
Ivory Tower.
The Grandmaster's
looking
glass      - TALISMAN INFO
QUISALISLOOKINGGLASS
- Purchasable in Delos for 200 bound credits.
- Precisely calibrated, this looking glass will allow the wielder to see the
members of the Ivory Mark in the realms.
A suspicious-looking Derillin dummy  - TALISMAN INFO
QUISALISDERILLIN
- Purchasable in Delos for 50 bound credits.
- Known for his shadowy motives and enticing deals, Derillin's
character
traits
have been accurately represented on this ventriloquist's dummy.
- The owner of this puppet may use DUMMY <dummy> SAY <comment> to make him
speak.
An Assassin's training dummy         - TALISMAN INFO
QUISALISDUMMY
- Purchasable in Delos for 50 bound credits.
- A vital tool for an aspiring Assassin, this training dummy will offer the
convenience of a willing victim for all of the owner's attacks.
- Once dropped, it will return to the owner's
inventory
in one Achaean day.
- All attacks that work on denizens may be used on this item.
A miniature tombstone                - TALISMAN INFO
QUISALISTOMBSTONE
- Purchasable in Delos for 600 bound credits.
- As cold and eerie as the New Thera
graveyard
, this small token will
transport the owner to outside the Quisalis Den under New Thera.
- This item will only work on the continent of Sapience, and requires the
user to be in good general health.
A coiled black steel armband         - TALISMAN INFO
QUISALISARMBAND
- Purchasable in Delos for 500 bound credits.
- As a devout follower of the Quisalis Mark, this armband bestows a custom
deathsight message upon the owner while worn.
- For kills attributed to the wearer, adventurers will see: In accordance
with the traditions of the Quisalis Mark, Killer has assassinated Victim.
A cloak of the Blood Maiden          - TALISMAN INFO
QUISALISCLOAK
- *THIS ITEM IS NOT CURRENTLY AVAILABLE IN DELOS OR FOR PARTIAL COMPLETION*
- A perfect reproduction of the cloak of the storied Quisalis Assassin,
Isiva, this cloak functions as a
Shadowcloak
, and also drinks in the
blood of vanquished foes.
- Once it has
drunk
in enough blood (
5
deaths, player kills are worth
double) the cloak will allow the owner to ACTIVATE BLOODSHIELD, providing
a
shield
that will block the next attack against you.
- Be warned, the cloak's thirst for blood must be sated,
failing
to make
a
kill
within
3
minutes
will cause the blood reserves to deplete.
- *THIS ITEM IS NOT CURRENTLY AVAILABLE IN DELOS OR FOR PARTIAL COMPLETION*
A Death Keeper's mask                - TALISMAN INFO
QUISALISMASK
- Purchasable in Delos for 1000 bound credits.
- An armament of the Quisalis of old, this mask will provide the wearer
with the area that the victim died in via DEATHSIGHT.
Partial Completions
--------------------
If you have two or three pieces of a talisman in the death item you can
TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL <talismanname> to complete it for the remainder
of the bound
credit
value of the item as it is in the Delosian Talisman shop.
DOING THIS WILL COMPLETE THE TALISMAN AND
BIND
IT TO YOU.
Talismans completed in this manner will have an altered
tradein
that
reflects its credit worth, including the credit purchased completion.
I.E
You have: cruciblebase and cruciblebowl.
You use TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL
CRUCIBLE
and pay 500 bound credits.
This is half the cost of the crucible in the shop because you already
have half of the pieces.
You get: a completed soulfire crucible talisman.
This item has a tradein value of: 346 bound credits.
This is
2
/3rds of 520 credits (The 500 credits paid above, plus your 2
pieces valued at 10 credits each.)
