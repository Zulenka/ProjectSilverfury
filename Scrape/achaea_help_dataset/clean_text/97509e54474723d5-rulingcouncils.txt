10.2 RULING COUNCIL
The ruling council of a
city
-state is comprised of either five or seven members.
In most cases, these members are elected by the populace via the methods
described in
HELP ELECTIONS
.
HELP
<city> will
show
you
who
the members of the
ruling council are. In
cities
with a strictly theocratic government, council
members are appointed by the
divine
patron(s).
While the perceived responsibilities of a ruling council may vary in the
eyes
of each
citizen
, it can safely be said that the ruling council must take
credit
, or blame, for what happens to their city. While the
power
to actually
affect most city-related
things
lies in the hands of the
Ministers
and their
aides
, it is the ruling council who chooses the leader and appoints Ministers.
If Ministers or the leader are doing a poor job, that would be the concern of
the ruling council.
Members of the Ruling Council earn 200
renown
per
hour
while logged in, up to
400 renown per
day
or until they hit cap (see HELP RENOWN).
As a
member
of a ruling council, you may use the
following
commands
:
CITY
FINANCE
SUMMARY [<year>]
- Summary of all city
ministries
gold
movement
.
CITY FINANCE REPORT <ministry> [<year>]
- Line by line expenditures and income for a particular ministry.
CITY FINANCE REPORT
CREDITS
[<year>]
- Line by line expenditures and income for credit movement.
CITY REPORTS <ministry> <year>
- The detailed report for any ministry for the given year.
CITY
CLAN
LIST
- View the
clans
that are owned by your city.
CITY
ENEMY
/UNENEMY <person> <reason>
- Brand someone an official enemy of your city.
CITY
ALLY
/UNALLY <person>
- Add/
Remove
someone as a city ally.
CITIZEN <person>
- Make someone a citizen of your city.
CITY ECONSTATUS
- See some basic economic info on your city.
CITYDISFAVOUR <person>
- Grant your city
disfavour
to a person.
CITYFAVOUR <person>
- Grant your city
favour
to a person.
CITY MUTE/UNMUTE
- The
syntax
is: CITY MUTE <infidel> <TEMP|INDEFINITE> <reason>. TEMP will
mute for an hour then wear off. INDEFINITE will mute until someone does
CITY UNMUTE on the muted person. The reason will be recorded in the city
logs
. This is on a
trial
basis only. Cities abusing this power to mute
left and right will find they no longer have access to it.
FUNDS <ministry>
- See the funds
level
for a selected ministry. Must be in
the Council Room for this. Will not work on the Stewardship.
READREVLOG/REVLOG
- Same as READLOG in
HELP CITYCOMMANDS
, but
shows
you logs for
ingoing and outgoing revenue.
READMILLOG/MILLOG
- Same as READLOG in
HELP CITYCOMMANDS
, but shows you logs for
ingoing and outgoing military revenue.
SUPPORT
<person>
-
Throw
your support behind a member of the ruling council
for leadership of the city.
UNCITIZEN <person>
- Kick someone out of your city.
WARSTATUS
- See the military
status
for your city.
Open
Referendums
(see HELP REFERENDUMS)
Please also see
HELP IMPROVEMENTS
for more commands and responsibilities
relating to City
Improvements
.
