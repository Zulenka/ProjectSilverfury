19.24.8 Monks Talismans
The "Monks"
talisman
set:
-------------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Promotions
(this will always be announced via the relevant
news
when the
pieces are currently available)
-
Trading
between players (pieces only)
- Whole talismans bought from the Delosian
shop
(when in stock)
- Partial completion (see below.)
The
Monks
set contains the following talismans:
-----------------------------------------------
An odyssean tongue         - TALISMAN INFO ODYSSEANTONGUE
- Purchaseable in
Delos
for 200
bound
credits
.
- You may WHISPERTONGUE <phrase>, and then you will automatically utter that
phrase at the moment of your demise.
A black headband         - TALISMAN INFO MONKHEADBAND
- Purchasable in Delos for 300 bound credits.
- While wearing this headband none of your
mana
draining
defences
shall
impose their drains upon you, so as long as you are MEDITATING.
A taloned
ring
- TALISMAN INFO TALONEDRING
- Purchasable in Delos for 500 bound credits.
- While wearing this ring you will grip tightly to all items that you are
wielding
, much like the gripping defence's functionality.
A cranes pennon         - TALISMAN INFO CRANEPENNON
- Purchasable in Delos for 1000 bound credits.
- UNFURL this pennon and the next two people to BOW to the pennon will enter
into a mutually acknowledged duel.
- Neither of those people will be able to stray more than one room from the
pennon until either five
minutes
has passed or one of the combatants is
slain.
- Note: you cannot force someone to bow, before anyone gets
ideas
!
An olive branch         - TALISMAN INFO
OLIVEBRANCH
- Purchasable in Delos for 2000 bound credits.
- RAISE this branch and you will take no damage from external sources.
- This can only be done every 10 minutes and lasts ten
seconds
.
- Note that this does not protect you against self-inflicted damage such as
HOLOCAUST or FULMINATION. It also respects all forms of hinder such as
prone/paralysis/
entanglement
.
- Any offensive action will cause the effect to dissipate.
A rallying
banner
- TALISMAN INFO MONKBANNER
- Purchasable in Delos for 2000 bound credits.
-
PLANT
BANNER and all of your mutual
allies
upon the same
continent
and
plane
,
who
are also on
balance
and not on a
monolith
will be summoned
to the standard. Once planted you will need to wait for the standard to
reset to you before you may PLANT it again.
A dark
ash
wood
staff  - TALISMAN INFO ASHSTAFF
- Purchasable in Delos for 2000 bound credits.
- This staff functions as a
level
3
Shikudo
staff, but also permits you to
SMITE THE GROUND WITH STAFF to cause a variety of effects, from the minor
to the not so minor.
- Note that this is entirely uncontrollable so you should not expect to rely
on any particular effect.
Partial Completions
--------------------
If you have two or three pieces of a talisman in the
death
item you can
TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL <talismanname> to complete it for the remainder
of the bound
credit
value of the item as it is in the Delosian Talisman shop.
DOING THIS WILL COMPLETE THE TALISMAN AND
BIND
IT TO YOU.
Talismans completed in this manner will have an altered
tradein
that
reflects its credit worth, including the credit purchased completion.
I.E
You have: cruciblebase and cruciblebowl.
You use TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL
CRUCIBLE
and pay 500 bound credits.
This is half the cost of the crucible in the shop because you already
have half of the pieces.
You get: a completed soulfire crucible talisman.
This item has a tradein value of: 346 bound credits.
This is
2
/3rds of 520 credits (The 500 credits paid above, plus your 2
pieces valued at 10 credits each.)
