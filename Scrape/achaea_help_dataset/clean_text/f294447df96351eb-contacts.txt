19.19 Email Contact Information
Most of the
gods
and goddesses in
Achaea
have individual
email
addresses under the domain achaea.com (such as
tecton
@achaea.com).
It is preferable to contact in
character
roles in-game for in character
things
.
But sometimes you have an
OOC
question
, first recourse is to file an
ISSUE
!
ISSUE
ME
<your question with as much information as you can give.>

emails
