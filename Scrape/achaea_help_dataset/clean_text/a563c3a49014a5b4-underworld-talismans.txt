19.24.20 Underworld Talismans
The "Underworld"
talisman
set:
-------------------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Promotions
. This will always be announced via the relevant
news
when the pieces are currently available.
- Defeating monsters in the
Underworld
itself
-
Trading
between players (pieces only)
The Underworld set contains the following talismans:
-----------------------------------------------------
A withered crypt worm				- TALISMAN INFO CRYPTWORM
- Priced at 50
credits
- This wriggling legendary
mount
bears the effects of the Collar of
Lupus
and the Badge of Returning.
The culling blade				- TALISMAN INFO CULLINGBLADE
- Priced at 1500 credits
- While
wielding
this blade you may utilise a special
battlerage
attack
with the REAP
command
.
- This will strike your specified
target
and a second creature in your location of the same type (REAP
UNDEAD
for instance).
- On the treacherous
planes
of
Annwyn
and the Underworld, reap will hit a third target as well.
- This consumes the same amount of
rage
and rage
balance
as a standard, high- damage rage attack, and also does the same damage.
A funeral shroud				- TALISMAN INFO FUNERALSHROUD
- Priced at 2000 credits
- WRAP yourself in this shroud and you will vanish from sight in a location.
- This specifically hides you on
LOOK
, QUICKLOOK, GLANCE, OBSERVE, VEILGLANCE, and similar
abilities
.
- It does not hide you from
sense
abilities.
- Performing any action while concealed by this shroud will cause it to immediately unwrap.
- Wrapping yourself carries a three
minute
cooldown if not in the Underworld.
A talisman of custody				- TALISMAN INFO CUSTODYTALISMAN
- Priced at 400 credits
- BREAK a
piece
off this talisman and hand it to an adventurer with less
experience
than yourself.
- If that adventurer dies while you are both in the realms, while you are wearing the talisman of custody, you will take the experience loss in their place.
- Each
time
you use the talisman in an
RL
day
, the experience cost will increase:
* At first you will only
lose
the same experience they would.
* After ten deaths, you will lose 10% of the experience they would and 90% of the experience loss you would have suffered had you died yourself in their place.
* The talisman will break on the tenth
death
, but it recharges all of its uses once a
month
.
- SHAKE the talisman to make it whole again if you change your mind.
- TOUCH the talisman to see
who
currently holds the piece.
A sliver of fog-wreathed obsidian	- TALISMAN INFO SOULSLIVER
- Priced at 800 credits
- Each time you
die
this sliver will absorb part of your fading life essence.
- You may BREAK this sliver to restore your
health
(the more charge, the bigger the health gain).
- This healing can push you over your max health by up to 25% of maximum! (This does also work if you have your max health increased by the effect of the death cape.)
A
totem
of unlife			- TALISMAN INFO UNLIFETOTEM
- Priced at 500 credits
- TOUCH this totem while standing next to an entrance or
exit
to the Underworld and a list of all currently in the Underworld will be carved upon it.
- Be warned that everyone in the Underworld will see a
message
whenever the totem is used.
- There is a 30 second cooldown on its use, shared by everyone, but any time it is used, all
totems
held by adventurers in eligible rooms will also bear the new carving.
A skein of
sanity
- TALISMAN INFO SANITYSKEIN
- Priced at 220 credits
-
WIND
this skein to add balance time to it while in the Underworld.
- PULL this skein to consume the time you've wound onto it, allowing you to act with normal balance while under the corrupting effects of the Underworld.
A splinter of shattered psyche		- TALISMAN INFO PSYCHESPLINTER
- Priced at 180 credits
- This splinter will double your rate of corruption while within the Underworld.
