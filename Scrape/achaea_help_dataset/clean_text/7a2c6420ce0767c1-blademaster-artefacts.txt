19.10.29 Blademaster Artefacts
(see also:
HELP ARTEFACTS
, HELP ARTEFACT TRANSFERS,
HELP
ARTEFACT
TRADEINS
)
Artefacts
are special items that can be purchased only with
Bound
Credits
. Unless otherwise stated, artefacts can be purchased in Merentesh's series of
shops
in
Delos
. They will reset into your
inventory
frequently, which means they cannot be loaned out, or stolen(!), for very long. Some only work for the owner. They never
decay
. They cannot be changed to reset to anyone else's inventory, so do not
buy
artefacts of any kind if you intend to
sell
them.
Note About Stacking
-------------------
Artefact effects (for similar artefacts) don't combine or stack. If you have,
say
, a Collar of
Ceylon
and a Collar of
Agatheis
, then only one will boost damage of a magical
nature
. If you have a Logosian
Ring
and a Mayan Ring, then only one will increase the value of sipping a
health
elixir
. In general, the best item rules, but there may be exceptions. So don't count on having two artefacts and somehow stacking their effects.
A Blademaster's band of
fire
:                                 350 Credits
- This band may be ATTACHed to a Blademaster's sword, increasing the
speed
and damage of
attacks
by
5
%.
- This also makes the
weapon
reset to its owner.
- This may be DETACHed.
A Blademaster's band of lightning:                            800 Credits
- This band may be ATTACHedto a Blademaster's sword, increasing the speed and damage of attacks by 10%.
- This also makes the weapon reset to its owner.
- This may be DETACHed.
A Blademaster's band of ice:                                  1600 Credits
- This band may be ATTACHED to a Blademaster's sword, increasing the speed and damage of attacks by 15%.
- This also makes the weapon reset to its owner.
- This may be DETACHed.
An irreverent's
guard
chain:                                  150 Credits
- Attach this to your
Blademaster
sword and you can give it to another person.
- ATTACH <chain> TO <sword>.
- This will allow the sword to then be banded as usual if desired.
- This does not add resetting or non-decay.
- This cannot be used to
steal
another's sword, this can only be used by the sword's current owner.
- This cannot be used on an already banded sword.
Bracers of the Phoenix:                                       250 Credits
Artefact
power
:
shin
_drain
- These bracers will slow the rate at which a Blademaster's shin energy
decays
.
An ethereal lightning eagle feather cuff:                     500 Credits
Artefact power: enhanced_shin
- Increase the chance of ANNIHILATE
throwing
someone from the room. Increase the length of HAMSTRING duration by 5
seconds
(when applied via THUNDERSTORM).
