5.8 BLEEDING
When you are hit by a cutting
weapon
in
Achaea
, you will usually begin to
bleed. After you begin
bleeding
, you'll continue to bleed
health
points every
so often. Your body will gradually clot the blood, but this will take
time
.
There are
other
methods available to quicken the clotting of blood. There are
certain
class
-related
abilities
, as well as an
ability
in
survival
that lets
you do it. There is also the
moss
tattoo
, which, when inscribed on your body,
will automatically clot your blood for you.
