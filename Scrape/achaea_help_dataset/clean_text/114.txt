13.4.5 Finding Weapons or Armour to Buy
(See also:
HELP ARMOUR
,
HELP WEAPONS
,
HELP
BUYING
)
Many, especially younger Achaeans, focus a lot of energy on locating
weapons
and
armour
. Sometimes this is not a good
idea
at all, so the first
thing
to do
is ask: DO I NEED ARMOUR, or DO I NEED A
WEAPON
?
Do You Need a Weapon?
---------------------
Probably not. If you're a
Bard
, you'll need a rapier soon. If you're one of the
three knightly
classes
(
Infernal
,
Paladin
,
Runewarden
), you'll want a sword,
probably a shortsword. That's it!
If you're an
Apostate
,
Alchemist
,
Blademaster
,
Druid
,
Sentinel
,
Jester
, Mage,
Monk
,
Occultist
,
Priest
,
Serpent
,
Shaman
, or
Sylvan
, you either don't use
weapons, don't use weapons yet (younger Serpent), or you have a special way to
get your own (
Priests
summon their own maces, and
Jesters
wish
for their own
blackjacks).
Do You Need Armour?
-------------------
Armour can be pricey, for a younger
Achaean
. Buying armour can definitely wait,
if you can even use it at all.
Magi
can't use armour or
shields
at all.
Monks
can't use shields. Everybody
else can use both to some degree. See
HELP ARMOUR
for details.
How Much Do Weapons Cost?
-------------------------
You should be a
member
of a
House
, in which case you will likely get a free or
very cheap weapon to start.
Failing
in that, you'll pay from perhaps 500 to 1500
gold
for a shortsword, and
a somewhat broader range (
say
, 500-4000 or so) for a rapier.
Where Are Weapons and Armour Sold?
----------------------------------
Both are sold primarily in adventurer-
run
shops
scattered throughout the
cities
of
Sapience
. Start with your own
city
whenever possible, since there are often
discounts available.
If you don't have a city, then start
walking
! There are dozens of shops in each
city, stocked by their owners as they are able. Stock changes from
minute
to
minute, and there is no guarantee that a price is fair either, so bring a
friend
(a friend with the WeaponProbe
ability
if at all possible).
(See also:
HELP ARMOUR
,
HELP WEAPONS
, HELP BUYING)
