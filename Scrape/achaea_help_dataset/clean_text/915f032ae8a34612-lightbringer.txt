11.7.3 Aurora, the Lightbringer
"
Light
is revelation and redemption. It illuminates the true
nature
of life. As it is and as it should be."
Called by
Aeon
from the depths of ages past, Light incarnate hearkened to His
voice
and walked the passage of
Time
. Emerging into a land wracked by the
war
of the Worldreaver, the
world
bore little resemblance to that which She had left behind.
Choosing
to defy Her Fated path,
Aurora
reigns as once more
Sovereign
to Her sacred Realm,
forging
new destinies for Herself and those with the
strength
to
walk
in Her illumination.
A Goddess whose birth remains forever rooted in Logosian duality, the
mantle
of Suffering born with Her Twin,
Apollyon
, is now Hers and Hers alone to bear in solemn
devotion
to Creation. It is She
who
claims its pain, that all who dwell within
might
be redeemed. It is She who permits naught in Her way as a
guardian
of life and Light, scouring Her foes from existence with the shining brilliance of Her sword. And it is She who goes to battle as a radiant warrior, Her stark form the resplendent
vanguard
to which the forces of Good rally in hallowed duty.
For those who refuse to unite under the
banner
of Good, the
Lightbringer
offers only scorn. For the cowardly and apathetic, Her indignation blazes bright. Likewise, any who dare obscure Her gaze are excised from the fabric of Creation, that its healing may proceed unopposed by Dark's deceit, Evil's
dominion
, or Chaos's taint.
Born of stellar sundering, tempered by Time, and harrowed by Memory, Aurora is as She ever shall be: the guiding
beacon
for the forces of Good.
