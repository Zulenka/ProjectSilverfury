19.10.32 Giftcards
Giftcards
are a promotional item available occasionally from www.
achaea
.com/
credits
!
GIFTCARD
SEND [ANON] <giftcard type> <person> <message>:
Send a giftcard to someone (or yourself!)
If you choose to send it anonymously, your identity will be concealed.
You can send to yourself with your
name
or
me
/self.
GIFTCARD
READ
[ALL [VERBOSE]|<number>]:
Read a giftcard!
If you include no arguments, it reads the first giftcard in your
inventory
.
If you specify all, it reads all giftcards and gives you a summary of
everything
you received.
If you read ALL VERBOSE, it
shows
you what each card is giving as well as the summary (this
might
get spammy if you have a lot of giftcards!).
You can read a specific giftcard by number if you want to do that for some reason!
GIFTCARD
STATUS
:
Shows you how many giftcards you have available to send and how many you have to read!
GIFTCARD OPTIONS:
Shows you the types of giftcards available for you to send.
Not all will be available to everyone, each org has their own special
design
for instance.
Options affect the
style
of the giftcard and the delivery manner!
On
reading
a giftcard you receive:
- One of the major prizes.
- One of the consumeable prizes.
- A bonus. If you are missing one of the giftcard bonuses, you will get that with a 12
hour
duration. If you have all the bonuses, you'll get a random extension of 1-
3
hours
on an existing bonus.
- If the card was not sent to you by yourself, the sender also gets a random bonus under the same rules.
Major items:
- Card sleeves.
-
Talisman
caches.
-
Collection
caches.
- Bronze, silver,
gold
, and platinum giftboxes.
- A concoctorial strand.
Consumeables:
- Grey and clouded bottle (Consumeable beckon)
- Ledolian
surcoat
(Consumeable +
armour
/resistances)
- Amber ampoule           (Consumeable
goblin
minipet
racial
traits
)
- Carved wooden horse     (Consumeable Raido)
- Bottle of fairy dust    (consumeable prismatic barrier)
See HELP PROMO ITEMS for more information on these
Bonuses:
- 10%
xp
bonus.
- 30%
battlerage
generation bonus. This does stack with the mineral one, though there is diminishing returns (together they push you to a 140% bonus).
- A
critical
hits bonus.
