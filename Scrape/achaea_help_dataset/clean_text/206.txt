15.7 SPAMMING
Spamming
, in the context of
Achaea
, includes repetition,
advertising
,
and
flooding
:
1) REPETITION: repeatedly sending, transmitting, or
communicating
in
any way. This may be repeated yells,
shouts
,
says
, or use of
market
,
clan
,
city
, order,
house
, or any
other
private,
group
, or public
channels
of
communication
.
2
) ADVERTISING: sending any
message
that is
OOC
and unsolicited or
may reasonably be understood as advertising. (see
HELP
ADVERTISING)
3
) FLOODING: sending any overly lengthy text, whether repeated or not.
4)
SYSTEM
MESSAGING
: If you are sending so much information to a clan or
party
that you require it to be gagged by
others
, to the
point
where
anyone without your gags is getting unbearably spammed.
Spam
is still spam whether it be directed at a single person, or a
group, or broadcast generally without any specific
target
, or with
multiple
targets
. It is never justified, especially in the case where
the intended recipient or target does not respond.
If spam is used in a
harassing
manner, it can have severe consequences,
including permanent loss of your
character
, banning, and notification
of relevant authorities.
