8.17 HOUSE LOGS
In order to maintain
accountability
within a
House
,
logs
are kept for a full
week (real
time
) of all important House actions. These include housefavouring,
housedisfavouring, casting out, inducting,
choosing
a patron, putting someone
on
probation
, paroling someone, and more.
The
syntax
to see a
log
is:
READLOG <your House shortname> <line to begin at - optional> <
day
to
read
-
optional>
If you simply
wish
to read the current day's log, from the beginning, then
all you have to do is READLOG <your House shortname>. If you wish to read
other
days' logs, then you must include all four fields. For instance:
READLOG
ASHURA
1
3
would start you
reading
the House of the Ashura's log from 3
days
ago,
starting at line 1.
