19.24.11 Races Talismans
The "Races"
talisman
set:
-------------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Promotions
(this will always be announced via the relevant
news
when the pieces are currently available)
-
Trading
between players (pieces only)
- Whole talismans bought from the Delosian
shop
(when in stock)
- Partial completion (see below.)
The
Races
set contains the following talismans:
-----------------------------------------------
A dwarven
lodestone
- TALISMAN INFO LODESTONE
- Purchasable in
Delos
for 300
bound
credits
.
- DROPping this lodestone in a mine you own will cause all miners in that mine to have increased
experience
gain.
- This does not
apply
to soldiers, only to miners.
- A mine can only have one lodestone in it at a
time
and said lodestone will be returned to the owner once that mine collapses.
- Themed for dwarves.
A shamanistic
rattle
- TALISMAN INFO RATTLE
- Purchasable in Delos for 400 bound credits.
- This rattle gives one a limited amount of
control
over the
weather
.
- Simply by SHAKEing RATTLE, a random weather effect will take place.
- These range from rainstorms, to conjured snowstorms, to the very rare (and best avoided) lightning strikes.
- Themed after the atavian
race
.
A collapsible
catapult
- TALISMAN INFO CATAPULT
- Purchasable in Delos for 900 bound credits.
- These marvels of siege
weaponry
were popularly used by the legendary Mercenaries of Arn.
- SETUP CATAPULT will set this
weapon
up in a room.
- Then anyone may ENTER CATAPULT to climb into the basket, at which
point
the owner may
FIRE
CATAPULT <direction>, launching that person in the given
direction
.
- Where they come down is completely random (and is sure to be unpleasant for your wayward volunteer), but it is based on the area of the direction you fire in.
-
Think
of the possibilities!
- If you've ever struggled to infiltrate
enemy
cities
, you can now do it in
style
.
- This talisman is themed for the troll race.
A blood daubed
cloth
of whispers   - TALISMAN INFO
WHISPERCLOTH
- This item is not available in the Delosian talisman shop.
- When wearing this cloth, the next time you slay an adventurer there will be absolutely no deathsight.
- This cloth is only good for one use an
hour
.
- Themed for the rajamala race.
A demolition sphere                - TALISMAN INFO
DEMOLITIONSPHERE
- Purchasable in Delos for 800 bound credits.
- ACTIVATE SPHERE to destroy all ice, stone, and lightwalls in a location.
- These spheres have five
charges
and
regenerate
one charge per hour.
- Note: this is not per
Achaean
day
, the timer starts from when you ACTIVATE the sphere.
- Themed for the mhun race.
A
piece
of horkval
chitin
- TALISMAN INFO CHITIN
- Purchasable in Delos for 500 bound credits.
- This fragment of chitin will allow you to tap into a hivemind, granting the
ability
to siphon your
endurance
to another adventurer.
- HIVE SIPHON <amount> TO <person>.
- If the person you are siphoning to also has the talisman, there is no loss of endurance in the transfer.
- If they do not, there is a 25% loss of endurance in the transfer.
- Naturally, themed for the horkval race.
A flattened
frogskin
- TALISMAN INFO FROGSKIN
- Purchasable in Delos for 1200 bound credits.
- This somewhat odd collectable allows one to FORM BUBBLE when underwater.
- This will create a bubble around that person, granting the racial
regeneration
that grooks enjoy.
- For as long as the bubble is present, it will also act as if you are in a water body for the purposes of said regeneration.
- However, if your
health
dips below half, the bubble will burst, and you will need to return to an underwater location to re-establish it.
- Themed for the grook race.
A pernicious pair of Dala'myrr claws - TALISMAN INFO
DALAMYRRCLAWS
- This item is not available in the Delosian talisman shop.
- These deadly claws function similarly to wolverine claws in that they grant the ability to
burrow
.
- However, they have different tunnelling
messages
, and upon burrowing below and above, rubble will be created in the entry and
exit
rooms, blocking a random exit.
- Themed for the xoran race.
Bottled siren song                 - TALISMAN INFO
SIRENSONG
- Purchasable in Delos for 700 bound credits.
- OPEN BOTTLE and all in your current location will cease to
consider
you a personal enemy.
- Additionally, they will be unable to re-enemy you for a short time.
- OPENing comes with a cooldown.
- Themed for the siren race.
Crystalline
panpipes
- TALISMAN INFO PANPIPES
- Purchasable in Delos for 600 bound credits.
- These mysterious panpipes allow one to PLAY PANPIPES <dream>.
- All in your location will then see that dream when they next
sleep
, in place of the first dream they would normally see.
- In addition, hearing these panpipes conveys a sleep style effect, stripping
insomnia
if present and inducing sleep if it is not.
- Themed for the satyr race.
An arachnid's
eye
- TALISMAN INFO
ARACHNIDEYE
- Purchasable in Delos for 300 bound credits.
- The spider
mounts
of the ancient Tsol warriors were ferocious in battle.
- FEED <eye> TO <mount> and you can imbue a
mount
with a fragment of said potency.
- A mount that has been fed an eye will gain the ability to TRAMPLE <target>, a targeted single person
attack
that shall break two random limbs on a
target
and deal some damage.
- This does not require the target to be prone like standard trample, but does require you to possess the TRAMPLE ability in
Riding
to utilise.
- Note: this item is consumed on use.
- Themed for the Tsol races.
A figurine of the suffering
Maya
- TALISMAN INFO
MAYAFIGURINE
- Purchasable in Delos for 2500 bound credits.
- This figurine of the mother of humanity conveys the ability to store the damage from
world
-shattering or
plane
-razing
critical
hits, allowing you to transfer that damage over to the next target you strike (providing you do so swiftly).
- Note: this will not allow you to transfer the damage if you
attempt
to strike an uncrittable
denizen
with said transfer prepared.
- Themed for the human race.
A tincture of Soulbleed -
- Purchasable in Delos for 1500 bound credits.
- Themed for the
undead
race, DRINK from this tincture and for the next five
minutes
,
denizens
of the
Underworld
or
Azdun
will
ignore
you in much the same way as a translucent mask.
- Carries a one hour cooldown.
An armoured brace of the Kelstaad -
- Purchasable in Delos for 450 bound credits.
- Themed for the tash'la race, when you
tumble
into a room, someone in that room will be knocked prone if they are not already.
- Does not fire in rooms with retardation.
An iridescent rose
- Purchasable in Delos for 500 bound credits.
- Themed for the fayad race, you may
WISH
FOR KNOWLEDGE in a forest room and
learn
the number of living and dead players in
Annwyn
.
A jagged black rock
- Purchasable in Delos for 2000 bound credits.
- Themed for the Tsol'teth race, simply SIPHON <corpse> using a player
corpse
and gain their racial
traits
for thirty minutes or until you next
die
, whichever is sooner.
- This carries a cooldown of one hour and does not work on restricted races (those which cannot be created as a new
character
).
A double headed coin
- This item is not available in the Delosian talisman shop.
- Themed for the Aldar, this is the reward for completing the entire Races talisman set.
- FLIP this coin at a target and for the next 35
seconds
, they will consider all members of their race to be an enemy.
Partial Completions
--------------------
If you have two or three pieces of a talisman in the races set you can
TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL <talismanname> to complete it for the remainder
of the bound
credit
value of the item as it is in the Delosian Talisman shop.
DOING THIS WILL COMPLETE THE TALISMAN AND
BIND
IT TO YOU.
Talismans completed in this manner will have an altered
tradein
that
reflects its credit worth, including the credit purchased completion.
I.E
You have: cruciblebase and cruciblebowl.
You use TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL
CRUCIBLE
and pay 500 bound credits.
This is half the cost of the crucible in the shop because you already
have half of the pieces.
You get: a completed soulfire crucible talisman.
This item has a tradein value of: 346 bound credits.
This is
2
/3rds of 520 credits (The 500 credits paid above, plus your 2
pieces valued at 10 credits each.)
