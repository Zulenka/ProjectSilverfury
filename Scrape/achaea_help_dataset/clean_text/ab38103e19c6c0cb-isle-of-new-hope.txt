12.7.8 Isle of New Hope
Located in the southern ocean a short distance southwest of Shallam's ruins is
the Isle of New Hope. Much of the
island
is filled with wild animals, but along
its northern coast lies a small village founded a short
time
before the fall of
the
Seleucarian
Empire. Originally sponsored by the Church as a place for
citizens
of
Shallam
, weary of endless conflict, to lead a quieter life, they
are led by a small sisterhood of priestesses, whose monastery is the literal
and spiritual centre of the community.
