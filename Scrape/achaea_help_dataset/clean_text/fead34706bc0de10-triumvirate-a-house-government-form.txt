8.3.2 Triumvirate - a House Government Form
(See also: HELP GOVERNMENT FORMS,

In a
Triumvirate
, three leaders (
triumvirs
)
hold
office until contested and
defeated in an
election
, with limits that may vary from
House
to House:
1) the triumvirs may be restricted to a single consecutive term (or not), and
2
) the triumvirs may serve a term of fixed length (or indefinite).
Limits on Return to Office
--------------------------
Once a triumvirate leaves office, he may not contest for, nor hold the
position again until at least six
other
people, not himself, hold it.
Special Powers of the
Ascendant
-------------------------------
One
triumvir
, known generically as the Ascendant, has enhanced powers:
- appointments to House Positions
-
selecting
the
Divine
Patron
The three triumvirs select one of their number to
wield
these additional powers
via
showing
SUPPORT
(see HELP SUPPORT). If two or three support one triumvir,
then that triumvir becomes Ascendant. If only one
supports
any given triumvir,
then that triumvir is not Ascendant. If a triumvir is Ascendant and then
doesn't have support of at least two triumvirs, then that triumvir is no longer
Ascendant.
Each house selects the
title
for this most powerful triumvir.
Council members (not Ascendant) earn 200
renown
per
hour
while logged in, up
to 400 renown per
day
or until they hit cap (see HELP RENOWN).
