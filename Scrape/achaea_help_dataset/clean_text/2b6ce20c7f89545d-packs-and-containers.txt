2.13 Packs and Containers
Packs
and some
containers
can be very useful in many ways. Some
things
should
NOT
go
into them, though (see below). For things that SHOULD go into packs
and/or containers, here's how to use them properly:
LOOK
IN
PACK
- see what's inside of it.
PROBE
PACK           - see the
description
of the pack, PLUS what's inside it.
P PACK               - same as PROBE PACK.
PUT
thing
IN PACK    -
move
something
from your
inventory
into your pack.
GET thing FROM PACK  - move something out of your pack and into inventory.
TAKE thing FROM PACK - same.
"Thing" can be any thing you can
hold
, such as a sword, an
herb
, a
book
, some
food
, whatever, including
gold
sovereigns
. You can also have a count of how
many of the "thing" you want to move, in case you have more than one. "Thing"
can include a
name
, a name and number, or just a number. See below for details.
EXAMPLES
--------
PUT BOOK IN PACK
PUT BOOK143221 IN PACK
PUT 143221 IN PACK
PUT BOOK IN PACK28910
GET
2
BOOK FROM PACK
GET 100 GOLD FROM PACK
PUT 10000 GOLD IN BOX
GET TRUFFLE FROM
LETTER
WHAT SHOULD NOT GO INTO PACKS/CONTAINERS
----------------------------------------
Some items should be kept in the
Rift
, rather than in a pack or
other
physical
container
.
Herbs
,
inks
,
commodities
, crystals, and some other items should be
kept in the Rift. See
HELP RIFT
for more details.
