3.8 Emotions
(See also HELP EMOTION LIST,
HELP EMOTICONS
,
HELP
EMOTING
)
Emotions
are a shorthand way of expressing various emotions with one or two
words. Generally, these emotions will be seen by everyone in the same location
as you. There are hundreds of
emotes
, and more being added whenever an
adventurer suggests a good one. An example of an
emote
would be SMILE, to smile
(unsurprisingly) or SALUTE.
Most emotes may be directed at someone too. So you could, for example, POKE
MISTRAL to poke Mistral, or HANDSHAKE DEMETRIOS (guess what that does).
Emotions
run
the gamut from the mundane to the sometimes silly. Some
adventurers delight in trying to discover new ones.
If
Valnurana
were to type FLUTTER
THOTH
, then
Valnurana would see: You flutter your eyelashes charmingly at Thoth.
Thoth would see:     Valnurana flutters her eyelashes charmingly at you.
Onlookers would see: Valnurana flutters her eyelashes charmingly at Thoth.
A partial list of emotions may be found in
HELP EMOTIONLIST
.
To make chosing your emotes a bit easier try SHOWEMOTE <emote>.
Information on how to send out
custom
emotes you create yourself can be found
in
HELP EMOTING
.
Tagged emotes like *grin* or *laughs mockingly* are considered an
OOC
device. Please do not use them in any method of
communication
where staying
in-
character
is required. (See
HELP CHANNELS
.)
