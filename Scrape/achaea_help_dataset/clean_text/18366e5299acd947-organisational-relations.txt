6.36 Organisational Relations
ORGANISATIONAL RELATIONS
All
cities
,
Houses
, and
Divine
Orders
may declare organisational relations towards each
other
, as well as towards
denizen
organisations. Some
clans
may also set relations with other organisations (see section on clans below).
City
- City Relations
-----------------------
Each city has a
background
hostility counter against every other city. When
members of a city perform certain aggressive actions against another city, the
counter rises. The exact number is not player-visible, a general
idea
can be
had from ORG RELATIONS HOSTILE <city>.
HOSTILE NO LONGER EQUALS
WAR
. See
HELP WAR
for details.
The
following
actions generate hostility:
-
guard
kills
-
tank
detonations
- sanctions gained
-
killing
citizens
inside a city
-
sinking
org-owned
ships
The hostility
metric
slowly
decays
every
Achaean
month
, and
decay
at an
increased rate if there has been zero hostility by the city for three
months
.
When the hostility from one city (let's call them city A) towards another city
(let's call them city B) reaches a certain
level
, city A will automatically
declare itself hostile to city B. In this instance the CITY SET RELATIONS
command
can no longer be used until the hostilities drop natively.
When city A is declared hostile towards city B, be it through use of the
CITY SET RELATIONS command or by acts of hostility, the following happens:
- citizens of B get bonus
xp
on denizen kills in city A and its allied areas.
- citizens of B can't get
quests
in A's allied areas.
- citizens of B don't
lose
infamy
when
dying
in A's allied areas.
Conversely, if city A declares an alliance with city B, then:
- citizens of A get higher xp loss if they
die
to a denizen loyal to city B.
- citizens of A can't gain sanction for
raiding
city B.
If cities A and B are mutually allied, then:
- non-enemied citizens can call for
help
in either city.
- non-enemied citizens get bonus xp for quests in the allied city and its areas.
- non-enemied citizens can defend city without XP loss during
raids
(but they
also count for tank/sanction kills.)
HOSTILE NO LONGER EQUALS WAR. See
HELP WAR
for details.
House
- House / Order - Order Relations
----------------------------------------
If two adventurer organisations mutually declare a hostile stance to each
other, they are considered at war (this is separate from the current city/city
system
).
All members of two organisations at war with each other have a
roleplay
reason
to freely
attack
each other at any
time
while this
status
remains in effect.
Adventurer-Denizen Organisational Alliances
---------------------------------------------
If an adventurer organisation is mutually allied to a denizen organisation,
members of the adventurer organisation may defend the
denizens
of the
denizen organisation. See HELP DENIZEN ALLIANCES for more information.
Adventurer-Adventurer Organisational Alliances
------------------------------------------------
If two adventurer (non city) organisations mutually declare a hostile stance
to each other, they are considered at war. All members of two organisations
at war with each other have a roleplay reason to freely attack each other.
Experience
gain and loss reduction between city soldiers, as described in
HELP CITY SOLDIERS, only applies to city soldiers. They do not
apply
for
members of other organisations at war, or for citizens of a city at war
who
are not enlisted.
Clans and Organisational Alliances
-----------------------------------
Clans, by default, do not possess the
ability
to declare organisational
relations. A
clan
must have proved in the
eyes
of the
Garden
that they can
use this ability responsibly, and that they are an entirely
IC
organisation,
before being granted this ability. If your clan is interested in gaining
this ability, seek audience with a Divine friendly to your cause, if there
is one. If such avenues have been attempted and exhausted, the clan leader
may
ISSUE
with the request. Private or secret clans may not declare
organisational relations.
Syntax
-------
<org> SET RELATIONS <other org> <HOSTILE|ALLIED|NEUTRAL>.
- For cities, Houses, Divine Orders, and clans, <org> is the type of
organisation.
Only a God may formally declare a Divine Order's stance towards
another organisation.
Setting hostile with this command has no
bearing
on natively earned
hostility status for the purposes of city vs city war.
Examples: CITY SET RELATIONS
PETRA
ALLIED
ORG RELATIONS HOSTILE
- View all organisations with declarations of hostile relations. If two
(non city) orgs are mutually hostile, they are considered to be at war.
ORG RELATIONS ALLIED
- View all organisations with declarations of allied relations. If an
adventurer organisation is mutually allied with a denizen organisation,
the adventurers may defend the denizens.
<CITY|HOUSE|ORDER|CLAN> RELATIONS
- View current relations for your personal city, House, Order, or
currently selected clan.
Example: CITY RELATIONS
<CITY|HOUSE|ORDER|CLAN> RELATIONS [
shortname
of city|shortname of House|
name
of Order's divine patron|
alias
of clan]
- View current relations for a specific city, House, Order, or clan.
Example: CITY RELATIONS
ASHTAN
ORDER RELATIONS
VASTAR
