9.37.1 Design and Crafting
(See also HELP TAILORING DESIGN, HELP JEWELLERY DESIGN, HELP COOKING DESIGN, HELP FURNISHING DESIGN, and
HELP
DESIGN
SHARING
)
Sketches
,
Patterns
,
Schematics
, Recipes, Blueprints, and
Designs
!
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Blank sketches are what you design a new item on!
Patterns, Schematics, Recipes, and Blueprints are
base
versions of the item you want to make, these are copied onto blank sketches to set the item type, and in some
tradeskills
, what
commodities
are required to make it.
A design is created when a
sketch
is completed, submitted, approved, and paid for, and is what you use to SEW/CRAFT/FURNISH/COOK your designed item.
Designs are interacted with via the
NDS
system
!
NDS -
NEWDESIGN
-----------------------
The main
command
is NDS. Enter the command for the full listing!
If you are a crafter, you have the
following
options.
NDS
BUY
<design#> [YES]                - buy an unowned design.
NDS DESTROY <design#>                  - destroy a design.
NDS DISOWN <design#> [CONFIRM]         - give up ownership of a design.
NDS EXTEND[LIFE] <design#> [YES]       - adds to life of ownership.
NDS GIVE <design#> TO <
who
|ORG org|PUBLIC> - transfer a design.
- If given to an Organisation this is a permanent transfer and the design no longer
decays
.
- If given to the Public, this is a permanent transfer and the design no longer decays.
NDS HIDEMARK <design#> YES|NO          -
show
or hide crafter's
mark
on new items for 1000
gold
.
NDS LIST [A|J|T|C|F] [
X
[#]]           - public, org, and your designs.
- A is a list of all designs of all you have access to.
- J is
Jewellery
, T is
tailoring
, C is
cooking
, F is
furnishing
.
NDS LISTMINE [A|J|T|C|F] [X [#]]       - your designs.
NDS LISTORG [A|J|T|C|F] [X [#]]        - organisation designs.
NDS LISTPUBLIC [A|J|T|C|F] [X [#]]     - public designs.
NDS MATCH APPEARANCE <match>           - your designs matching appearance.
NDS MATCH DROPPED <match>              - your designs matching dropped.
NDS MATCH EXAMINED <match>             - your designs matching examined.
NDS MATCH FIRSTEATEN <match>           - your designs matching first eaten.
NDS MATCH OWNER <
name
|org|NONE>        - designs of that owner.
NDS MATCH SMELLTEXT <match>            - your designs matching smell text.
NDS MATCH TASTETEXT <match>            - your designs matching taste text.
NDS MATCH THIRDEATEN <match>           - your designs matching third eaten.
NDS MATCH TYPE <word>                  - your designs matching a type.
NDS
PASSCODE
<design#> <passcode#>     - passcode a design (0 to clear).
- see HELP DESIGN SHARING
NDS
PRESERVE
<design#> [CONFIRM]       - irrevocably preserve a design.
NDS PRIVATE <design#> YES|NO           -
marks
design as private or not for
1000 gold.
- this will hide your designs from
others
when they do NDS MATCH
searches.
NDS P[ROBE] <design#> [passcode]       -
look
at a design in full.
NDS SHARE <design#> YES|NO             - allow share access to a design.
- see HELP DESIGN SHARING
NDS UNOWNED [V|J|T|C] <match>          - designs without owners.
Ownership in your designs will slowly
decay
.
When they
run
out of
time
, then your ownership will terminate, and others may
possibly be able to purchase them. Before your ownership expires, you can:
NEWDESIGN DESTROY <design#>    - to get rid of the design entirely.
This is permanent and irreversible.
NEWDESIGN DISOWN <design#> YES - to clear your ownership.
Then someone else
might
buy it.
NEWDESIGN EXTEND <design#>     - to extend your
period
of ownership. You
will see the fee, but not be charged, at
first.
NEWDESIGN EXTEND <design#> YES - Pay the gold and extend the period of
ownership.
The extension will always extend by a fixed amount, up to a certain maximum. If the standard extension would take the lifetime of your ownership beyond
the limit, then you will be unable to extend the life of your ownership for now.
Come back again later, when the life of your ownership will be lower, and you'll be able to do it. You can continue to do this over and over again forever. You just can't crank the total at any one time above a certain limit. The fee is a function of, and somewhat less than, the original
pattern
or
schematic
fee. The fee does not get smaller every time you extend the lifetime of your ownership. But you can continue to extend the lifetime of your ownership forever. There is no arbitrary limit to how many
times
you may do this.
NEWDESIGN UNOWNED           - All designs that are not currently owned.
NEWDESIGN BUY <design#>     - See the cost of an unowned design.
NEWDESIGN BUY <design#> YES - Purchase an unowned design.
