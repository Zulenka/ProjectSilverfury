7.30.3 Cognition Rules
You are responsible for what you put in the
prompts
for your
denizens
. While none of us can
control
the output of an
AI
, we can control what we send it. It is explicitly against our rules to try to 'crack/
hack
/break' the AI by including
anything
but in-game knowledge appropriate for a
denizen
in
Achaea
.
It is also against the rules to try to get the AI to do anything obviously inappropriate, though we care less if you are doing it in private. Doing so risks
losing
the
ability
to use cognitive denizens, or worse.
Note: Achaea's rules on discriminatory
language
and hatespeech remain in place, no matter how private.

HELP COGNITION
, HELP COGNITION PERSONALITY, HELP COGNITION FAQ, HELP AI ETHICS
