5.5 DEATH AND DYING
(See
HELP GRACE
,
HELP IMMOLATION
,
HELP
RESURRECTION
)
Death
is not permanent. It causes you to
lose
some
experience
, but not too
much.
When you
die
, you will immediately lose a small portion of your total
experience and your
mana
will begin to drain away. It takes great mental effort to remain present as a soul, and when your mana is expended you will be forced to
embrace
death, whether you are prepared for it or not.
Those at or under
level
10 are protected from death by the blessing of the
Gods
. Should they die, they'll be transported to the
Ring
of
Portals
and cured
of their ailments.
Coming Back
-----------
Return to life in one of three ways:
1) EMBRACE DEATH,
2
)
Immolation
in the
Flame
of
Yggdrasil
, and
3
) Resurrection
The simplest is
embracing
death. Just EMBRACE DEATH and you'll begin the rather
tedious process of facing Ugrach, the
Finality
and returning to life. Once you
return to life, you will find yourself in a place called "Outside the
Cave
."
Immolation requires that someone bring your dead body to the Flame of Yggdrasil,
and is close to instantaneous.
Resurrection, if possible, is also close to instantaneous.
Grace
-----
After you have died and embraced death, you will be "
walking
in the grace
of the Gods". See
HELP GRACE
for more details.
(See
HELP GRACE
,
HELP IMMOLATION
, HELP RESURRECTION)
