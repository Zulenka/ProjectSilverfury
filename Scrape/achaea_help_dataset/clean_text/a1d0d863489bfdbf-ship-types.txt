23.9 Ship Types
(See also:
HELP COMPASS
,
HELP CREW
,
HELP SAILING
, HELP SHIP COMMAND,
HELP SHIP REPAIR,
HELP SHIPS
)
Three types of
ships
are currently available to adventurers:
1. The Thalassian Seastrider can be commissioned through Captain
Maelstrom
in a hidden port in Tasur'ke.
The cost of a Thalassian seastrider is:
1,250,000
gold
sovereigns
, 200 pitch, 400 shipwood, 400 shipiron,
300 shiplines, and 300 shipcloth.
2
. The Windcutter is a smaller, nimbler
ship
able to turn more
quickly than the Seastrider. This ship has less deck space and a
smaller room for
cabin
expansions
, and a bit less
speed
running
before the
wind
.
For its disadvantages the Windcutter is available for:
625,000 gold sovereigns, 100 pitch, 200 shipwood, 200 shipiron,
150 shiplines, 150 shipcloth.
To commission one of these you should seek out Shipwright Hawkins
in the Eastern Shore.
3
. The
War
Galley is a large ship in all respects that can accommodate
far greater amounts of
weaponry
, has a higher top speed, and
catches more wind in its
sails
.
Bring him 2,500,000 gold, 400 pitch, 800 shipwood, 800 shipiron,
600 shiplines, 600 shipcloth and Scurvy Jim at the Port of
Mysia
will deliver a ship much stronger than the
other
available types.
Note:
Commodity
costs are optional and may be offset by paying extra gold at the
harbour
in
question
.
