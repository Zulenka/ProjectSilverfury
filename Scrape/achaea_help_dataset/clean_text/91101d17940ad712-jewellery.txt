9.37.5 JEWELLERY
(See also:
HELP DESIGN
, HELP JEWELLERY DESIGN, and
HELP
CRAFTING
GUIDELINES)
As most
jewellers
will be happy to
tell
you,
jewellery
is one of the noblest of
the practical crafts. With it, one may take beautiful raw materials and create
items of surpassing beauty. Warriors have fought and died over the product of a
master jeweller.
By gaining the
tradeskill
of Jewellery, you will be able to create your own unique styles of jewellery, engrave existing jewellery, and even pierce
people's bodies for them.
In order to gain Jewellery, you must purchase a permit from the
Jewellers Union Headquarters in
Delos
.
You must be over
level
10 to access the jewellery tradeskill.
BUY
JEWELLERY PERMIT
- Use this
command
at "The Jewellers Union Headquarters" in Delos.
- This will cost 200
credits
.
- If you have a bronze
trade
seal, please
READNEWS
ANNOUNCE
4247
- Once purchased, this permit will
show
up on
SCORE
LICENSES
.
- This will never expire and cannot be traded in.
GAINING THE JEWELLERY
SKILL
- Make sure you have Bought your Jeweller's Permit as per above!
- Make sure you have a tradeskill slot empty
-
TRADESKILLS
will show your tradeskill slots
- SCORE LICENSES will show how many slots you have in full.
- Visit Amarisse, Secretary of the Crafting Council.
- ASK AMARISSE GAIN JEWELLERY.
Once you are a licensed jeweller, you will have basic
ability
in the Jewellery
tradeskill.
Abilities
Gained
-----------------
At Novice:       The ability to craft jewellery from existing
schematics
.
At Adept:        The ability to create new schematics.
At Fabled:       The ability to do
piercings
.
At Mythical:     The ability to repair jewellery.
At Transcendent: The ability to engrave jewellery.
For Portrait
Locket
Inserts
============================
Jewellers can make portrait lockets from the portrait locket
schematic
, these
special items combine with portrait locket inserts painted by
artists
to
create a proper portrait locket!
(This is the new portrait locket schematic, and is not compatible with older
lockets)
PUT INSERT IN LOCKET
This will add the portrait's
description
to the locket.
This will consume the insert.
This is not reversible.
OPEN LOCKET
This will show your portrait description on
PROBE
/EXAMINE.
CLOSE LOCKET
This will hide the portrait's description on PROBE/EXAMINE.
If you are in possession of a mithril jeweller's loupe, you may CRAFT any item
in your personal
NDS
list without needing the Jewellery tradeskill.
These do not have a
tradein
value.
