3.7 Channels and using them
(See also:
HELP CHANNELHISTORY
,
HELP
EXPRESSIVENESS
)
A
channel
is way to
communicate
with a
group
-
houses
,
clans
,
cities
,
orders
,
newbies
, and even yelling and
shouting
. For all
channels
except _SOME_
clan
channels,
HELP LANGUAGERULES
apply
.
The first channel for most is
NEWBIE
. Just type NEWBIE <message> and everyone
listening to the newbie channel will see the
message
.
Other
channels are:
Name
Command
For whom?
----     -------  ---------
ARMY
- ART    - members of the army (see HELP
ARMIES
)
City
-
CT
(*) -
citizens
(see HELP
CITYCOMMANDS
)
Clan   -
CLT
- clan members (see HELP
CLANCOMMAND
)
House
-
HNT
- new members
HT
- general house
membership
HTS
- For higher members of the house
Market
- MARKET -
buying
and
selling
, for
level
11+
Newbie - NEWBIE - for asking newbie
questions
and helping newbies
Order  - OT     - members of your ORDER (HELP
DIVINE
ORDERS)
Shout
- SHOUT  -
levels
20+, worldwide, must be at a horn
Yell   - YELL   - anybody, to all in same local area
(*) you may also use the city name in the place of CT.
History
-------
Newbie and Market have history (See HELP
CHANNELHISTORY
). Cities and
Houses may or may not. Ask your City or House!
To turn off channels
--------------------
To turn almost any channels off, put OFF after its name. For example,
NEWBIEOFF, HNTOFF, etc.
To turn on channels
-------------------
To turn most channels on, put ON after its name. NEWBIEON,
CLTON
, etc.
To turn 'em all off or on at once
---------------------------------
You can type SILENCEON to turn off most channels all at once, and SILENCEOFF
to turn them back on.
Colours
-------
Type
CONFIG
COLOUR to
control
how
messages
appear to you.
Expressiveness
--------------
If you
wish
to alter how your
communication
on channels 'sounds' to people, see
HELP EXPRESSIVENESS
.
Staying IN
CHARACTER
, and Other Restrictions
--------------------------------------------
Always stay in character (
IC
) on channels - you are playing the role of a
person in a magical
world
called
Achaea
.
Talk
about real life, other
muds
, or
your favourite singer - this should all be done outside the game or, if you
must, use
TELLS
.
Please do not use tagged
emotes
on channels, such as "*cough*" or "-smile-"
or "<stumbles around blindly>". The same applies to
emoticons
such as :) or
^_^. All these are considered
OOC
devices which deteriorate the IC
atmosphere of the channels.
Be most careful with
shouts
- stay in character, watch
language
rules.
Market channel is only for buying and selling: no chatting please.
Newbie channel is for questions and answers about how to play the game: no
chatting please. Due to its
nature
, it is entirely possible that some questions
may be entirely OOC in scope. We recommend turning this channel off if you do
not wish to be exposed to OOC
communications
from new players "
learning
the
ropes."
Most other channels have their own rules, but are generally more tolerant of
chatting and general conversation.
All channels are subject to
HELP LANGUAGERULES
, except clan channels.
(See also: HELP CHANNEL HISTORY)
