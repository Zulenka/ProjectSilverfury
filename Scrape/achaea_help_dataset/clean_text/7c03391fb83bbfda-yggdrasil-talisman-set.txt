19.24.9 Yggdrasil Talisman Set
The "Yggdrasil"
talisman
set:
-------------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Promotions
(this will always be announced via the relevant
news
when the
pieces are currently available)
- From
questing
on
Yggdrasil
.
-
Trading
between players (pieces only)
- Whole talismans bought from the Delosian
shop
(when in stock)
- Partial completion (see below.)
The Yggdrasil set contains the following talismans:
-----------------------------------------------
A
dreadpilar
figurine             - TALISMAN INFO DREADPILAR
- This item is not available in the Delosian talisman shop.
- This figurine of the deadly dreadpilar can be used to battle your foes.
A
squargon
.                       - TALISMAN INFO SQUARGON
- This item is not available in the Delosian talisman shop.
- This Squargon of Yggdrasil may be ridden into battle as a trusty
mount
.
A young
valdblom
.                 - TALISMAN INFO VALDBLOM
- This item is not available in the Delosian talisman shop.
- These petals are a weaker form of the fabled kvkir
plant
that can be
found upon Yggdrasil.
- This talisman is consumed on use and has between
2
and 4 uses
(varying by completion).
- The bonuses granted last for one
achaean
day
.
- PULL <item> to make use of one of the petals.
- The bonuses do not stack with actual kvkir, as this is an inferior strain.
A wax tablet of petrified
wood
.   - TALISMAN INFO
YGGDRASILTABLET
- Purchaseable in
Delos
for 150
bound
credits
.
- Designed by the scribes of the
Ioje
compound, this tablet can be twinned
with another to share written information!
- PUSH <yourtabletID> PAIR WITH <othertabletID> to create a link.
- WRITE TABLET SHAKE TABLET and the words written on yours will appear on
the twinned tablet!
A Tash'la earthshaping tome.      - TALISMAN INFO
EARTHSHAPINGTOME
- Purchaseable in Delos for 300 bound credits.
- Possession of this tome grants access to five unique
forging
descriptors
for the regions available via Yggdrasil.
An aureate sighting
nexus
.        - TALISMAN INFO
NEXUSCUBE
- Purchaseable in Delos for 400 bound credits.(nexuscube)
- GAZE upon this
cube
and witness the heavens of the various
planes
(different for each
plane
you're on).
A glittering
fire
crystal.        - TALISMAN INFO
FIRECRYSTAL
- Purchaseable in Delos for 400 bound credits.
- This fire crystal will halve the
time
it takes when travelling between
the planes of Yggdrasil via
EMBRACE
FLAME
.
A mote of
planar
flame.           - TALISMAN INFO
PLANARMOTE
- Purchaseable in Delos for 1100 bound credits.
-
THROW
MOTE INTO FLAME at one of the flames upon Yggdrasil, and the next
- time you EMBRACE
DEATH
you will
resurrect
at that flame instead of your
- normal location.
A length of golden snake skin.    - TALISMAN INFO
GOLDENSNAKESKIN
- Purchaseable in Delos for 1300 bound credits.(goldensnakeskin)
- With this length of enchanted snakeskin, you may SHED PURSUERS in order
to have all
denizens
tracking you abandon their
task
.
A petrified branch of Yggdrasil.  - TALISMAN INFO
YGGDRASILBRANCH
- Purchaseable in Delos for 1600 bound credits.
- You may TIE <
bait
type> TO LINE then
FISH
FOR
RATS
and be whisked away
to the location that bait links up with.
- Baits are obtained through performing various
quests
upon the various
planes available via Yggdrasil.
- You may
probe
the line to see what baits you have available.
A town crier's bell.              - TALISMAN INFO
CRIERSBELL
- Purchaseable in Delos for 2000 bound credits.
-
RING
BELL AT <target> and any magical
shields
they possess will be
shattered.
- If they lack a
shield
but possess a prismatic barrier, this barrier
will be shattered instead.
- You may also TOLL BELL AT <target> to begin the process of sending your
foe to the
Halls
of Ugrach.
Partial Completions
--------------------
If you have two or three pieces of a talisman in the death item you can
TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL <talismanname> to complete it for the remainder
of the bound
credit
value of the item as it is in the Delosian Talisman shop.
DOING THIS WILL COMPLETE THE TALISMAN AND
BIND
IT TO YOU.
Talismans completed in this manner will have an altered
tradein
that
reflects its credit worth, including the credit purchased completion.
I.E
You have: cruciblebase and cruciblebowl.
You use TALISMAN COMPLETE PARTIAL
CRUCIBLE
and pay 500 bound credits.
This is half the cost of the crucible in the shop because you already
have half of the pieces.
You get: a completed soulfire crucible talisman.
This item has a tradein value of: 346 bound credits.
This is 2/3rds of 520 credits (The 500 credits paid above, plus your 2
pieces valued at 10 credits each.)
