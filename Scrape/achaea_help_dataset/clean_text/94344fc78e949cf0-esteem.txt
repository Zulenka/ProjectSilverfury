15.18 Esteem
The
Esteem
system
allows you to offer praise for
other
players'
roleplay
or to simply share a positive comment or good vibe to let someone know you're having fun without breaking
character
to do so.
The
feedback
from esteem is considered fully
OOC
and should have no
bearing
upon your character's actions.
Esteem works like so:
- ESTEEM
-
Shows
you how many more
times
you can express esteem for someone today.
- This defaults to
3
,
5
for leaders.
- EXPRESS ESTEEM FOR <person> <reason>
- EXPRESS ESTEEM FOR
GARDEN
<reason>
- ANONEXPRESS ESTEEM FOR <person> <reason>
- Express esteem for someone,
- ANONEXPRESS makes this anonymous but cannot be used for the Garden/a God.
- Note: reasons are mandatory, will be perpetually visible, and must be between 20 and 255 characters.
-
Esteeming
the Garden or a God will not consume an esteem charge.
- It is not possible to esteem someone you are
ignoring
, or
who
is ignoring you, on your own
alts
, or on someone with esteem toggled off.
ESTEEM <ON|OFF>
- Toggle the system on or off - this enables or disables both sending AND receiving, and has a 1
hour
cooldown.
ESTEEM RECEIVED
- View all the
esteems
you've received.
ESTEEM SENT
- View all the esteems you've sent.
Note: This system is intended to foster positive feedback and encouragement between players and also provide a vehicle for positive feedback towards the team without the need to file
issues
or send awkward OOC
messages
to
Gods
in order to do so. It is not a venue for negative commentary or insults, and the administration will
look
very poorly on anyone attempting to use it for these.
