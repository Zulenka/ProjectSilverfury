17.7.6 The Aurora Supernalis
(See also:
HELP
VAULT
)
The
aurora
supernalis
is a celestial phenomenon of
light
that shimmers and
brightens the skies. First observed at the beginning of the modern age, 172 years
after the fall of the
Seleucarian
Empire, it did not appear again until thirteen
cycles had passed in the Dance of the Vault. Since then it has appeared somewhat
regularly, marking the same step in the Dance. It has been declared an auspicious
time
of great fortune; as such, it is a time of celebration,
contests
, and
games
!
The aurora supernalis coincides with the
OOC
anniversary
of Achaea's founding
and is celebrated across all of the Iron Realms!
