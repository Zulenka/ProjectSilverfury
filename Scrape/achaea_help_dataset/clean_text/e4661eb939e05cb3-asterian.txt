Asterian Restoration
An eon ago, the
Seleucarian
Empire stood as the pinnacle of mortal
accomplishment, founded on the
dreams
of Nicator and the genius of
Severian, strengthened by the brilliance of leaders like Catarin
deSangre. With the fall of
Seleucar
and the sundering of
Sapience
, those
who
remained on the new
continent
of
Meropis
were forced to
fight
against unimaginable dangers. Thanks to the heroism of warriors such as
Davis Kephry and the discovery of a boy named Durande, the descendants
of those remnants live on today. However, much of the knowledge gained
by the Empire that was has been
lost
or set aside, irrelevant to the
Meropians' everlasting battle for
survival
.
As the stewards of what was once the duchy of
Aster
Malik, we have taken
it upon ourselves to create the first public sanctuary dedicated to the
free exchange of
ideas
on the continent of Meropis since the fall of the
Seleucarian Empire. With institutions like the Ingram Cairo Memorial
Library
and the Theatron Meropian, we form a foundation for the
discussion and
sharing
of ideas away from the boundaries often created
by the delineations of
citizenship
. Despite this, we do not eschew the
idea
of
service
to the various mortal and
Divine
ideals present in the
world
. We
learn
from each
other
as we view the world through the lenses
of our differing experiences and identities. The pursuit of scholarly
and philosophical excellence should not be limited by ideological
borders, and the
Asterian
Restoration
was founded with the
goal
of
offering
knowledge to all, regardless of those barriers.
Achaea is developed and published by
Iron Realms Entertainment.
