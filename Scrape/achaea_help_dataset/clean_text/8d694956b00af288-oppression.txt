9.85 Oppression
"The body may be made stronger through
combat
."
Of the
servants
of Evil, none embody this Truth more than the
Infernal
Knights
of His
city
. Versed in the ferocious art of
Oppression
and armed with befouled
weapon
and plate, the
vanguard
of Evil are not lightly crossed.
Focused upon devastating, overwhelming force, the practitioner of Oppression is a juggernaut of profane wrath. Where lesser warriors would falter at the deaths of their own, these exemplars of iniquity will continue the
fight
with ever greater fervour.
Woe
to those that stand before them, and woe to those
who
flee
; for the arm of Evil is long, and it wields a hellforged blade.
