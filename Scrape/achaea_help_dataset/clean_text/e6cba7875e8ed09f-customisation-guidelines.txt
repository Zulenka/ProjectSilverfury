19.13.7 Customisation Guidelines
(See also HELP GIFT CUSTOMISATIONS, HELP CUSTOMISATION REQUESTS,
HELP CLASS ITEM CUSTOMISATION,
HELP ARTEFACTS
, and
HELP
ARTEFACT
TRADEINS
)
Whilst every effort is made to accommodate even the most imaginative of
customisations
, there are certain limits and restrictions that cannot be
overlooked.
Base
Words
-----------
The base word is that which appears in INFO
INV
or INFO HERE. Though the general
appearance of an item/creature may be altered, its base word cannot. A 'falcon'
must remain a 'falcon', a 'rose' must remain a 'rose', a 'ribbon' must remain
a 'ribbon', and so forth.
There is only one exception to this rule, and that is if you are making the base
word more simple whilst retaining the
nature
of the item. For example, a
'shortsword' could become a 'sword' or a 'darkbow' could become a 'bow'.
Materials
----------
The
following
materials are not permitted:
- Shadows/Darkness
Though the Tsol'teth have gifted
Depthswalkers
with some understanding and
manipulation over shadow, they are not masters. The knowledge is stolen,
and so there is no 'base' understanding that would allow for the creation
of, as an example, a shadow longsword or a
shield
of darkness. Items such
as these would require intervention from
Twilight
or the Tsol'teth.
-
Clockwork
This includes any mechanism akin to clockwork, such as gears and cogs.
General Themes
---------------
The following item/creature themes are not permitted:
- Historicity
An item cannot be linked to a prominent moment in
history
. This means that
it cannot have been forged by the
skill
of the Tsol'teth, nor forged in
the dragonfire of Sycaerunax, nor broken away from the boughs of
Yggdrasil
. Similarly, items may not link back to sources of great
power
.
- Player Resemblance
A
customisation
may not bear the resemblance of either yourself or
another adventurer.
