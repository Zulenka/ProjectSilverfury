19.24.7 Seafaring Talisman
The "Seafaring"
talisman
set:
-------------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Trading
between players (pieces only)
- Completing
Voyages
!
The
Seafaring
set contains the following talismans:
---------------------------------------------------
An empty
smoke
flare                     - TALISMAN INFO
SMOKEFLARE
- Load this flare up with red
ink
and
LIGHT
FLARE to send a plume of red
smoke up on the
seas
.
A frosted pail of pitch                  - TALISMAN INFO
FROSTPAIL
- Usable once every 24
hours
, this pail will transform the
description
of
one of your ship's rooms to take on an icy aspect.
A neatly-appointed pail of pitch         - TALISMAN INFO
NEATPAIL
- Usable once every 24 hours, this pail will transform the description of
one of your ship's rooms to take on an meticulously clean aspect.
An overgrown pail of pitch               - TALISMAN INFO
SEAWEEDPAIL
- Usable once every 24 hours, this pail will transform the description of
one of your ship's rooms to take on an seaweed-covered aspect.
A scintillating pair of cascading wings  - TALISMAN INFO
ISLANDWINGS
- These wings are enchanted to take you to a special clouds room with
exits
to Shala Khulia,
Polyargos
,
Mysia
,
Ulangi
,
Minos
,
Eirenwaar
,
Tasur'ke, and shipwrecks in the Tears of
Sarapis
.
A Shipwright's union
chit
- TALISMAN INFO
SHIPCHIT
- This chit completely covers the cost of your next
shipfitting
upgrade/creation.
- The chit is consumed on use.
A polished green bottle                  - TALISMAN INFO
GLASSBOTTLE
- Place a
seamonster
trophy inside this bottle and it will be transformed
into a themed item with
descriptions
and
reactions
befitting the
seamonster the trophy came from.
A cargo crate of clovers                 - TALISMAN INFO
CLOVERBOX
- DROP this crate while on the quarterdeck of your
ship
to increase the
rate at which your
denizen
crew
gains
experience
for 24 hours.
An etched and lacquered plaque           - TALISMAN INFO
XPPLAQUE
- DROP this plaque on the quarterdeck of your ship to increase the
experience rewards from
seamonsters
for adventurers.
A figurine of Captain
Maelstrom
- TALISMAN INFO MAELSTROM
- Battle all
challengers
with the salty Captain Maelstrom battle figurine.
A stylised ocean
map
- TALISMAN INFO
MONSTERMAP
-
READing
this map will give you a heading and approximate distance to
the nearest zone in which seamonsters have been sighted. This item
will
decay
in 10
Achaean
months
after being assembled.
Note: The crossbow of coconut lumber has been removed from this set and is
no longer available.
Any remaining pieces should
tradein
for 50
bound
cr.
