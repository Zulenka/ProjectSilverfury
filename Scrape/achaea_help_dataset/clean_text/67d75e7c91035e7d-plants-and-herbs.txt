18.5 Plants and Herbs
(See HELP HEALING LIST, HELP HEALING YOURSELF,
HELP CURES
, and HELP CURE LIST.)
Plants
and
herbs
abound in
Achaea
.
Learning
their properties should be a high
priority for all adventurers.
By the proper use of herbs and plants you may
-
remove
or
heal
numerous
afflictions
.
- erect
defences
that prevent certain afflictions.
- create
salves
and
elixirs
, using the
Remedies
skill
(
HELP
REMEDIES).
Store herbs and plants in the
rift
(HELP RIFT).
Plants and herbs may be eaten or smoked, in addition to their use as
ingredients for practitioners of the skill of Remedies.
The plants listed below are those that can be harvested and can be either used
in and of themselves, or combined with
other
elements in salves and elixirs
for beneficial effects. You may encounter many other kinds of plants throughout
your journeys in Achaea, including but not limited to mandrake root, coriander,
saffron, and many more -- some with unique properties and purposes, both
beneficial and nefarious, and some without.
