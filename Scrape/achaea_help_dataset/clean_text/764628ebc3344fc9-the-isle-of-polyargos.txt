12.14.2 The Isle of Polyargos
The Isle of
Polyargos
is a fairly remote
island
, off the western coast of
Sapience
near the
Aalen
Forest. The most common manner in which to get there is to journey to the Aalen, then trek south through it until you come to a
ferryman
on the shore. He will take you to Polyargos, for a fee.
Polyargos is a massive
dormant
volcano that rises up out of the ocean. Its main inhabitants are a
colony
of lepers living there to avoid persecution from the mainland, and some extremely ill-tempered harpies. Most fearsome, however, is one of Achaea's legendary foes: the red
dragon
Yudhishthira. Yudhishthira lives somewhere in the heart of the volcano and is one of the last
things
you'll ever want to trifle with, unless you possess the courage of a lion.
