9.37.8 Design Sharing and Passcodes
(See also
HELP CRAFTING GUIDELINES
,
HELP TAILORING
,
HELP JEWELLERY
,
and
HELP
DESIGN
)
The owner is generally the only one
who
can
look
at or manipulate a design.
Two
other
forms of access can be permitted to
others
as follows:
1)
Sharing
with
passcode
-
NEWDESIGN
SHARE <design#> YES            - the design is now shareable.
NEWDESIGN PASSCODE <design#> <passcode>  - sets or changes the passcode.
When you make a design shareable, then anybody with the passcode can do:
-
NDS
PROBE
<design#> <passcode>
- SEW <design#> <passcode>
- CRAFT <design#> <passcode>
If the person enters the proper passcode, then the act (NDS PROBE, SEW,
CRAFT, etc) succeeds.
2
) Making a design public -
NEWDESIGN GIVE <design#> TO PUBLIC
- This is permanent.
Once this has been done, the design becomes available to all for
listing (NEWDESIGN LIST, NEWDESIGN LISTPUBLIC), probing, and
crafting
/sewing.
(See also
HELP CRAFTING GUIDELINES
,
HELP TAILORING
,
HELP JEWELLERY
,
and HELP DESIGN)
