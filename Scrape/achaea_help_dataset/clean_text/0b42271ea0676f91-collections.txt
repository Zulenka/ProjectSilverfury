7.29 Collections
Certain items in
Achaea
fit within the
Collections
system
. These currently
include
minipets
and battle figurines.
Battle Figurines
------------------
Should you manage to collect an entire set of figurines (all Battle figurines
will eventually fall into one of the collections), you
might
be rewarded with a
special, prize figurine!
Minipets
------------------
By
collecting
creatures and potted
plants
of a wide and myriad variety, you can
climb in the
rankings
of hoarders and zoologists and may even earn yourself an
honours
line.
Talismans
------------------
Certain special
talisman
sets are collectable (
Death
at this
time
). Completing
these may earn special items!
Collecting- How To
------------------
COLLECTIONS LIST [ALL]                  - Will list all of the available
collections.
COLLECTION
ADD <collectionname> <item>* - Add an item that you possess to a
collection.
- The item must belong to you (I.E is
loyal to you)
COLLECTION
SHOW
<collectionname>        - List the details of a specific
collection.
RANKINGS COLLECTIONS                    - See where you fall amidst the
collectors of Achaea!
* Adding an item to the collection merely associates it with that listing, it
will not affect the
status
of the creature or figurine in your possession. Once
added, an item may not be removed from a collection.
