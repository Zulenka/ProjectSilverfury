1.16 The Tasks System
(See also:
HELP
TUTORIAL
)
All new adventurers to
Achaea
are given a series of
tasks
to
assist
them in
learning
about the
world
and
guide
them through the many
commands
they have at
their disposal. It is a form of tutorial that lets you work at your own pace.
Many of the tasks must be completed before more advanced tasks are available.
Once you have escaped from
Miba
and solved the Cultists' Conspiracy, you will
unlock even more tasks. Some of these are optional; however, if you belong to a
house
or
city
, these organisations MAY require you to complete some of them. You
do not have to complete them in any order.
Most tasks will grant you some kind of reward upon completion - these rewards
may be in the form of an
experience
boost, or a small amount of
gold
, a
piece
of
equipment
, et cetera.
Syntax
:
TASK
[S] [LIST]             - List tasks.
TASK[S] <#> [INFO]         - Details on a specific task.
TASK[S] COMPLETED          - List completed tasks.
TASK[S] <GROUP>            - List all tasks in a category.
TASK[S] ALL                - List all tasks (completed and uncompleted).
