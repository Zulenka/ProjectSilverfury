19.24.16 Modern Gods Talismans
The "Modern Gods"
talisman
set:
-------------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Promotions
(this will always be announced via the relevant
news
when the
pieces are currently available)
-
Trading
between players (pieces only)
- Whole talismans bought from the Delosian
shop
(when in stock)
The Modern
Gods
set contains the following talismans:
-----------------------------------------------
A tear of Ethian                        - TALISMAN INFO
AURORA
- Priced at 200
-
THROW
this tear and all in your current room and those adjacent will be unphased (including you).
- In addition, everyone unphased in your area will then be revealed as if they had been illuminated by a star
sigil
.
A wrap of lightless
cloth
- TALISMAN INFO
BABEL
- Priced at 300
- WEAR this wrap and for the next five
minutes
all information about you will be concealed (on
honours
and
other
means to view the same information).
- This has a one
hour
personal cooldown.
A tattered swatch of ashen cloth        - TALISMAN INFO
DEUCALION
- Priced at 400
- While carrying this cloth all of your non
critical
strikes against
denizens
not upon the prime
plane
have a 33% chance to gain a
5
% damage bonus.
A Tritonic coin of salt-crusted silver  - TALISMAN INFO
NERAEOS
- Priced at 50
- This coin will be consumed in place of
gold
when attempting to bribe sailors to throw your
target
off their
ship
.
- Bribes resolve much faster when handled in this way.
- This talisman can be used twice before it is CONSUMED.
A comet-etched barb                     - TALISMAN INFO
OURANIA
- Priced at 1200
- While carrying this barb, meteor
arrows
you
fire
will strike your target even if they
flee
indoors.
- They must still be outdoors when you initially fire the meteors, and this only applies to arrows (not star
tarot
or similar).
A prismatic
eye
of rainbow glass        - TALISMAN INFO
PANDORA
- Priced at 500
- You may drop this eye in a location where it will "disappear".
- You may then GLASSEYE to
look
into that location as if you were standing there yourself.
- This eye will stay placed for 59 minutes, will be invisible once placed, and -does- bypass shimmering orbs.
A cruel stalagmite fang                 - TALISMAN INFO
SARTAN
- Priced at 800
- You may use this talisman against someone
who
considers you a mutual
ally
.
- When you IMPALE <target> WITH <this talisman>, you will take some of their
health
for your own.
A shadowy capsule                       - TALISMAN INFO
TLALAIAD
- Priced at 2000
- Turn this capsule and after a 12 second
channel
a paradoxical effect begins.
- Ten
seconds
after this effect begins, so long as you remain in the area where you initially activated the capsule, you will be returned to the location where you twisted it and your health and
mana
set back to what they were when the effect began.
- There is a five
minute
area wide cooldown on a capsule being used, and you must be alive when the effect resolves for the reset to take place.
