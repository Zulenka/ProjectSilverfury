8.1.95 Class ranks - fledgling, journeyman/woman, full member
(See also
HELP CLASS
, HELP SKILL RANKS,
HELP
EMBRACE
)
Each
member
of a
class
is at one of three stages, or
ranks
. These are
Fledgling
,
Journeyman
, and Full Member.
Fledgling
When you first
join
a class, you will be a fledgling. You'll have only
2
of
the
3
skills
in the class and will not be able to
learn
them above the
level
of Skilled. This is about the middle
rank
in skills. See
HELP SKILL
RANKS for more detail.
Journeyman
When you
reach
level 20, you'll automatically be counted a Journeyman, and
will be able to learn your class skills up to Fabled. Fabled is the tenth
skill
rank out of twelve (see HELP SKILL RANKS).
Full member
Once you reach level 50, you'll be counted as a full member of your class.
As a full member you will have access to all three class skills with no
restriction on skill rank.
If you do EMBRACE CLASS then you'll gain three benefits:
1) You'll be a full member of your class.
2) You'll gain your third class skill.
3) All limits on your
learning
will be removed, which means you
will be able to
study
to the highest skill rank: Transcendent.
This only applies while you are at or above the required level. If you reach
that
point
and then fall beneath it, you will not be able to EMBRACE CLASS
until you regain the minimum level to embrace class.
(See also
HELP CLASS
, HELP SKILL RANKS, HELP EMBRACE)
