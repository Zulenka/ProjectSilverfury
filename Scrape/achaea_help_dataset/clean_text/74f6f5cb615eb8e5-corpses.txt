4.24 Looting Corpses
(See also HELP DENIZEN RETURN,
HELP QUESTS
,
HELP OFFERINGS
,
HELP SHRINES
,
HELP
GODS
)
Looting
or searching
corpses
for
gold
or items is never needed.
Gold and
Loot
-------------
If you
kill
a
denizen
(
mobile
/
npc
), its
corpse
will automatically be placed in
your possession. When it dies, any possessions or gold fall from its corpse
onto the ground. Pick it up or not as you choose. There is
nothing
you have to
do, or can do, to "loot" a now-dead
enemy
.
How Long do Corpses Last?
-------------------------
You don't have forever to do
something
with those corpses. They'll
decay
on you
fairly quickly. You have anywhere from a quarter of an
Achaean
day
and up.
There's really no way to be certain, so if you intend to find a corpse
buyer
,
better hurry!
Selling
Corpses
---------------
Many corpses, especially those in the areas of
Minia
and
Lodi
, may be sold to
certain interested
buyers
. Finding those buyers is a sort of
quest
which can be
quite rewarding!
Most corpses have no interested buyers, in which case you may then use them as
offerings
at a
shrine
to whatever
Divinity
you
wish
.  These offerings help the
Divinity in mysterious ways unfathomable to mere mortals. It can never hurt to
have one of the Gods of
Achaea
happy with you, and it may help a great deal!
Adventurer Corpses
------------------
Adventurer corpses are not handled in the same way. If you do manage to kill
another adventurer, neither gold nor possessions are involved. The adventurer
leaves a corpse that may be offered up to the
Divine
.
