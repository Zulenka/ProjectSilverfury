19.10.10 Artefact (Permanent) Tattoos
(See
HELP CREDITS
,
HELP ARTEFACTS
,
HELP TATTOOS
,
HELP
TATTOO
BODY PARTS)
Artefact
Tattoos
, also known as Permanent Tattoos are available to the
discriminating
buyer
at any
time
for the cost of a mere one hundred
bound
credits
.
Nearly all tattoos can be made permanent, except those which are single use.
Starburst tattoos may not be made permanent.
To obtain such an astonishingly wonderful
thing
, simply make sure that you have
the tattoos on your person already, and obtain a
phial
of shimmering permanent
ink
from the artefact
shops
in
Delos
, and:
TRACE OVER <existing tattoo> ON <body part> WITH PERMANENT INK.
(See
HELP CREDITS
,
HELP ARTEFACTS
,
HELP TATTOOS
, HELP TATTOO BODY PARTS)
