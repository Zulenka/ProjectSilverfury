12.14.3 The Island of Shala-Khulia
The
island
of Shala-Khulia is in the
oceans
to the southwest of
Sapience
,
accessible by a
ferry
off the
Aalen
forest coast and further south than the
island of
Polyargos
. It is a warm, tropical island covered by a dense
rainforest. Within the rainforest is Shala-Khulia
city
, the stronghold of a
primitive civilization that has dwelt upon the island since
time
immemorial. A
separate tribe called the Khish resides within caves in the rainforest. It is
rumoured that a Necromancer laid a terrible curse upon the island long ago, a
curse which somehow links the rainforest to the forest of
Rheodad
in far
northern Sapience.
