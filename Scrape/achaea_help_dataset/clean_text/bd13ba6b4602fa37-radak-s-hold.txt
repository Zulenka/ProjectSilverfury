12.17.2 Radak's Hold
Nestled deep within the
Plane
of Frysta is Radak's
Hold
, a fortified encampment
acting as a sanctuary for the unsavoury. Home to the Murai, the Buluz, and the
Salthu, the Hold is a melting pot of scoundrels, murderers,
thieves
, and thugs,
dealing in all manner of black
market
goods and occasionally
other
"services".
None are more ruthless and decadent than Radak himself, surrounded by a trophy
assistant and a serai of beautiful women as he rules with an iron
fist
.
