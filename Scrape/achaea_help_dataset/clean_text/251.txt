4.13.1 Language Learning
(see HELP LANGUAGE TEACHERS, HELP LANGUAGE FLUENCY, and
HELP
LANGUAGE
LIST)
To
learn
new
languages
requires:
1)
gold
sovereigns
2
) language
learning
lessons
3
) a language
teacher
4)
time
Language learning related
commands
----------------------------------
LEARN languagename FROM denizenteacher
SCORE
LANGUAGE (to see how many lessons you have available)
SPEAK
(to see what languages you can speak)
SPEAK languagename (to start
speaking
in that language)
LANGUAGE (to see what language you are currently speaking)
Language learning sessions: gold costs
--------------------------------------
Learning a new language costs 1,000 gold sovereigns per
lesson
for the first
twenty six sessions that you learn, which would take you to full fluency in
one language. The gold cost for this would be 26,000.
After that, every twenty six sessions cost three
times
as much as the previous.
So your second 'set' of twenty six sessions will cost 3,000 gold sovereigns per
session. Your third, 9,000.
NOTE: The gold and language lesson cost is extracted at the VERY END of any
learning session. Don't put your
money
away! Have it available as the lesson
ends.
Language learning sessions: session costs
-----------------------------------------
Each language session costs one lesson, no matter how many languages you have
learnt.
Language learning sessions: time costs
--------------------------------------
Beyond the time during the sessions, which is not too terribly long, there is
also the wait between sessions, which may only be taken once each
Achaean
month
.
Language lessons
-----------------
Newly-arrived adventurers, at
18
years of age, gain nine language lessons.
Each Achaean year, all adventurers gain an additional three language lessons.
To
tell
how many language learning lessons you currently possess, check
SCORE LANGUAGES.
Language learning for languages you already know
------------------------------------------------
You may know a language due to your
race
, such as if you're a Xorani you will
speak Xorani! You can STILL
study
Xorani - and then you will never
lose
the
language even if you change race.
Language learning lessons:
buying
,
trading
, converting, etc.
-------------------------------------------------------------
You can't do it. Lessons may only be gained by
ageing
.
Period
. They can't be
given, traded, bought, sold, loaned, converted, traded in, gambled, or
anything
else.
(see HELP LANGUAGE TEACHERS, HELP LANGUAGE FLUENCY, and HELP LANGUAGE LIST)
