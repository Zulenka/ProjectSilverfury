12.7.10 The Eastern shore of Sapience
The Eastern shore encompasses the section of Sapience's coast from Adryn
Keep northward, past
Shastaan
, to the cliffs near
Riparium
. Mostly wild and
uninhabited, there is one small, secluded beach inhabited by an eccentric,
ageing
hermit.
