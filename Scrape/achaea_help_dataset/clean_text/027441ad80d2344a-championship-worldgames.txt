17.7.5.3 Championship Worldgames
The
Championship
Worldgames
Extravaganza consists of the
following
games
:
1. The
Foozle
- this will cover a vast array of
denizens
across multiple
planes
.
HELP FOOZLE
to refresh yourselves on the
commands
!
- In Championship mode, there are tiers of difficulty, and participants will work their way up the tiers, with the higher tiers rewarding higher points per item.
- Foozle items can not be skipped by passing in this mode, one must complete the box before submitting it. We'll have measures in place to ensure that denizens required for the contest are alive for the duration.
- The
event
will be timed (one
hour
) or will
run
until ten adventurers have completed the highest tier of items, whichever comes first. Should the
time
limit be reached, standings will be based on scores at that time.
- Travelling through the
wilderness
is enabled during
foozles
once more.
- At the end of the hour, partial points will be awarded for partially finished cards.
2
. A
PK
Egghunt
- see
HELP EGGHUNT
for all of the details. There will be a lot of
eggs
.
- This will last one hour
-
Sharing
WILL be allowed
3
. The
Bandersnatch
- see
HELP BANDERSNATCH
.
- This will last one hour
NOTE: The overall points winner of
events
1 and 2 will decide whether or not this is a PK bandersnatch!
If PK is enabled for the bandersnatch, sharing will be permitted. If it is no PK, there will be no sharing.
While specifics per game are outlined above, the following applies to ALL games:
- Major
movement
, movement bonuses, and forest
defences
will be disabled for the duration of the event.
Additional rules:
You need to be accessible to everyone else in the game mechanically - this doesn't exclude special
exits
or entrances provided they are universally accessible, but
anything
that is gated in any way - by time, by
class
, by organisation, or otherwise, is not allowed.
Shrines
, long range movement
abilities
such as empress and deliver, will not function.
Abilities which force
others
to fly or change elevation will not function.
Implanted
totems
will not function.
Scores will be totalled across the three games and Championship Points (and
credits
) awarded based on the winners across the
board
.
Championship points and credits will be awarded to the top six finishers:
1st wins 200 credits and 7 Championship points
2nd wins 175 credits and
5
Championship points!
3rd wins 150 credits and 4 Championship points!
4th wins 125 credits and 3 Championship points!
5th wins 100 credits and 2 Championship points!
6th wins 75 credits and 1 Championship points!
