15.11 LANGUAGE RULES
(See also
HELP LANGUAGE
for the various
languages
of
Achaea
.)
(See also
HELP OOC
and
HELP INSANITY
for in and out of
character
.)
(See also HELP DISCRIMINATORY LANGUAGE for an explanation of what this is.)
The rules for using
language
in Achaea are simple. Do not inflict overly
obscene language on people
who
you do not _know_ are willing to hear it, and do
not use discriminatory language (racist, homophobic, sexist, etc) AT ALL, in any circumstance.
Aside from the blanket ban on discriminatory language, we don't care what you
talk
about
- in
tells
with
friends
(not just anyone: friends only), or
-
says
(if with only friends present),
- private
clan
channels
.
but we do not want to see awful or offensive language on any public
channels (
market
,
shout
, yell,
newbie
), any
city
channels,
House
, Order, or organizationally-related clan channels.
Our language rules fit within the general context of
HELP OOC
(or
HELP ROLEPLAYING
, or
HELP
INSANITY
).
WHERE DO THESE RULES
APPLY
--------------------------
In short: LANGUAGE RULES apply everywhere except tells to friends, says in the
presence of friends only, and in
clans
(where the clan guidelines rule). They
apply to
warcries
, mottoes,
titles
, and EVERYWHERE else beyond the exceptions
noted.
The exception is discriminatory language - that's banned anywhere in Achaea, regardless of how private you
think
the conversation is.
(See also
HELP LANGUAGE
for the various languages of Achaea.)
