19.3.2 The FLASH client
Our
FLASH
client
is available at: http://www.
achaea
.com/game/play
Flash Client Basics
-------------------
The four
status
bars on the bottom left of the Flash client are your
character's four states.
Health
,
mana
,
endurance
, and
willpower
.
The top left corner has various buttons:
1.
Games
- lets you try
other
IRE
games
2
.
Map
- gives you a small mini-map that you can
move
around
3
.
Editor
- lets you write in
journals
or
letters
easier
4. Settings - lets you change client preferences or add
aliases
,
triggers
,
and
macros
5
. About - self-explanatory: about the client
6
.
Help
- opens up Achaea's help webpage
Resizing the
Window
-------------------
The Flash normally opens in a small, non-resizable window. To open the Flash
Client in a resizable window, press the blue button on the upper right when you
choose a game. Alternatively, you can navigate to
http://www.
ironrealms
.com/flash/ireclient.swf.
Selecting
Text
--------------
You are not able to just select text in the buffer. If you need to copy/paste
something
, just double click on the output window and a translucent screen will
appear that you can copy from. You can double click again below it to
remove
it.
