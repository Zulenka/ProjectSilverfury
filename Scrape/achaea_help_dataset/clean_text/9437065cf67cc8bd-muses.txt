1.11 AN INTRODUCTION TO MUDS
This brief introduction is intended for those
who
are unfamiliar with
MUDs
,
which are also called:
-
MUD
-
Multi
-User Dungeon, or Multi-User Dimension.
-
MUG
- Multi-User Game.
-
MOO
- MUD,
Object
Oriented.
- MU*    - Any of these
other
things
.
-
MMOG
- Massively
Multiplayer
Online Game.
-
MUCK
- Multi-User
Chat
Kingdom.
-
MUSE
- Multi-User Shared
Experience
.
-
MUVE
- Multi-User Virtual
Environment
.
-
MUSH
- Multi-User Shared Hallucination.
-
ORPG
- Online Role Playing Game.
-
MURPE
- Multi-User Role Playing Environment.
-
MMORPG
- Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game.
(there are a few
others
, such as MAZE, MARE,
MUX
, and probably some we've
missed, but that should be enough to get you
going
).
If you have played one of these before, it's unlikely you'll need to
read
below.
Starting: Your Adventurer
-------------------------
So you've created an adventurer in
Achaea
! Great start! You've also finished
the '
Escape
from Miba', which is our introductory
tour
.
What is your adventurer? For the purposes of the
Achaean
realms, it is you.
Your adventurer can
walk
, fly,
look
at things,
learn
things,
talk
to people,
make
friends
or
enemies
,
join
or
leave
organisations such as
cities
or Great
Houses
or
Divine
Orders
, and almost
anything
else you can imagine doing.
What do we know about your adventurer? How do we
describe
him, or her?
SCORE
,
SKILLS
, and
STAT
go
a long way to answer this,
showing
:
-
statistics
such as
strength
,
intelligence
,
constitution
,
dexterity
,
health
,
endurance
,
willpower
, and
mana
(magical
power
).
- tiredness,
hunger
.
- age.
- how many
lessons
are available to increase skills.
- (and a great deal more).
Interaction
-----------
Achaea is not a solo
adventure
. You are far from alone!
Whatever you do, you'll be doing it with hundreds and even thousands of other
adventurers (other players), plus what we call
denizens
, which are
computer-controlled beings. Talk to them, ask them things, find out about
quests
. Return a
lost
animal to its owner; end an evil monster's reign of
terror over a fearful township; re-unite lost loved ones; find rare treasures
for wealthy collectors: the possibilities are endless!
Setting
-------
Achaea is a fantasy environment that combines elements of classical Greece and
Rome, medieval Europe, ancient Persia, and other early empires.
Growth: MUDS are About Increasing and
Learning
-----------------------------------------------
Your adventurer will grow and gain new
abilities
as your knowledge and
experience in the land increases.
Study
in various skills to increase what you
can do and how well you can do it.
Location
--------
You are standing in what is called a "room" or "location". You will always be
in a room/location in Achaea, one of thousands (actually, there are well over
twenty thousand locations, plus the
wilderness
).
A location could be field, a section of
desert
, a part of a
city
street, or
just a room in a
house
. Locations are usually connected to each other by
directions
(e.g. north, northeast, up, down, in and out). Sometimes, special
sorts of things
connect
remote locations, such as
portals
.
To "see" your current location, use LOOK, or just L. This will
show
you several
things:
- a
name
(a phrase like "At the centre of Dockhand Square"),
- a detailed
description
of the room,
- what is there: a
thing
(sword, box, etc.), an animal, people, etc.
Too Much Detail!
----------------
Sometimes you may not want to read the full description of your location, but
still want to see
everything
else in it. Just do QL (quick look).
Moving
------
You can
move
around easily enough by simply giving the
direction
you want to
go. For instance, NORTHEAST or NE to go northeast. As you rise in power, you
may be able to move around in other ways, including
flying
, burrowing,
riding
mounts
, or creating portals to faraway places, travelling in an instant.
Confusion
---------
MUDs can be confusing at first. There's a lot going on!
People (both adventurers and denizens) are
walking
around, coughing, burping,
whatever. You may hear yelling or
shouting
and tons of just plain
talking
. You
will hear noises. Information is whirring past, and it's not clear what is
important, and what can be safely ignored.
That's ok. We can
help
. Many others in Achaea can help you if you'll just ask.
Try doing this:
NEWBIE
I'm not sure what to do now. Could someone advise
me
privately?
Many are willing to take
time
out to advise the newest members of Achaea.
Who's Out There?
----------------
Do WHO (to see what adventurers are in the realms). Pick someone:
TELL
jacko I'm new here. Could you help me figure a few things out?
HELP
----
Or you can go straight to HELP. We have an extensive help
system
with thousands
of entries. HELP alone will give you plenty of
ideas
. HELP 1 is all about
getting going, and HELP
2
has basic
commands
.
Fun
---
This can be a heck of a lot of fun. Try out interesting things. Greet everybody
you meet. Listen and learn. You may want to become a great fighter, or a mighty
mage, or an experienced diplomat. Perhaps political leadership is more to your
liking. Like real life, the options are close to endless. It's up to you to
decide and to have fun.
We
wish
you all the best.
