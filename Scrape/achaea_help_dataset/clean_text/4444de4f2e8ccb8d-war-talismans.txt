19.24.21 War Talismans
The "War"
talisman
set
----------------------
Talismans
and
pieces
from this set can be obtained in the
following
ways:
-
Promotions
. This will always be announced via the relevant
news
when the pieces are currently available.
-
Trading
between players (pieces only)
NOTE: All items from the "War" set are valued at 80
credits
. Individual pieces
trade
in for 10
bound
credits.
The
War
set contains the following talismans:
---------------------------------------------
A portly pigeon		 - TALISMAN INFO PIGEON
- You may FARSCOUT <target> to send your pigeon off to find that person if they are on the same
plane
as you, revealing everyone visible in their local area.
- This has a three
minute
cooldown.
Some rotting remains - TALISMAN INFO ROTTINGREMAINS
- Scatter these remains upon the ground and all people in the current location will be periodically afflicted with either dementia or nausea.
- You can SCRUB REMAINS to immediately
remove
them, and they will tick around 15
times
before rotting away.
- Scrubbing or rotting away puts them on cooldown for five
minutes
, at which
time
they will return to the owner.
A gilded bit 		 - TALISMAN INFO GILDEDBIT
- Attach this bit to a
mount
and it will function as both a collar of
lupus
and convey a new
ability
:
- ORDER MOUNT
GO
RAID <city>.
- This
command
will take you to a
city
that your city is at war with and drop you in a random room without
guards
.
- People do not follow you through this action, and it requires you be at maximum
hp
and close to full
mana
to initiate.
- Note that this functionality requires you be a
member
of a city for it to function, as rogues can not currently be at official war.
An observant
sigil
- TALISMAN INFO OBSERVANTSIGIL
- Possessing this sigil will inform you of the location from which someone is stationing guards in your local area.
A dense cloud of war  - TALISMAN INFO WARCLOUD
-  Loose this cloud upon a location and it shall function as temporary fog.
- This is similar to that caused by
weather
.
- Lasts three minutes.
A collapsible hoop	  - TALISMAN INFO PORTALHOOP
- This hoop will allow you to open a gateway to another player.
- This gateway is two-way, and lasts one minute.
- Opening a gateway takes six
seconds
, and is stopped by a
monolith
sigil.
- To do so, simply DEPLOY HOOP <person>.
- Upon use, the hoop will disappear, reforming after twenty minutes.
