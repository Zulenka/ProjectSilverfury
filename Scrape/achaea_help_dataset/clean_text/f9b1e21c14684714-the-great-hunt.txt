17.2.12 The Great Hunt
The Great
Hunt
is a periodic worldwide
event
that brings the popular pasttime
of
hunting
to a head with an all-out slaughter! This competition lasts for
approximately one
Achaean
month
.
For every
denizen
or creature you
kill
over the span of the Hunt, you receive
points. The higher the
level
, the more points you get!
Rats
,
butterflies
,
class
loyals,
mounts
,
other
adventurers, and creatures far below your own level are
excluded from your
score
.
Once a Great Hunt has begun, use the
GREATHUNT
command
to keep abreast of your
score and that of
others
.
Credit
prizes vary and will be announced in-game,
with all other pertinent details, via the
Announce
newsboard.
In
events
where multiple tiers are available, the
rankings
and final results
are based upon your level at that
time
, regardless of your level when the Hunt
started. Skirting the intention of these tiers by repeatedly
dying
to
artificially stay in a bracket or purposefully
losing
experience
to
move
down a
bracket will result in disqualification.
Often appearing during the Great Hunt are unique caves - these can be identified by piles of bones outside them, tempting adventurers to
explore
. Within, many dangerous and unique creatures lurk,
offering
greater rewards - but also bigger
challenges
.
In addition, there are often 'swarm' type events that coincide with the Great Hunt - typically these will involve the Elemental
Planes
, the Gnolls of
Manara
, and the
Underworld
sending more of their loyal forces to battle, and thus providing a hugely increased number of
denizens
to kill.
