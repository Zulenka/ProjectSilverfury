8.1.12 THE PRIEST CLASS
(See also:
HELP SPIRITUALITY
,
HELP DEVOTION
,
HELP ZEAL
,
HELP
8, HELP
CLASS
)
Priests
are one of the most ancient
classes
in
Achaea
, and unlike many
others
,
history
has preserved their founding. Millennia ago, mourning her
death
and
celebrating her long life, Imithia performed the Rite of Passing for Pasiphae,
a daughter of Sinope and Callisto, and in doing so founded the Church of Achaea,
which also birthed two guilds, the
Templars
and Priests.
Today, Priests find their true home in
Targossas
, the Dawnspear, empowered by
service
to Lord
Deucalion
and Lady
Aurora
. A
member
of the
Priest
class is
someone greatly concerned with the righteous advancement of Creation and ever
seeks to
shine
a
light
on truth, no matter the depth of shadow that would keep
it concealed.
In battle, a Priest wields a great mace she summons to inflict damage, break
limbs, and more. But the real
power
of the Priest is the close relationship
with her
guardian
angel. Through this perpetual companion practitioners of
Spirituality
gain great power. Her angel can burn her
enemies
, trace their
movements, or even tear out their very souls.
If this wasn't enough to instill respect for the class, the Priest also wields
Zeal
, the scriptures of Good recited and empowered by the song of the Phoenix,
a powerful set of blessings and
boons
allowing the priest to stride forth on
the battlefield, feared in her own right.
The
skills
a Priest gets are Spirituality,
Devotion
, and Zeal. A
fledgling
will
gain Spirituality and Devotion, gaining Zeal upon
embracing
class.
** BE WARNED: if a Priest strays from the path of the Bloodsworn
Gods
, they
will find themselves excommunicated and
lose
the
ability
to use Devotion.
(See also:
HELP SPIRITUALITY
,
HELP DEVOTION
,
HELP ZEAL
, HELP 8, HELP CLASS)
