23.1 Ships
(See also:
HELP COMPASS
,
HELP CREW
,
HELP SAILING
, HELP SHIP COMMAND,
HELP SHIP REPAIR, HELP SHIP TYPES,
HELP
NEW TO
SHIPS
)
With a
ship
, you can navigate the vast
oceans
surrounding the
continent
of
Sapience
, giving
commands
to your
denizen
crew
which will result in your ship
being sailed or rowed through the
wilderness
map
interface (see HELP
WILDERNESS).
You can discover and
explore
islands
which can only be reached by
ships.
Go
deep-sea
fishing
, dive for treasure, battle with
other
ships, and
more!
Learning
the
skill
of
Seafaring
(see HELP SEAFARING) further expands your
options.
Harbours
may be found all around Sapience and on many of the surrounding
islands. To get started, try:
HARBOUR
SHIPS to see what ships are currently docked.
BOARD
SHIP <ship
name
|ship #> to
ascend
the gangplank!
Type HARBOUR at any harbour to see available commands. Man-made harbours will
have more available commands than natural harbours, such as
hiring
denizen crew
and purchasing repairs.
To purchase a new ship, you will need to acquire ship
commodities
(see HELP
SHIPFITTING
), then visit a shipyard and COMMISSION <vessel>.
See HELP SHIP TYPES for the different kinds of ships you may commission and
the associated costs.
Ship ownership is an expensive endeavour; you may be able to purchase a ship
from another adventurer, for a reduced cost! Even those
who
do not own a ship
may still
sail
the
seas
as captain or crew in another's fleet.
