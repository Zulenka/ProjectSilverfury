15.10 AFC and AFK
(See also
HELP TIMEOUT
and
HELP
TRIGGERS
)
There is no strict rule when it comes to players being
AFK
. AFK stands for Away
From Keyboard.
This does not preclude the strict rules outlined in
HELP TRIGGERS
.
It is generally considered in your best interest to be active at your keyboard
while you are playing in
Achaea
, as you
leave
yourself open to
theft
,
death
,
starvation, and various
other
nasty side-effects.
Should you
die
, get robbed, and/or
lose
experience
due to your triggers keeping
you online while AFK, you will not be compensated for these losses.
(See also
HELP TIMEOUT
and HELP TRIGGERS)
