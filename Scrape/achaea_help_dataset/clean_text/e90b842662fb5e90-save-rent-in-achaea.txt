4.23 Save / Rent in Achaea
(see
HELP RAMPAGE
to see how to
leave
a
rampage
event
)
(See also
HELP REGISTER
,
HELP INACTIVITY
, and
HELP
QUIT
)
How to leave
Achaea
:
GOOD:  Type
QQ
(or QUIT). Best to be registered (see HELP
REGISTER
).
BAD:   Close your
client
or browser without QQ or QUIT.
Read
on to see why.
In Achaea, you never need to
rent
or
save
in order to leave the game. Simply
type QQ or QUIT and, after a brief delay, your soul will be kept safe until
your return. You can abort the QQ or QUIT any
time
during the brief delay by
typing STOP. Also if you take any damage during this time you will also stop
your
exit
. You may benefit from
registering
, and we would certainly appreciate
it if you were to register. Please see
HELP REGISTER
and the notes below about
inactivity
.
QQ/
Quitting
the right way
-------------------------
-
Gold
should be in your
pack
or some
other
container
.
-
Plants
/
herbs
should be in the
rift
(HELP RIFT). If they aren't, they will
fall to the ground as you leave the realms.
-
Quest
items, including
corpses
, will fall to the ground no matter what.
The WRONG Way
-------------
If you just close your browser
window
, or
disconnect
from Achaea (or the window
of whatever
mud
client you happen to be using) then your
character
will still
be playing the game (but without benefit of your loving attention). You may
begin to get
hungry
. Someone may
steal
your possessions. You may be killed
without ever
embracing
death
. Risky business! That's why just closing
your client is a bad
idea
. Always do QQ or QUIT and wait for a few
seconds
until Achaea
says
all is well and disconnects you.
About Registering and Inactivity
--------------------------------
Characters may be erased after a
period
of inactivity which varies from a few
days
to a few
months
. The more play time you have, the longer we wait before
counting you as
inactive
and removing your character.
Unregistered characters are erased after only a single
day
of inactivity.
(See also
HELP REGISTER
,
HELP INACTIVITY
, and HELP QUIT)
