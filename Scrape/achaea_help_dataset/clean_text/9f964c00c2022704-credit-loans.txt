19.4.5 Credit Loans
(See also:
HELP
CREDIT
TRANSFER)
The
loans
system
allows for formalised loans between a player and another player, or an organisation.
Organisations cannot currently
loan
to one another -the person being given the loan must be a player. If you owe people a loan, you cannot retire until you repay that loan.
To begin working on a loan agreement:
LOAN
COMPOSE
FOR <person taking out the loan>
Once you have started
composing
an agreement, you must set various attributes of that agreement:
LOAN
PARTY
<loaning party>: this can either be yourself or a
city
or
house
. Only city and house leaders may currently
issue
loans on behalf of their organisation.
LOAN AMOUNT <amount> <currency>: current supported currencies are
credits
. We may expand this to
other
currencies later if people find the system useful.
Once these fields are set, you must:
LOAN OFFER
The loan will then enter a pending state during which
time
the offer can be accepted by the targeted party with LOAN ACCEPT <loan number>.
So, for a full example:
Makarios
wishes
to offer a loan of 500 credits to Ictinus. Makarios would enter:
LOAN COMPOSE FOR ICTINUS
LOAN PARTY MAKARIOS
LOAN AMOUNT 500 CREDITS
LOAN OFFER
Ictinus would then get a
message
about the loan and would do:
LOAN LIST (to find the number of the pending loan, let's
say
it is #1)
LOAN ACCEPT 1
Now hopefully Ictinus will use:
LOAN REPAY <loan number> <number to repay> to repay Makarios loan.
Loan repayments can be in any amount up to the full amount, it does not need to be done all at the same time.
