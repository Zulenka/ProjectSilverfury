19.10.23 Legends Deck

(See Also: LEGENDDECK HELP)

The deck of legends is a set of playing cards boasting an array of weird and
wonderful powers. These are obtained via giftbags and other promotional items
and begin life as a simple decorated sleeve which, when unwrapped, reveals the
image of a famous figure from Achaean history. Each card belongs to a specific
suit, and each suit also has a joker card.

Details on the cards, sleeves, and decks are outlined below:

A deck of mystical cards
   - These decks are awarded the first time you UNWRAP a decorated sleeve (see
     below), and are used to store and activate the mystical cards of Achaean 
     legends!
   - LEGENDDECK SHOW to list your deck's contents.
   - LEGENDDECK DRAW <cardname> [target] to activate a card.
   - LEGENDDECK ADD <card> to add a held card to the deck.
   - LEGENDDECK REMOVE <card> to remove a card from the deck.
                              Only when the card is fully charged.
                              Does not apply to jokers.
   - LEGENDDECK HELP to show the most up-to-date list of Legend Deck cards

A decorated sleeve
   - UNWRAP these sleeves to reveal one of the mystical cards of legends!
   - The sleeves reset until unwrapped and cannot be traded.

A playing card featuring <name>
   - These are the cards obtained from the decorated sleeves.
   - Once unwrapped, cards can be traded in (ARTEFACT <card#> TRADEIN).
   - Alternatively, cards can be traded to players.
   - There are four face cards in each suit, each with a unique effect.
   - Each suit also has a joker card.
   - These Joker cards can be drawn for another card in their suit, and they'll 
     add one charge to it (if not already at max).
