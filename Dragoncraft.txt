Abilities in Dragoncraft
Dragonform	Transform into your draconic form.
Syntax:	DRAGONFORM
LESSERFORM
Works against:	Self
Cooldown:	4.00 seconds of balance
Details:	Through your deeds and the gift of Ashaxei you can now transform into a Greater Dragon. Use LESSERFORM to change back to your weak body.
Heal	Purge yourself of afflictions.
Syntax:	DRAGONHEAL
Required:	1500 mana
Works against:	Self
Cooldown:	2.50 seconds of equilibrium
Details:	Use your superior dragon immune system to throw off multiple afflictions at once. Due to the nature of this ability, you will be unable to muster the concentration to activate it if you are afflicted with both weariness and recklessness simultaneously.

Summon	Summon your breath weapon.
Syntax:	SUMMON <breath weapon type>
Required:	300 mana
Works against:	Self
Details:	Summon your breath weapon. Each type of Dragon has a different breath weapon
Black: Acid - Acid breath. Unblockable damage.
Blue: Ice - Cold damage. Chance of becoming frozen.
Gold: Psi - Psychic damage instead of breath weapon. Chance of eq loss.
Green: Venom - Poison damage. Chance of a random venom affliction.
Red: Dragonfire - Fire damage. Sets the target on fire also.
Silver: Lightning - Electric damage. High chance of epilepsy.

Devour	Destroy them in a single bite.
Syntax:	DEVOUR <target>
Works against:	Adventurers
Cooldown:	2.00 seconds of balance
Details:	Use your massive jaws to rip your opponent's head from their shoulders. The more restoration broken limbs your victim suffers from, the faster you may complete your devouring.
Note that a damaged torso will boost the speed of this ability significantly more than other limbs. If your devour would take less than six seconds to complete in total, it will not be interruptable via several of the standard methods, such as via the paralysis affliction or being knocked to the ground.

Curse	Lay down a terrible curse to cripple your foe.
Syntax:	DRAGONCURSE <target>
DRAGONCURSE <target> <affliction(*)> <delay in seconds(**)>
Required:	500 mana
Works against:	Adventurers
Cooldown:	4.00 seconds of equilibrium
Details:	With no affliction and delay specified, your victim will be cursed with a number of random masked afflictions. With an affliction specified, the curse will strike the target after the specified delay. It should be noted that the curse will not consume equilibrium when employed in this way, but may only be invoked periodically.
(*) Affliction must be one of: paralysis, impatience, sensitivity, asthma, stupidity, weariness or recklessness.
(**) Delay must be between one and ten seconds.

Swipe	Maul your prey with your talons.
Syntax:	SWIPE <target> <limb> <HEAD|TORSO> [venom]
Cooldown:	2.50 seconds of balance
Details:	Swipe at the upper body of your prey with your deadly talons, carving into a limb followed swiftly by a followup strike at either the head or
torso. This does the equivalent damage of rending with your claws, but equally split across both limbs(*).
* Note that both limbs are still vulnerable to being parried.
